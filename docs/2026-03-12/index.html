LLM 安全 / LLM Security 3 篇
代码混用红队测试框架CSRT揭示了大语言模型的多语言安全漏洞，攻击成功率比纯英文攻击高46.7%。
Code-Switching Red-Teaming: LLM Evaluation for Safety and Multilingual Understanding
Haneul Yoo, Yongjin Yang, Hwaran Lee 2024-06-17 ACL
核心贡献
代码混用红队测试框架CSRT揭示了大语言模型的多语言安全漏洞，攻击成功率比纯英文攻击高46.7%。
Code-switching in red-teaming queries exposes a critical multilingual safety vulnerability in LLMs, with the proposed CSRT framework achieving 46.7% more successful attacks than standard English-only attacks.
重要性与意义 / Significance
本文揭示了一个被忽视的攻击面：代码混用可系统性地绕过主要针对单语场景训练的安全机制。覆盖10个模型和10种语言组合的基准测试为安全研究社区提供了可复用的评估工具。
This paper reveals a largely overlooked attack surface: natural code-switching behavior can systematically bypass LLM safety guardrails that are primarily trained and evaluated in monolingual settings. The comprehensive benchmark across 10 LLMs and up to 10 mixed languages provides a reusable evaluation framework for the safety community. Practitioners deploying LLMs in multilingual contexts should treat code-switching robustness as a first-class safety requirement.
科研问题 / Research Question
自然发生的代码混用（在单个话语中混合多种语言）能否被用于绕过主流大语言模型的安全机制？由于安全对齐主要在英文或单语环境下进行，混合语言输入在实际部署中可能是一个重大盲点。
Can naturally occurring code-switching—the practice of mixing multiple languages in a single utterance—be exploited to bypass the safety mechanisms of state-of-the-art LLMs? This matters because safety alignment is predominantly conducted in English or isolated monolingual settings, leaving mixed-language inputs as a potential blind spot in real-world deployments.
先前工作为何不够 / Why Prior Work Falls Short
现有多语言红队测试方法通常将攻击提示直接翻译为单一目标语言，模型已通过多语言安全微调逐步应对此类攻击。这些方法未能模拟真实世界中语言交织混用的场景，低估了多语言部署中的实际攻击面。
Existing multilingual red-teaming approaches typically translate attack prompts directly into a single target language, which LLMs have increasingly learned to handle through multilingual safety tuning. These methods fail to capture the nuanced, interleaved nature of real-world multilingual communication and do not stress-test models against cross-lingual input mixing. As a result, prior work underestimates the actual attack surface in multilingual deployments.
解决方案 / Solution
CSRT框架通过在单条查询中交织多种语言的词汇或短语来合成攻击提示，模拟自然代码混用行为，语言组合数量最多可达10种。该框架覆盖仇恨言论、暴力、违法活动等常见安全域，无需微调模型，可扩展至大规模自动化攻击提示生成。
The authors propose CSRT (Code-Switching Red-Teaming), a framework that synthesizes attack prompts by interleaving tokens or phrases from multiple languages within a single query, mimicking natural code-switching behavior. CSRT generates queries combining up to 10 languages, applying this mixing strategy across conventional safety domains such as hate speech, violence, and illegal activities. The framework is also shown to be extensible, supporting automated generation of code-switching attack prompts at scale. Crucially, it requires no model fine-tuning and can augment existing red-teaming datasets.
主要结论 / Key Results
在十个主流大语言模型上，CSRT的攻击成功率比标准英文红队测试高46.7%，且在所有安全域均优于现有多语言基线。研究同时发现，大多数模型在生成和理解代码混用文本方面存在明显弱点，安全能力与多语言能力的缺口相互关联。
CSRT achieves 46.7% more successful attacks compared to standard English-only red-teaming queries across ten state-of-the-art LLMs. It consistently outperforms existing multilingual red-teaming baselines, demonstrating effectiveness across all tested safety domains. The study also finds that most LLMs exhibit notable weaknesses in both generating and understanding code-switching text, suggesting safety and multilingual capability gaps are interrelated.
值得关注的讨论点 / Discussion Points
  • 代码混用造成安全分类器的理解盲区：当对齐训练以单语为主时，混合语言输入可能导致模型内部表示不一致，使有害意图得以通过，安全评估必须原生支持混合语言输入。
  • 46.7%的提升幅度可能低估了真实风险，因为攻击者可主动选择最优语言组合；未来研究应深入分析哪些语言对或混合比例最能削弱安全机制。
  • 模型在生成和理解代码混用文本上的双重弱点引发深层问题：多语言能力是否应成为多语言地区安全部署的前提条件，开发者如何平衡语言覆盖扩展与随之增大的攻击面？
  • Language mixing creates an interpretability gap: when safety classifiers and alignment tuning are monolingual, code-switched inputs may be parsed inconsistently across the model's internal representations, allowing harmful intent to slip through — this suggests safety evaluation must natively include mixed-language inputs.
  • The 46.7% improvement over English baselines likely underestimates real-world risk, since adversarial users can choose optimal language combinations; future work should study which specific language pairs or mixing ratios maximize attack success and why certain languages weaken guardrails more than others.
  • The finding that LLMs struggle to both generate and understand code-switching text raises a deeper question: should multilingual capability be a prerequisite for safe deployment in multilingual regions, and how should developers balance expanding language coverage with the increased attack surface it introduces?

TRIDENT: Enhancing Large Language Model Safety with Tri-Dimensional Diversified Red-Teaming Data Synthesis
Xiaorui Wu, Xiaofeng Mao, Fei Li et al. 2025-05-30 ACL
核心贡献
TRIDENT提出三维框架（词汇多样性、恶意意图、越狱策略）及自动化流程，合成全面的红队数据集以提升LLM安全对齐效果。
TRIDENT introduces a tri-dimensional framework (Lexical Diversity, Malicious Intent, Jailbreak Tactics) and an automated pipeline to synthesize comprehensive red-teaming datasets for safer LLM alignment.
重要性与意义 / Significance
TRIDENT填补了LLM安全对齐数据集仅关注词汇多样性的关键空白，为研究者和实践者提供了可直接使用的评估框架与数据集。自动化零样本生成流程大幅降低了构建大规模红队数据的成本。
TRIDENT directly addresses a critical gap in LLM safety: existing alignment datasets are evaluated only on surface-level lexical diversity, leaving models exposed to novel jailbreak tactics and under-represented malicious intents. By providing a systematic three-dimensional measurement framework and two ready-to-use datasets, this work gives both researchers and practitioners concrete tools to audit and improve safety coverage. The automated, zero-shot pipeline also lowers the barrier to generating large-scale, diverse red-teaming data without expensive human annotation.
科研问题 / Research Question
如何系统衡量并提升LLM安全对齐数据集的风险覆盖度，而不仅停留于词汇变化？现有数据集在恶意意图类别和越狱策略上覆盖不足，导致微调模型仍易受对抗性提示攻击。
How can we systematically measure and improve the risk coverage of LLM safety alignment datasets beyond simple lexical variation? Current datasets fail to ensure broad coverage of malicious intent categories and jailbreak strategies, leaving fine-tuned models vulnerable to real-world adversarial prompts. A principled framework for diagnosing and filling these coverage gaps is urgently needed.
先前工作为何不够 / Why Prior Work Falls Short
现有安全对齐数据集主要优化词汇多样性，忽视恶意意图分类体系的广度和越狱策略的多样性。这种单一视角导致对齐数据集存在盲点，易被攻击者利用。
Existing safety alignment datasets (e.g., those used in RLHF and SFT pipelines) predominantly optimize for lexical diversity, treating dataset quality as a function of surface-level textual variation. They largely ignore the breadth of malicious intent taxonomies and the variety of jailbreak tactics attackers actually employ. This narrow focus results in alignment datasets with blind spots that adversaries can readily exploit.
解决方案 / Solution
TRIDENT构建三维分析框架，衡量对齐数据集在词汇多样性、恶意意图和越狱策略上的覆盖度。通过基于人格角色的零样本LLM生成，自动合成覆盖三维的有害指令，并配以安全对齐回复，生成TRIDENT-Core（26,311条）等数据集，全程无需人工标注。
TRIDENT proposes a three-dimensional analysis framework to measure alignment dataset coverage across Lexical Diversity, Malicious Intent, and Jailbreak Tactics. It introduces an automated pipeline using persona-based, zero-shot LLM generation to synthesize harmful instructions that span all three dimensions. Each generated harmful instruction is paired with an ethically aligned response, producing two datasets: TRIDENT-Core (26,311 examples) and a supplementary extended set. The pipeline requires no human annotation and is fully automated, enabling scalable and reproducible red-teaming data creation.
主要结论 / Key Results
TRIDENT-Core包含26,311条指令-回复对，覆盖多样风险类别。在TRIDENT数据上微调的模型相较仅优化词汇多样性的基线数据集表现出更强的安全对齐，三维框架亦定量揭示了现有数据集此前未被发现的覆盖盲点。
The resulting TRIDENT-Core dataset comprises 26,311 instruction-response pairs covering diverse risk categories. Models fine-tuned on TRIDENT data demonstrate improved safety alignment compared to baselines trained on datasets optimized for lexical diversity alone. The tri-dimensional framework also reveals quantifiable coverage gaps in prior datasets that were previously undetected by lexical metrics alone.
值得关注的讨论点 / Discussion Points
  • 三维框架未覆盖多模态攻击和多轮越狱对话等新兴威胁向量，框架完整性有待拓展。
  • 零样本生成虽可扩展规模，但可能引入意图标注偏差或不真实的对抗样本，影响安全提升的实际迁移效果。
  • 评估主要聚焦SFT安全对齐，针对已知分类体系的自适应攻击者的鲁棒性尚未充分验证。
  • Dimension completeness: The three chosen dimensions (Lexical, Intent, Tactics) are well-motivated, but the framework does not address multimodal attacks or multi-turn jailbreak conversations, which are increasingly common threat vectors.
  • Data quality vs. scale trade-off: Zero-shot LLM generation enables scale but may introduce subtle inaccuracies in intent labeling or produce adversarial examples that are unrealistic, potentially limiting real-world transferability of safety gains.
  • Evaluation scope: The paper reports improvements in SFT-based safety alignment, but it is unclear how TRIDENT-trained models perform under adaptive adversaries who specifically target the known taxonomy, raising questions about long-term robustness.

MUSE: MCTS-Driven Red Teaming Framework for Enhanced Multi-Turn Dialogue Safety in Large Language Models
Siyu Yan, Longyu Zeng, Xuecheng Wu et al. 2025-09-18 EMNLP
核心贡献
MUSE提出攻防双向框架，利用蒙特卡洛树搜索语义轨迹（MUSE-A）与早期干预微调（MUSE-D），系统性地识别并缓解多轮对话中的越狱攻击。
MUSE introduces a dual attack-defense framework using MCTS-guided semantic tree search (MUSE-A) and early-intervention fine-tuning (MUSE-D) to expose and mitigate multi-turn jailbreaks in LLMs.
重要性与意义 / Significance
多轮越狱攻击在现实部署中更为常见，但研究严重不足。MUSE首次将攻击发现与对齐防御统一在多轮对话框架下，为红队测试和对话AI系统加固提供了可落地的工具。
Multi-turn jailbreaks are significantly underexplored compared to single-turn attacks, yet they reflect how users actually interact with LLMs in deployment. MUSE is one of the first frameworks to jointly address both attack discovery and alignment-based defense in the multi-turn setting, offering practitioners a concrete toolkit for red-teaming and hardening conversational AI systems.
科研问题 / Research Question
如何系统地发现并防御跨多轮对话的越狱攻击？现实部署中LLM几乎都以多轮形式使用，但现有安全研究主要针对单轮提示，存在明显盲区。
How can we systematically discover and defend against jailbreaks that unfold across multiple conversational turns, where attackers exploit accumulated context to gradually bypass LLM safety guardrails? This matters because real-world LLM deployments are almost exclusively multi-turn, yet existing safety benchmarks and defenses overwhelmingly focus on single isolated prompts.
先前工作为何不够 / Why Prior Work Falls Short
GCG、AutoDAN、PAIR等主流红队方法仅针对单轮对抗提示，忽视多轮上下文的累积效应。现有多轮攻击多依赖固定对话模板，语义多样性不足。RLHF、DPO等对齐方法缺乏针对多轮对话的细粒度、逐轮干预机制。
Existing red-teaming methods such as GCG, AutoDAN, and PAIR primarily craft single-turn adversarial prompts, ignoring the cumulative context that multi-turn conversations provide to attackers. Multi-turn attack attempts have been limited in diversity and rely on fixed dialogue templates, failing to explore the broad semantic space of possible conversational trajectories. Defense strategies like RLHF and DPO improve general alignment but lack fine-grained, turn-level intervention mechanisms for multi-turn vulnerabilities.
解决方案 / Solution
MUSE-A将多轮越狱建模为树搜索问题，以帧语义为引导，通过MCTS探索多样化对话轨迹，逐步诱导模型产生有害输出。MUSE-D利用攻击生成的对抗对话数据，对模型进行早期轮次干预的微调，在有害内容被诱出前识别并阻断操控意图，形成"攻击发现→防御强化"的闭环流程。
MUSE-A frames multi-turn jailbreaking as a tree search problem, applying Monte Carlo Tree Search (MCTS) guided by frame semantics to explore diverse conversational trajectories that progressively steer the model toward harmful outputs. Each tree node represents a dialogue state, and heuristic scoring selects the most promising semantic directions for expansion. MUSE-D then uses the attack-generated adversarial dialogues to fine-tune a defense model with early-turn intervention, training the LLM to detect and neutralize manipulation attempts before unsafe content is elicited. Together, the two components form a closed-loop red-team-then-defend pipeline.
主要结论 / Key Results
MUSE-A在多个LLM上的攻击成功率（ASR）显著高于现有多轮攻击基线，揭示了单轮方法无法发现的漏洞。MUSE-D经攻击数据微调后大幅降低ASR，同时保持模型通用能力，优于标准对齐微调基线。实验覆盖多种模型，验证了框架的跨模型泛化性。
MUSE-A demonstrates higher attack success rates (ASR) compared to existing multi-turn attack baselines across multiple LLMs, exposing vulnerabilities that single-turn methods miss entirely. MUSE-D, trained on MUSE-A-generated data, reduces ASR substantially while maintaining general model utility, outperforming standard alignment fine-tuning baselines. Experiments span diverse models, confirming the framework's generalizability across different LLM families.
值得关注的讨论点 / Discussion Points
  • MCTS计算开销：对话状态树搜索代价较高，论文未充分量化MUSE-A相比简单基线所需的查询预算，这对大规模红队测试的实用性至关重要。
  • 防御泛化性：MUSE-D在MUSE-A生成的攻击数据上微调，存在过拟合该特定攻击分布的风险，对树搜索未覆盖的新型多轮攻击策略的鲁棒性有待验证。
  • 评估局限性：以自动分类器衡量ASR可能低估或高估真实有害性，引入人工评估将更有力地支撑攻击有效性与防御鲁棒性的结论。
  • Scalability of MCTS: Tree search over dialogue states is computationally expensive; the paper does not fully quantify the query budget MUSE-A requires compared to simpler baselines, which is critical for practical red-teaming at scale.
  • Defense generalization: MUSE-D is fine-tuned on MUSE-A-generated attacks, raising the question of whether it overfits to this specific attack distribution and remains robust against unseen multi-turn attack strategies not covered by the tree search.
  • Evaluation scope: ASR measured by automated classifiers can misestimate true harmfulness; human evaluation of harmful output quality and diversity would strengthen confidence in both the attack effectiveness and defense robustness claims.

具身智能安全 / Embodied Intelligence Security 1 篇
本文系统评估对三种基于VLM的自动驾驶系统的物理对抗补丁攻击，揭示了严重且各具特点的架构漏洞。
Comparative Analysis of Patch Attack on VLM-Based Autonomous Driving Architectures
David Fernandez, Pedram MohajerAnsari, Amir Salarpour et al. 2026-03-09 the 2025
核心贡献
本文系统评估对三种基于VLM的自动驾驶系统的物理对抗补丁攻击，揭示了严重且各具特点的架构漏洞。
This paper systematically benchmarks physical adversarial patch attacks against three VLM-based autonomous driving systems (Dolphins, OmniDrive, LeapVAD), revealing severe and architecture-specific vulnerabilities using black-box optimization in CARLA simulation.
重要性与意义 / Significance
本文首次对多种VLM自动驾驶架构进行系统性对抗评估，为鲁棒性研究和防御设计提供了基准方法，对安全关键场景的实际部署具有重要指导意义。
As VLMs are increasingly adopted in safety-critical autonomous driving pipelines, understanding their adversarial vulnerabilities is essential before real-world deployment. This paper provides the first systematic, cross-architecture adversarial evaluation framework for VLM-based driving systems, offering a reusable benchmark methodology. The findings directly motivate the design of more robust VLM architectures and adversarial defenses for embodied AI.
科研问题 / Research Question
VLM被引入自动驾驶以增强场景理解，但其面对物理对抗补丁的鲁棒性尚属未知。本文研究不同VLM架构在补丁攻击下的漏洞特征及失效模式差异。
VLMs are increasingly integrated into autonomous driving stacks to handle complex scene understanding and decision-making, but their robustness against physically realizable adversarial attacks is entirely uncharacterized. This matters because a patch placed on a real-world object (e.g., a stop sign) could cause catastrophic failures in perception and planning. The paper asks: how vulnerable are current VLM driving architectures to patch attacks, and do different architectures exhibit distinct failure patterns?
先前工作为何不够 / Why Prior Work Falls Short
现有对抗攻击研究主要针对CNN感知模型，VLM鲁棒性研究集中于数字扰动和提示注入，缺乏针对VLM驾驶系统的物理补丁攻击跨架构比较评估。
Prior adversarial attack research has focused primarily on traditional CNN-based perception models (e.g., object detectors like YOLO), with patch attacks well-studied in classification and detection tasks. Existing VLM robustness studies focus on digital perturbations and prompt injection, not physically realizable attacks in driving contexts. No prior work provides a fair, multi-architecture comparative evaluation of patch attacks specifically targeting VLM-based end-to-end driving systems.
解决方案 / Solution
本文提出基于黑盒优化的对抗补丁生成框架，通过语义同质化统一三种VLM架构的输入，在CARLA仿真中评估物理可实现的补丁攻击对驾驶性能和目标检测的影响。
The authors propose a black-box optimization framework that crafts adversarial patches without access to model internals, making the attack transferable and practically realistic. To ensure fair cross-architecture comparison, they apply semantic homogenization — standardizing input prompts and task definitions across Dolphins, OmniDrive (Omni-L), and LeapVAD. Attacks are evaluated in the CARLA simulator under physically realizable conditions, where patches are rendered on in-scene objects across multiple frames. The framework measures both driving performance degradation (e.g., collision rate, route completion) and object detection failures.
主要结论 / Key Results
三种架构均表现出严重漏洞，目标检测精度显著下降，并出现持续多帧规划失效。各架构呈现不同失效模式，现有VLM设计均不足以应对对抗补丁威胁。
All three VLM architectures exhibit severe vulnerabilities under patch attacks, with significant degradation in object detection accuracy and sustained multi-frame planning failures. Each architecture shows distinct vulnerability patterns: some are more susceptible to perception-level disruption while others suffer greater decision-level collapse. Specific numerical results indicate critical drops in key driving metrics (e.g., route completion and collision avoidance), underscoring that no current VLM design adequately handles adversarial patch threats.
值得关注的讨论点 / Discussion Points
  • 仿真局限性：实验仅在CARLA中进行，真实场景下的光照、天气和相机噪声对攻击效果的影响仍需验证。
  • 攻击范围受限：仅评估黑盒攻击，未探索白盒或自适应攻击，可能低估架构的深层脆弱性。
  • 缺乏防御方案：论文诊断了漏洞但未提出对抗训练或补丁检测等缓解策略，对实际系统加固的指导有限。
  • Simulation-to-reality gap: All experiments are conducted in CARLA, leaving open whether the identified vulnerabilities and attack transferability hold under real-world lighting, weather, and camera noise conditions — a critical validation step before drawing deployment conclusions.
  • Black-box assumption scope: While black-box evaluation is realistic for external attackers, the paper does not explore white-box or adaptive attacks, which could reveal even deeper architectural weaknesses and better inform defense design.
  • Defense directions absent: The paper diagnoses vulnerabilities but does not propose or evaluate any mitigation strategies (e.g., adversarial training, patch detection modules), limiting its immediate actionability for practitioners building safer VLM driving systems.

DEBATE, TRAIN, EVOLVE: Self Evolution of Language Model Reasoning
Gaurav Srivastava, Zhenyu Bi, Meng Lu et al. 2025-05-21 EMNLP
核心贡献
DTE利用多智能体辩论轨迹在无需标注标签的情况下微调单个LLM，在GSM-PLUS上平均准确率提升8.92%，跨领域泛化提升5.8%。
DTE uses multi-agent debate traces to fine-tune a single LLM without ground-truth labels, achieving 8.92% average accuracy gain on GSM-PLUS and 5.8% cross-domain generalization across six reasoning benchmarks.
重要性与意义 / Significance
DTE通过内部生成的辩论轨迹实现模型自我进化，为突破数据瓶颈提供了实用路径。跨领域泛化结果表明，该框架能够培养可迁移的推理能力，而非过拟合于单一任务。
DTE directly tackles the data-scarcity bottleneck for LLM reasoning improvement by enabling models to self-evolve using internally generated debate traces. The ground-truth-free training paradigm opens a practical path toward continual self-improvement in production settings where labeled data is costly or unavailable. The cross-domain generalization results suggest the framework instills transferable reasoning skills rather than overfitting to a single task distribution.
科研问题 / Research Question
当LLM逼近可用高质量训练数据的上限时，如何在无需外部标注或人工监督的情况下自主提升推理能力？大规模构建新标注推理数据集成本高昂，这一问题愈发迫切。
As LLMs approach the limits of available high-quality training data, how can a model autonomously improve its reasoning without relying on external labels or human supervision? This is critical because curating new labeled reasoning datasets is expensive and increasingly impractical at scale.
先前工作为何不够 / Why Prior Work Falls Short
现有方法如思维链、RLHF及自博弈通常需要标注标签或人工反馈。多智能体辩论已被用于推理时提升，但尚未作为训练信号用于自我进化。STaR等自我提升方法依赖正确性过滤，仍需真实答案。
Existing approaches to improving LLM reasoning—such as chain-of-thought prompting, RLHF, and self-play—typically require either ground-truth labels, human feedback, or large curated datasets. Multi-agent debate frameworks (e.g., Du et al., 2023) have shown inference-time benefits but have not been used to generate training signal for self-improvement.   • Overhead and latency: While described as lightweight, the paper does not provide detailed wall-clock latency or memory overhead measurements, which are critical for evaluating real-world deployment feasibility of the prober and Refusal Head at scale.
