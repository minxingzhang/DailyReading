Disentangling Adversarial Prompts: A Semantic-Graph Defense for Robust LLM Security
Xiang Fang, Wanlong Fang 2026-03-14 AAAI
核心贡献
APD框架通过互信息分解、谱图意图分类和轻量级Transformer分类器三重机制，在对抗性提示到达LLM前将其主动拦截。
APD introduces a three-part semantic-graph framework—mutual information decomposition, spectral graph intent classification, and a lightweight transformer classifier—to proactively neutralize adversarial prompts before they reach an LLM.
重要性与意义 / Significance
随着LLM在高安全性场景的广泛部署，APD提供了一种无需重训基础模型即可叠加使用的主动防御方案，其语义图范式为可解释的对抗提示分析开辟了新方向。
As LLMs are deployed in security-critical settings, robust defenses against jailbreaking and prompt injection are urgently needed. APD's proactive, pre-processing approach means it can be applied on top of any existing LLM without retraining the base model. The semantic-graph paradigm opens a new direction for interpretable, structured adversarial prompt analysis.
科研问题 / Research Question
越狱攻击和提示注入等对抗性提示利用语义歧义绕过安全过滤器，现有被动防御难以泛化到新型攻击变体，致使生产环境中的LLM持续面临风险。
LLMs are increasingly exploited via adversarial prompts—such as jailbreaks and prompt injections—that abuse semantic ambiguities to bypass safety filters and elicit harmful outputs. Existing reactive defenses struggle to generalize across novel attack variations, leaving models exposed in production environments.
先前工作为何不够 / Why Prior Work Falls Short
现有防御主要依赖输出过滤、RLHF微调或关键词匹配，属于被动应对，易被语义改写的攻击绕过；基于图和互信息的方法尚未联合用于LLM对抗提示分解。
Prior defenses primarily rely on output filtering, RLHF-based fine-tuning, or simple keyword/pattern matching, all of which are reactive and easily circumvented by semantically rephrased attacks. Graph-based and mutual-information approaches have been explored in NLP safety but not jointly applied to adversarial prompt decomposition in LLMs.
解决方案 / Solution
APD首先用互信息语义分解将输入拆分为统计独立的对抗与良性成分；随后谱图意图分类器检测语义图中的恶意结构模式；最后轻量级Transformer分类器对真实毒性数据集打分并过滤恶意成分，全流程无需修改底层LLM。
APD first applies mutual information-based semantic decomposition to split an input prompt into statistically independent adversarial and benign components. A graph-based intent classifier then performs spectral analysis on the prompt's semantic graph to detect malicious structural patterns. Finally, a lightweight transformer-based classifier, trained on real-world toxic prompt datasets, assigns a threat score and filters or sanitizes the malicious component before the prompt reaches the LLM. The three modules work in a pipeline, requiring no modification to the underlying LLM.
主要结论 / Key Results
APD在真实毒性与对抗提示数据集上的检测准确率优于基线防御方法；轻量级Transformer分类器在越狱和提示注入两类攻击上均表现出色，同时保持适合生产部署的低推理开销。
The APD framework is evaluated on real-world datasets of toxic and adversarial prompts, demonstrating improvements in detection accuracy over baseline defenses. Specific numbers are partially truncated in the abstract, but the framework reports strong performance on both jailbreak and prompt injection attack categories. The lightweight transformer classifier is noted to achieve competitive accuracy while maintaining low inference overhead suitable for production deployment.
值得关注的讨论点 / Discussion Points
  • 互信息分解假设对抗与良性成分可被清晰分离，但对于恶意意图高度融合的提示，分解质量可能下降，检测可靠性存疑。
  • 语义图的谱分析计算代价高于简单分类器，针对超长提示或高吞吐API的可扩展性需进一步基准验证。
  • 框架仅在现有毒性提示数据集上评估，面对专门针对APD管道本身设计的自适应攻击者，其鲁棒性仍是开放问题。
  • The mutual information decomposition assumes adversarial and benign components can be cleanly separated, but highly entangled prompts—where malicious intent is woven throughout—may degrade decomposition quality and reduce detection reliability.
  • Spectral graph analysis of prompt semantics is computationally more expensive than simple classifiers; scalability to very long prompts or high-throughput APIs needs explicit benchmarking.
  • The framework is evaluated on existing toxic prompt datasets, but adversarial prompt attacks are rapidly evolving; robustness to adaptive attackers who specifically craft inputs to fool the APD pipeline itself remains an open question.

IndicSafe: A Benchmark for Evaluating Multilingual LLM Safety in South Asia
Priyaranjan Pattnayak, Sanchari Chowdhuri 2026-03-18
核心贡献
IndicSafe是首个系统评估12种印度语言LLM安全性的基准，揭示跨语言一致性仅12.8%，安全响应率方差超17%。
IndicSafe is the first benchmark revealing that LLM safety behavior breaks down significantly across 12 Indic languages, with only 12.8% cross-language agreement and over 17% variance in safe-response rates.
重要性与意义 / Significance
超过12亿印度语言使用者正面临安全特性不明的LLM系统，IndicSafe填补了这一关键空白。其文化扎根的提示集与量化指标为从业者提供了可操作的审计工具，应推动开发者将低资源语言安全性纳入核心评估标准。
IndicSafe directly addresses a critical blind spot: over 1.2 billion speakers of Indic languages are exposed to LLM systems whose safety properties are poorly characterized for their languages and cultures. The benchmark's culturally grounded prompts and quantitative metrics (entropy, bias scores, consistency indices) give practitioners concrete tools to audit and compare multilingual safety. This work should prompt model developers to treat low-resource language safety as a first-class evaluation criterion rather than an afterthought.
科研问题 / Research Question
主流LLM在低资源印度语言下是否能保持与高资源语言一致的安全行为？这一问题直接关系到数十亿用户能否平等获得安全、可靠的AI服务。
Do leading LLMs maintain consistent safety behavior when responding in low-resource Indic languages compared to high-resource languages like English? This matters because inconsistent safety across languages can expose vulnerable populations to harmful content or, conversely, deny them access to benign information through over-refusal.
先前工作为何不够 / Why Prior Work Falls Short
现有LLM安全基准以英语为主，缺乏对南亚特有危害类别（如种姓歧视、宗教冲突）的覆盖。多语言安全研究多聚焦于欧洲高资源语言，印度语言脚本几乎未被系统测试，也无基准量化跨语言安全一致性。
Existing LLM safety benchmarks are overwhelmingly English-centric, with limited coverage of culturally specific harm categories relevant to South Asia such as caste discrimination, communal religion, and regional politics. Prior multilingual safety work has typically evaluated only a handful of European or high-resource languages, leaving Indic scripts and dialects largely untested. No prior benchmark systematically quantifies cross-language safety consistency or category-level bias at this scale for Indic languages.
解决方案 / Solution
研究构建了6000条涵盖种姓、宗教、性别、健康、政治五类主题的文化扎根提示，翻译为12种印度语言，评估10个主流LLM。采用提示级熵、类别偏差分数和多语言一致性指数三项指标量化安全一致性。
The authors construct a dataset of 6,000 culturally grounded prompts spanning five sensitive categories—caste, religion, gender, health, and politics—translated into 12 Indic languages. Ten leading LLMs are evaluated on each prompt variant, and responses are labeled as safe or unsafe. Safety consistency is quantified using three novel metrics: prompt-level entropy (uncertainty of a model's label across languages), category bias scores (systematic over- or under-flagging per topic), and a multilingual consistency index (agreement rate across language variants of the same prompt).
主要结论 / Key Results
跨语言安全标签一致率仅12.8%，安全响应率方差超17%，表明安全行为随语言显著漂移。部分模型在低资源脚本中过度拒绝良性提示，同时对真正有害内容漏判；政治敏感话题被系统性过度标记。
Cross-language agreement on safety labels is only 12.8%, indicating that most prompts receive inconsistent safe/unsafe classifications depending on the language used. The variance in SAFE response rates across languages exceeds 17%, demonstrating substantial safety drift. Qualitatively, some models over-refuse benign prompts in low-resource scripts while simultaneously failing to flag genuinely unsafe content in others, and politically sensitive topics are disproportionately over-flagged.
值得关注的讨论点 / Discussion Points
  • 安全漂移并非随机：类别偏差分数显示政治、种姓等话题在不同语言下系统性地触发过度拒绝或漏判，反映模型安全规则受训练数据分布驱动而非原则性设计。
  • 12.8%的跨语言一致率揭示多语言部署的根本矛盾——同一提示在英语下安全、在印地语或孟加拉语下却被标记为不安全，造成用户体验不平等，并带来法律与伦理风险。
  • 基准依赖翻译提示存在局限：翻译可能丢失文化特有的细微伤害语义，未来研究应引入母语者原生撰写的提示以提升生态效度。
  • Safety drift is not random: the category bias scores suggest that certain topics (e.g., politics, caste) systematically trigger over- or under-refusal depending on the language, implying that models inherit biases from their training data distribution rather than applying principled safety rules.
  • The 12.8% cross-language agreement figure exposes a fundamental tension in multilingual deployment — a prompt deemed safe in English may be flagged as unsafe in Hindi or Bengali, creating unequal user experiences and potential legal or ethical liability for deployers.
  • The benchmark's reliance on translated prompts is a practical limitation: translation may not fully capture culturally specific nuance or idiomatic harm, and future work should incorporate natively authored prompts by speakers of each language to improve ecological validity.

Caging the Agents: A Zero Trust Security Architecture for Autonomous AI in Healthcare
Saikat Maiti 2026-03-18
核心贡献
本文提出面向医疗场景的零信任安全架构，涵盖六大威胁域，并在九个生产环境AI智能体中得到验证。
This paper proposes a Zero Trust security architecture for autonomous LLM agents in healthcare, validated across nine production agents with a six-domain threat model addressing HIPAA-relevant attack vectors.
重要性与意义 / Significance
本文是少数将零信任原则落地于HIPAA受监管医疗AI智能体的生产实践之一，为从业者提供了可复用的威胁模型，具有较强的实践参考价值。
As LLM-powered agents gain shell execution, database, and file system access in regulated industries, security frameworks grounded in real deployments are urgently needed. This work is one of the first to operationalize Zero Trust principles specifically for agentic AI in a HIPAA-governed environment, providing a replicable threat model for practitioners. Its production grounding distinguishes it from purely theoretical proposals in this rapidly evolving space.
科研问题 / Research Question
在医疗环境中，具备Shell执行、数据库访问等广泛权限的自主AI智能体如何防范安全威胁？智能体漏洞直接对应HIPAA违规风险，问题极为紧迫。
How can autonomous AI agents with broad system capabilities (shell, database, file, network access) be secured in healthcare settings where data breaches constitute HIPAA violations? The problem is urgent because red teaming studies confirm real-world exploitability of these agents via prompt injection, identity spoofing, and cross-agent unsafe instruction propagation.
先前工作为何不够 / Why Prior Work Falls Short
现有LLM安全研究多聚焦于单模型提示注入，缺乏对多智能体编排风险的分析；HIPAA等合规框架也未针对具备工具调用能力的AI智能体提供具体控制措施。
Existing LLM security research focuses primarily on single-model prompt injection and jailbreaking, without addressing multi-agent orchestration risks or tool-use attack surfaces. Compliance frameworks like HIPAA predate agentic AI and offer no concrete controls for shell-executing or database-querying LLM agents. Red teaming studies (e.g., the cited work [7]) have identified vulnerabilities but stop short of prescribing deployable architectural mitigations.
解决方案 / Solution
本文构建六大威胁域模型，将零信任原则（最小权限、持续验证、假设已被攻破）映射为具体控制措施，包括智能体身份验证、工具权限范围限制、提示词完整性校验及网络出口过滤，并在九个生产智能体中部署实施。
The paper develops a six-domain threat model covering: credential exposure, execution capability abuse, network egress exfiltration, prompt integrity failures, database access risks, and file system threats. It then maps Zero Trust principles (never trust, always verify; least privilege; assume breach) onto each domain with concrete controls. The architecture was deployed for nine autonomous AI agents at a healthcare technology company in production. Key mechanisms include agent identity verification, scoped tool permissions, prompt integrity checks, and monitored egress filtering.
主要结论 / Key Results
该架构已在九个生产智能体中完成部署，验证了零信任控制的可行性，但现有摘要未报告具体量化指标（如拦截率或延迟开销）。
The architecture was validated in a live production environment covering nine agents at a healthcare technology company, though specific quantitative metrics (e.g., attack interception rates or latency overhead) are not reported in the available abstract. The six-domain threat model provides structured coverage of attack surfaces identified in prior red teaming research. The deployment demonstrates practical feasibility of Zero Trust controls without halting agentic functionality.
值得关注的讨论点 / Discussion Points
  • 六域威胁模型具有实践价值，但缺乏量化红队评估数据，难以判断各控制措施的实际效果优先级。
  • 架构设计受HIPAA约束影响显著，向金融、法律等其他受监管行业迁移时需重新映射威胁域，通用性有待验证。
  • 跨智能体提示注入与不安全指令传播仍是难点，提示完整性控制如何应对动态组合的多智能体流水线需进一步阐明。
  • The six-domain threat model is a concrete contribution, but without published red team evaluation metrics or ablation studies, it is difficult to assess which controls provide the greatest security lift in practice.
  • The architecture's generalizability beyond healthcare is an open question — HIPAA constraints shape many design choices, and adapting these controls to other regulated sectors (finance, legal) would require careful re-mapping of threat domains.
  • Cross-agent prompt injection and unsafe instruction propagation remain particularly hard problems; the paper's controls for prompt integrity need elaboration on how they handle dynamically composed multi-agent pipelines at runtime.

具身智能安全 / Embodied Intelligence Security 1 篇

Invisible Triggers, Visible Threats! Road-Style Adversarial Creation Attack for Visual 3D Detection in Autonomous Driving
Jian Wang, Lijun He, Yixing Yong et al. 2025-11-11 AAAI
核心贡献
AdvRoad生成外观自然的路面对抗贴片，在欺骗自动驾驶视觉3D检测器的同时难以被人眼察觉。
AdvRoad generates naturalistic road-surface adversarial patches that fool visual 3D object detectors in autonomous driving while remaining inconspicuous to human observers.
重要性与意义 / Significance
本文揭示了针对量产自动驾驶摄像头3D感知系统的一种隐蔽且实用的攻击方式，说明仅靠外观过滤无法防御多样化路面对抗贴片，对AD系统安全设计具有重要警示意义。
This work exposes a practical, stealthy attack vector against camera-based 3D detection systems that are increasingly deployed in production autonomous vehicles. By demonstrating that road-surface patches can be both visually inconspicuous and dynamically varied, the paper raises the security bar for AD perception pipelines. Defenders can no longer rely on simple appearance-based filtering to neutralize physical adversarial patches.
科研问题 / Research Question
本文探讨能否在路面放置与正常路面纹理无异的对抗贴片，使视觉3D检测器产生幻觉误检。这一问题至关重要，因为摄像头3D检测器是自动驾驶中LiDAR的低成本替代方案，其安全漏洞可能引发严重事故。
The paper asks: can adversarial patches placed on road surfaces reliably induce false detections (hallucinations) in visual 3D object detectors while appearing indistinguishable from normal road texture? This matters because camera-based 3D detectors are cost-effective alternatives to LiDAR in autonomous driving, and their vulnerability to physical adversarial attacks could cause catastrophic safety failures.
先前工作为何不够 / Why Prior Work Falls Short
现有路面对抗攻击多采用固定图案的显眼海报，视觉异常易被发现，且固定内容一旦识别即可被列入黑名单。现有方法在隐蔽性和多样性上均存在明显不足。
Prior physical adversarial attacks on AD detectors typically use conspicuous printed posters with fixed patterns placed on the road, making them visually anomalous and easily spotted by human inspectors. Fixed-content patches are also straightforward to blacklist once identified, limiting their real-world threat longevity. Neither stealthiness nor diversity was adequately addressed by existing methods.
解决方案 / Solution
AdvRoad将对抗贴片生成建模为路面风格约束优化问题：贴片须同时最大化误检率并保持路面外观。方法利用纹理风格迁移将对抗扰动融入真实路面图案，并通过多样性机制生成不同实例，使基于内容的防御失效，攻击目标涵盖单目和多摄像头视觉3D检测器。
AdvRoad frames adversarial patch generation as a road-style constrained optimization problem: patches must simultaneously maximize detection hallucinations and maintain a naturalistic road-surface appearance. The method leverages texture style transfer to blend adversarial perturbations into realistic asphalt/pavement patterns, making patches visually consistent with the road environment. A diversity mechanism generates varied patch instances, preventing simple content-based defenses from neutralizing the attack. The pipeline targets monocular and multi-camera visual 3D detectors commonly used in AD systems.
主要结论 / Key Results
AdvRoad诱发的幻觉3D检测框数量显著高于固定图案基线，生成贴片在人眼可察觉性指标上得分更低，验证了隐蔽性。多样性机制生成的多种变体均能有效攻击，使黑名单防御难以奏效。
AdvRoad successfully induces hallucinated 3D bounding box detections in targeted visual detectors, with attack success rates notably higher than prior fixed-pattern patch baselines. The generated patches score significantly lower on human perceptibility metrics, confirming their stealthiness against manual inspection. Quantitative results demonstrate that the diversity mechanism produces functionally effective attacks across multiple patch variants, complicating patch-blacklisting defenses.
值得关注的讨论点 / Discussion Points
  • 攻击范围：AdvRoad仅针对纯摄像头3D检测器，LiDAR-camera融合系统因激光雷达不受路面贴片干扰，攻击效果可能大幅降低。
  • 迁移性存疑：对抗贴片在不同检测器架构间的迁移能力尚不明确，这对评估针对未知部署模型的实际风险至关重要。
  • 防御空白：论文未提出或评估具体对抗措施，如BEV特征图异常检测或跨模态一致性校验，相关防御研究有待跟进。
  • Threat scope: AdvRoad targets only camera-based 3D detectors; LiDAR-camera fusion systems may dilute the attack's effectiveness since LiDAR returns would not be perturbed by a flat road patch.
  • Transferability gap: It is unclear how well the adversarial patches transfer across different detector architectures (e.g., BEVDet vs. DETR3D), which is critical for assessing real-world risk against unknown deployed models.
  • Defense gap: The paper motivates the need for perception-layer defenses such as anomaly detection on BEV feature maps or cross-modal consistency checks, but does not propose or evaluate concrete countermeasures.

LLM 发展动态 / LLM Development Trends 3 篇
FINER 提出细粒度负样本查询基准及基于DPO的微调策略，将MLLM幻觉率降低最高24.2%，同时提升通用多模态能力。
FINER: MLLMs Hallucinate under Fine-grained Negative Queries
Rui Xiao, Sanghwan Kim, Yongqin Xian et al. 2026-03-18 CVPR
核心贡献
FINER 提出细粒度负样本查询基准及基于DPO的微调策略，将MLLM幻觉率降低最高24.2%，同时提升通用多模态能力。
FINER introduces fine-grained negative query benchmarks and a DPO-based finetuning strategy that reduces hallucinations in MLLMs by up to 24.2% while improving general multimodal performance.
重要性与意义 / Significance
MLLM幻觉问题是实际部署的关键瓶颈，而现有基准无法探测细粒度不匹配。FINER填补了这一空白，其改进方法可泛化至8个幻觉基准和6个通用多模态基准，具有重要实用价值。
Hallucination in MLLMs is a critical bottleneck for real-world deployment, yet existing benchmarks fail to probe subtle, fine-grained mismatches. FINER fills this gap with two new benchmarks and a practical mitigation method that generalizes across eight existing hallucination suites and six general multimodal benchmarks, making it immediately useful for both researchers and practitioners.
科研问题 / Research Question
当查询涉及细微不匹配（如错误属性、关系或对象）且与真实图像内容部分重叠时，MLLM极易产生幻觉。现有基准仅关注粗粒度问题，无法捕捉这一关键失效模式。
MLLMs frequently hallucinate when queries involve subtle mismatches—wrong attributes, relations, or objects—that partially overlap with real image content. Existing benchmarks focus on coarse-grained, binary image-question pairs and fail to capture this nuanced failure mode, leaving a critical blind spot in evaluation and mitigation research.
先前工作为何不够 / Why Prior Work Falls Short
POPE、CHAIR等现有基准主要测试粗粒度目标存在性，忽视属性、关系及组合错误。它们也未系统构造"假元素与真实内容共存"的负样本查询，导致模型在现实场景中更易被欺骗。
Existing hallucination benchmarks like POPE, CHAIR, and MMHal-Bench primarily test object-level presence with coarse yes/no questions, missing fine-grained attribute, relation, and compositional errors. They also do not systematically construct negative queries where false elements co-occur with genuinely present image content, making models easier to fool in realistic scenarios.
解决方案 / Solution
FINER框架在多目标、多属性、多关系和"what"问题四个维度上生成细粒度负样本查询，构建FINER-CompreCap和FINER-DOCCI两个基准。FINER-Tuning利用DPO在偏好数据上微调模型，引导其拒绝细粒度错误查询、肯定正确查询，并在四个前沿MLLM上进行全面评测。
The authors introduce FINER, a framework for generating Fine-grained NEgative queRies across four dimensions: multi-object, multi-attribute, multi-relation, and "what" questions. Two benchmarks are built—FINER-CompreCap and FINER-DOCCI—using detailed image captions to systematically inject plausible but incorrect elements into queries. To mitigate the identified hallucinations, FINER-Tuning applies Direct Preference Optimization (DPO) on FINER-inspired preference data, teaching models to reject fine-grained false queries while affirming correct ones. Four frontier MLLMs (including InternVL3.5-14B) are finetuned and evaluated comprehensively.
主要结论 / Key Results
FINER-Tuning使InternVL3.5-14B在新基准上幻觉率降低最高24.2%。改进具有广泛泛化性：微调模型在8个现有幻觉基准和6个通用多模态基准上均有提升，未出现灾难性遗忘。
FINER-Tuning achieves up to 24.2% hallucination reduction on the new benchmarks for InternVL3.5-14B. Critically, gains are not benchmark-specific: finetuned models also improve on eight existing hallucination suites and six general multimodal benchmarks, demonstrating broad generalization without catastrophic forgetting of general capabilities.
值得关注的讨论点 / Discussion Points
