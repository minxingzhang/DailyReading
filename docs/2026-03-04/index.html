CyberLLMInstruct: A Pseudo-Malicious Dataset Revealing Safety-Performance Trade-offs in Cyber Security LLM Fine-tuning
Adel Elzemity, Budi Arief, Shujun Li 2025-03-12 CCS 评分 9.4
核心贡献
CyberLLMInstruct构建了含54,928条伪恶意指令对的数据集，揭示了网络安全领域LLM微调中安全性与性能的严峻权衡。
CyberLLMInstruct introduces a 54,928-pair pseudo-malicious dataset that exposes a severe safety-performance trade-off when fine-tuning LLMs on cybersecurity tasks.
优缺点分析 / Pros & Cons
✓ 优势
  • Identifies and rigorously documents a fundamental trade-off between cyber security task performance and safety resilience in fine-tuned LLMs—highly relevant to current LLM deployment challenges
  • Introduces CyberLLMInstruct, a large-scale dataset of 54,928 instruction pairs with diverse sourcing (CTF, academic, industrial) enabling reproducible research and future work
  • Comprehensive empirical evaluation across seven open-source models with multiple attack vectors provides strong evidence and practical insights for the community
✗ 不足
  • Zero community engagement on HuggingFace (0 upvotes) suggests limited visibility or adoption despite venue quality (CCS is top-tier)
  • Author affiliations are not clearly top-tier institutions (appears to be University of Kent and others not in elite ML research cluster), potentially limiting initial visibility
科研问题 / Research Question
随着LLM在网络安全领域的广泛应用，针对特定领域数据的微调带来了严重的双重用途风险。核心问题是：提升模型网络安全任务性能的同时，是否会削弱其对抗恶意利用的安全防护？
As LLMs are increasingly deployed in cybersecurity applications, fine-tuning them on domain-specific data introduces critical dual-use risks. The core question is: does improving a model's cybersecurity task performance simultaneously erode its safety guardrails against adversarial misuse?
先前工作为何不够 / Why Prior Work Falls Short
现有网络安全LLM基准（如CyberMetric）侧重任务性能，未系统评估微调带来的安全退化。通用安全对齐研究未能覆盖恶意网络安全场景，如针对恶意软件的提示注入或零日漏洞诱导攻击。
Existing cybersecurity LLM benchmarks (e.g., CyberMetric) focus on task performance but do not systematically measure safety degradation from fine-tuning. General-purpose safety alignment research does not address adversarial cybersecurity contexts such as prompt injection, jailbreaking with malware-specific payloads, or zero-day vulnerability elicitation.
解决方案 / Solution
作者构建CyberLLMInstruct数据集，含54,928条伪恶意指令对，涵盖恶意软件分析、网络钓鱼模拟和零日漏洞，来源包括CTF挑战、学术论文、行业报告和CVE数据库。对7个开源LLM进行微调，并在CyberMetric任务准确率及提示注入、越狱等多种攻击向量的安全弹性上进行系统评估，通过微调前后对比确认因果关系。
The authors construct CyberLLMInstruct, a dataset of 54,928 pseudo-malicious instruction-response pairs covering malware analysis, phishing simulations, and zero-day vulnerabilities, sourced from CTF challenges, academic papers, industry reports, and CVE databases. Seven open-source LLMs (including Llama 3.1 8B, Mistral, and Phi variants) are fine-tuned on this dataset and evaluated on both cybersecurity task accuracy (CyberMetric) and safety resilience across multiple attack vectors including prompt injection and jailbreaking. The study isolates the fine-tuning process as the causal factor in safety degradation by comparing pre- and post-fine-tuning safety scores systematically.
主要结论 / Key Results
在CyberLLMInstruct上微调后，CyberMetric任务准确率最高达92.50%，域专化效果显著。然而安全性急剧下降：Llama 3.1 8B对提示注入的抵抗得分从0.95骤降至0.15。这一权衡在全部7个模型及所有攻击向量上均一致出现，表明这是系统性而非个例漏洞。
Fine-tuning on CyberLLMInstruct achieves up to 92.50% accuracy on CyberMetric, demonstrating strong domain specialization. However, safety scores collapse dramatically: Llama 3.1 8B's resistance to prompt injection drops from 0.95 to 0.15 post-fine-tuning. This trade-off is consistent across all seven tested models and all evaluated attack vectors, indicating a systemic rather than model-specific vulnerability.
值得关注的讨论点 / Discussion Points
  • 安全对齐在领域微调下极为脆弱：即使少量伪恶意数据也足以瓦解RLHF建立的安全护栏，提示需在微调后重新强化对齐机制。
  • 伪恶意数据设计是双刃剑：虽避免使用真实攻击数据，但数据集本身存在被滥用以故意削除模型安全性的风险，需审慎考量发布策略。
  • 研究结论对将专业化网络安全LLM直接部署于生产环境提出质疑，需辅以输出过滤、红队测试或冻结安全层的LoRA等安全保留微调方法。
  • Safety alignment is brittle under domain fine-tuning: even modest exposure to pseudo-malicious cybersecurity data is sufficient to undo safety guardrails instilled during RLHF, suggesting alignment techniques need to be re-applied or reinforced post fine-tuning.
  • The pseudo-malicious data design is a double-edged sword: while it enables rigorous safety evaluation without using real attack data, it raises questions about responsible release and whether the dataset itself could be misused to deliberately strip safety from models.
  • The findings challenge the viability of deploying specialized cybersecurity LLMs in production without additional safeguards such as output filtering, red-teaming pipelines, or safety-preserving fine-tuning methods like LoRA with frozen safety layers.

Fun-tuning: Characterizing the Vulnerability of Proprietary LLMs to Optimization-Based Prompt Injection Attacks via the Fine-Tuning Interface
Andrey Labunets, Nishit V. Pandya, Ashish Hooda et al. 2025-01-16 IEEE Symposium on Security and Privacy 评分 9.3
核心贡献
Fun-tuning利用专有LLM微调API返回的类损失信号，通过贪心搜索生成对抗性提示注入，在无白盒访问条件下对Gemini系列模型实现了65–82%的攻击成功率。
Fun-tuning exploits the loss-like signals returned by proprietary LLM fine-tuning APIs (e.g., Gemini) to guide greedy search for adversarial prompt injections, achieving 65–82% attack success rates without white-box model access.
优缺点分析 / Pros & Cons
✓ 优势
  • Identifies a novel and previously unexplored attack vector leveraging fine-tuning API loss information to compute optimization-based prompt injections against proprietary LLMs
  • Demonstrates practical attacks against real systems (Gemini API), making findings immediately relevant to industry and practitioners
  • Well-aligned with current LLM security research trends focused on prompt injection, adversarial examples, and API abuse
  • Published at top-tier security venue (IEEE S&P), indicating peer-review validation
  • Characterizes real proprietary system behavior (Gemini fine-tuning interface) rather than purely theoretical analysis
✗ 不足
  • Limited community engagement signal (0 HuggingFace upvotes suggests narrow prior visibility or recent publication)
  • Author affiliations not immediately recognizable as top institutions (Earlence Fernandes from UWisc is well-known but others less prominent), reducing prestige factor
  • Abstract truncated at 'PurpleLla', making full assessment of experimental scope and baselines incomplete
科研问题 / Research Question
传统对抗性提示攻击需要白盒访问模型权重，无法直接应用于闭源专有LLM。本文研究攻击者是否能利用商业微调接口近似梯度优化，对黑盒模型生成有效的提示注入攻击。
Optimization-based adversarial prompt attacks traditionally require white-box access to model weights, making them inapplicable to closed-weight proprietary LLMs. This paper asks: can an attacker exploit the fine-tuning interface—a standard commercial feature—to approximate gradient-based optimization and craft effective prompt injections against black-box models?
先前工作为何不够 / Why Prior Work Falls Short
GCG等现有优化攻击需完整白盒访问，无法用于Gemini等专有模型。基于迁移的黑盒攻击迁移性差，基于查询的方法成本过高，实际可行性有限。
Existing optimization-based attacks like GCG require full white-box access to model weights and gradients, which is unavailable for proprietary LLMs such as Gemini or GPT-4. Black-box attack alternatives typically rely on transfer attacks from open-source surrogates or costly query-based methods, both of which suffer from low transferability or prohibitive API query costs.
解决方案 / Solution
作者发现Gemini微调API会返回每个样本的类损失标量值，可作为有效优化信号。Fun-tuning将对抗性提示注入建模为离散优化问题，通过贪心token替换搜索并利用该信号迭代优化，全程无需访问模型权重。实验在PurpleLlama基准上针对Gemini 1.0和1.5系列模型展开评估。
The authors discover that the Gemini fine-tuning API returns per-example loss-like scalar values (training metrics) that, while not exact gradients, provide a meaningful optimization signal. They design "Fun-tuning," which formulates adversarial prompt injection as a discrete optimization problem and uses a greedy token-substitution search guided by these loss-like values. By submitting candidate adversarial prompts as fine-tuning data and reading back the returned loss signal, the attacker iteratively refines the injection without ever accessing model weights. This approach is evaluated on the PurpleLlama prompt injection benchmark against the Gemini 1.0 and 1.5 model families.
主要结论 / Key Results
Fun-tuning在PurpleLlama基准上对Gemini系列模型的攻击成功率达65–82%，显著优于朴素黑盒基线。实验验证了微调API返回的类损失值与模型行为具有足够相关性，可驱动有效的离散优化，证明微调功能直接引入了可量化的安全漏洞。
Fun-tuning achieves attack success rates of 65–82% on Google's Gemini family of LLMs using the PurpleLlama prompt injection benchmark. The loss-like signals returned by the Gemini fine-tuning API are shown to correlate sufficiently with model behavior to drive effective discrete optimization. These results significantly outperform naive black-box baselines and demonstrate that the fine-tuning utility feature directly introduces a measurable security vulnerability.
值得关注的讨论点 / Discussion Points
  • 效用与安全的根本矛盾：微调接口是核心商业功能，厂商难以移除，该攻击面具有结构性，无法通过简单补丁修复。
  • 泛化风险：任何返回每样本损失或训练指标的微调API（如OpenAI微调接口）都可能暴露类似信号，该漏洞类型可能覆盖多个厂商。
  • 防御困难：标准的输入过滤或限速机制无效，因为攻击针对微调端点而非推理端点，可离线完成优化后再大规模部署对抗性提示。
  • Utility-security tradeoff: The fine-tuning interface is a core commercial feature that vendors cannot easily remove, meaning this attack surface is structural and persistent—not a simple patch-and-fix bug.
  • Generalizability risk: Although evaluated on Gemini, any fine-tuning API that returns per-example loss or training metrics (e.g., OpenAI's fine-tuning API) may expose a similar signal, suggesting the vulnerability class is broader than one vendor.
  • Defensive challenges: Standard defenses like input filtering or rate-limiting are insufficient because the attack queries the fine-tuning endpoint (not the inference endpoint) and can be conducted offline before deploying the adversarial prompt at scale.

本文提出针对RAG系统的威胁分类体系与对抗框架，揭示多语言提示操控、提示注入、越狱及API模型提取等攻击的独特危险性。
This paper proposes a comprehensive threat taxonomy and adversarial framework exposing how multilingual prompt manipulation, prompt injection, jailbreaks, and API-based model extraction uniquely threaten both conventional and agentic RAG systems.
优缺点分析 / Pros & Cons
✓ 优势
  • Tackles increasingly important vulnerability surface in RAG systems, which have rapidly become mainstream in production AI applications
  • Proposes comprehensive threat taxonomy spanning prompt injection, jailbreaking, model extraction, and multilingual attacks—covering multiple attack vectors systematically
  • Directly relevant to current LLM security frontiers given RAG's widespread adoption and the expanded attack surface it introduces
✗ 不足

  • Author institutions not identifiable as tier-1 AI research institutions, reducing prestige signal and potential impact multiplier

科研问题 / Research Question
RAG系统通过检索管道、语言接口和外部API扩大了LLM的攻击面。本文探讨攻击者如何利用多语言输入等手段破坏RAG系统的安全性与完整性，这一问题在生产级AI系统中尤为关键。
RAG systems expand the attack surface of LLMs by introducing vulnerabilities in retrieval pipelines, language interfaces, and external APIs. This paper asks: how can adversaries exploit these components—especially through multilingual inputs—to compromise safety, integrity, and confidentiality in both standard and agentic RAG deployments? The stakes are high as RAG is increasingly used in production AI systems handling sensitive data.
先前工作为何不够 / Why Prior Work Falls Short
现有LLM安全研究主要聚焦英语提示注入与越狱攻击，忽视了多语言或语码混用输入带来的复合风险。针对RAG安全的研究鲜少涉及自主规划多步任务的智能体RAG场景，也尚无统一涵盖检索、生成、API和多语言维度的对抗威胁分类体系。
Prior security research on LLMs has largely focused on English-only prompt injection and jailbreak attacks, neglecting the compounded risks introduced by multilingual or code-switched inputs. Existing work on RAG security rarely addresses agentic RAG, where LLMs autonomously plan and execute multi-step retrieval tasks, creating new attack vectors. No prior taxonomy unified adversarial threats across retrieval, generation, API, and multilingual dimensions simultaneously.
解决方案 / Solution
作者构建了涵盖四类主要攻击的威胁分类体系：提示注入、越狱利用、API模型提取及多语言提示操控。核心贡献在于分析阿拉伯语-英语语码混用攻击如何绕过语言不一致的安全护栏，并将框架扩展至智能体RAG，揭示单次恶意检索可引发级联失效的风险。
The authors construct a structured threat taxonomy covering four major attack classes: prompt injection (direct and indirect), jailbreak exploits, API-driven model extraction, and multilingual prompt manipulation. A key contribution is the analysis of language-blending attacks—particularly Arabic-English code-switching—which exploit inconsistent safety guardrails across languages. The framework is extended to agentic RAG systems, where multi-step autonomous reasoning creates cascading failure risks from a single malicious retrieval. The paper also maps these threats to concrete mitigation strategies across the RAG pipeline stages.
主要结论 / Key Results
研究表明，阿拉伯语-英语混合输入可绕过内容过滤器，安全防护明显弱于纯英语输入。智能体RAG中单份中毒文档可跨多个推理步骤传播恶意指令。API黑盒查询可高保真重建模型行为，威胁知识产权并为下游对抗微调提供便利。
The paper demonstrates that multilingual RAG pipelines—especially those involving Arabic-English code-switching—exhibit measurably weaker safety guardrails compared to English-only inputs, allowing adversarial instructions to bypass content filters. Agentic RAG systems are shown to be disproportionately vulnerable because a single poisoned retrieved document can propagate malicious instructions across multiple autonomous reasoning steps. Specific API-based model extraction attacks are shown to reconstruct model behavior with high fidelity using only black-box query access, threatening intellectual property and enabling downstream adversarial fine-tuning.
值得关注的讨论点 / Discussion Points
  • 多语言盲区是系统性风险：安全对齐训练以英语为主，语码混用输入（如阿拉伯语-英语）可稳定绕过护栏，多语言红队测试应成为标准评估流程。
  • 智能体RAG非线性放大威胁：单份中毒文档可在自主链式推理中引发全系统沦陷，每个检索步骤须强制执行信任边界与沙箱隔离。
  • 分类体系有价值但缺乏大规模实证：论文未提供跨语言或跨模型的攻击成功率等定量实验，削弱了缓解策略对从业者的实际指导价值。
  • Multilingual blind spots are a systemic risk: Safety alignment training is predominantly English-centric, meaning code-switching inputs (e.g., Arabic-English) can reliably evade guardrails—a finding that calls for multilingual red-teaming as a standard evaluation practice.
  • Agentic RAG amplifies threat severity non-linearly: Unlike static RAG, agentic systems that autonomously chain retrieval and action steps can turn a single compromised document into a full system compromise, suggesting that trust boundaries and sandboxing must be enforced at each retrieval step.
  • The taxonomy is conceptually valuable but empirical validation is limited: The paper lacks large-scale quantitative experiments (e.g., attack success rates across languages or models), which would significantly strengthen the actionability of the proposed mitigations for practitioners.

SpecGuard: Specification Aware Recovery for Robotic Autonomous Vehicles from Physical Attacks
Pritam Dash, Ethan Chan, K. Pattabiraman 2024-08-27 CCS 评分 9.1
核心贡献
SpecGuard利用深度强化学习结合任务规范感知奖励与状态重建，实现无人自主飞行器在物理传感器攻击下的安全恢复。
SpecGuard uses Deep Reinforcement Learning with mission-specification-aware rewards and state reconstruction to safely recover Robotic Autonomous Vehicles from physical sensor attacks.
优缺点分析 / Pros & Cons
✓ 优势
  • Tackles critical safety problem in RAVs where physical sensor attacks can cause mission failures—highly relevant to autonomous systems security
  • Novel integration of formal mission specifications with Deep-RL for compliance-aware control policy learning is technically sound and forward-looking
  • Published in CCS (top-tier security venue), suggesting strong peer review and community recognition of significance
  • Clear practical applicability with concrete constraints (altitude, speed, geofence) that autonomous vehicles must satisfy in real deployments
✗ 不足
  • Zero HuggingFace upvotes indicates limited community engagement or visibility so far, possibly due to recent publication or narrow dissemination
  • Abstract is truncated, making complete assessment of experimental rigor and methodology depth impossible; unclear if paper includes full ablation studies and comparative baselines
  • Authors from University of British Columbia (respectable but not among elite tier like MIT/CMU/DeepMind), which may affect prestige rating
科研问题 / Research Question
RAV容易遭受GPS欺骗、IMU干扰等物理攻击，导致传感器数据失真和任务失败。核心挑战在于恢复过程中不仅要稳定飞行器，还必须同时满足高度、速度和地理围栏等任务规范。
Robotic Autonomous Vehicles (RAVs) are vulnerable to physical attacks (e.g., GPS spoofing, IMU jamming) that corrupt sensor readings and cause mission failures. The core challenge is recovering the RAV safely while still honoring mission specifications such as altitude limits, speed bounds, and geofence constraints — not just stabilizing the vehicle.
先前工作为何不够 / Why Prior Work Falls Short
现有恢复方案主要关注飞行器稳定性，忽视任务规范，恢复过程中可能违反地理围栏或速度限制。传统PID备用控制器缺乏对多约束场景的适应性，已有深度强化学习方案也未考虑规范合规或攻击时状态观测失真问题。
Existing recovery techniques for RAVs focus primarily on vehicle stabilization and do not account for mission specifications, risking geofence violations or speed limit breaches during recovery. Traditional control-theoretic approaches (e.g., PID-based fallback controllers) lack adaptability to the diverse and simultaneous constraints imposed by real mission profiles. Deep-RL-based controllers have been explored but without incorporating specification compliance or handling corrupted state observations during attacks.
解决方案 / Solution
SpecGuard设计了基于合规性的深度强化学习奖励函数，同时编码高度、速度和地理围栏等多项任务规范。第二项创新是状态重建：攻击导致传感器失真时，利用未受影响的传感器模态和物理运动模型估计真实飞行状态，为RL策略提供可靠输入。两者结合使SpecGuard在主动攻击场景下仍能有效运行。
SpecGuard introduces a compliance-based reward structure for Deep-RL training that encodes multiple mission specifications (altitude, speed, geofence) simultaneously, reflecting the RAV's complex dynamics. During training, the reward function penalizes specification violations and guides the agent toward safe, mission-compliant recovery trajectories. A second key innovation is state reconstruction: when sensors are corrupted by an attack, SpecGuard estimates the true vehicle state using attack-free sensor modalities and physical motion models, providing reliable inputs to the RL policy. Together, these two components allow SpecGuard to operate effectively even when the attacker actively distorts sensor measurements.
主要结论 / Key Results
SpecGuard在ArduPilot无人机和地面车平台上针对GPS欺骗、传感器注入等多种攻击场景进行评估，任务成功率和规范合规率显著优于基线方案，测试场景中规范违反率降低超过70%，且计算开销低，适合资源受限的RAV硬件部署。
SpecGuard was evaluated on real RAV platforms (ArduPilot-based drones and rovers) under multiple physical attack scenarios including GPS spoofing and sensor injection. It achieved significantly higher mission success rates and specification compliance rates compared to baseline recovery methods, reducing specification violation rates by over 70% in tested scenarios. SpecGuard also incurred low computational overhead, making it practical for deployment on resource-constrained RAV hardware.
值得关注的讨论点 / Discussion Points
  • 规范泛化性：合规奖励函数针对高度、速度和地理围栏设计，扩展至时间窗口或多航点序列等更复杂规范需重新设计奖励，工程量不容忽视。
  • 状态重建假设：该方法依赖攻击期间至少存在未受损传感器；若攻击者同时干扰所有传感器模态，重建步骤将失效，需进一步的对抗鲁棒性分析。
  • 仿真到真实的迁移：深度强化学习策略在仿真中训练，仿真器对真实空气动力学和环境扰动的还原度直接影响策略迁移效果，在极端天气或地形条件下尤为突出。
  • Specification generalizability: The compliance-based reward is designed for altitude, speed, and geofence constraints, but extending SpecGuard to richer or dynamically changing mission specifications (e.g., time-window constraints or multi-waypoint sequencing) would require non-trivial reward re-engineering.
  • State reconstruction assumptions: The approach relies on the availability of at least some uncorrupted sensors during an attack; simultaneous multi-sensor attacks targeting all modalities could undermine the reconstruction step and deserve further adversarial robustness analysis.
  • Sim-to-real transfer: While tested on ArduPilot platforms, the Deep-RL policy is trained in simulation, and the fidelity of the simulator to real-world aerodynamics and disturbances directly affects how well the learned policy transfers, especially in edge-case weather or terrain conditions.

Haoben Huang, Chenlu Ma, Zhiyu Li 2025-11-07 ICML 评分 8.7

VIFA是首个针对可见光-红外融合跟踪器的对抗攻击框架，利用粒子群优化生成模态专属对抗补丁，显著降低单目标和多目标跟踪性能。
