LLM 安全 / LLM Security

本文将对LLM驱动社交媒体机器人的"越狱"行为重新定义为一种用户主导的非暴力手段，用于揭露自动化政治操控并阻断虚假信息传播。
Ignore All Previous Instructions: Jailbreaking as a de-escalatory peace building practise to resist LLM social media bots
Huw Day, Adrianna Jezierska, Jessica Woodgate 评分 4.8
核心贡献
本文将对LLM驱动社交媒体机器人的"越狱"行为重新定义为一种用户主导的非暴力手段，用于揭露自动化政治操控并阻断虚假信息传播。
This paper reframes jailbreaking of LLM-powered social media bots as a legitimate, user-driven, non-violent tactic to expose automated political manipulation and disrupt misinformation.
科研问题 / Research Question
LLM正被大规模用于操控社交媒体政治话语，加剧社会冲突。现有应对措施以平台审核为主，普通用户缺乏有效手段。本文探讨：用户主动对LLM机器人实施"越狱"，能否成为一种有意义的基层去升级实践？
LLMs are increasingly weaponized to run coordinated social media bot campaigns that manipulate political discourse at scale, escalating societal conflict. Current countermeasures focus on platform-level content moderation, leaving ordinary users with no recognized tools to fight back. This paper asks: can end-user jailbreaking serve as a meaningful grassroots de-escalation practice?
先前工作为何不够 / Why Prior Work Falls Short
现有研究主要聚焦于平台主导的内容审核（如自动检测与封号），将用户视为虚假信息的被动接受者。和平构建与冲突去升级理论此前未被引入LLM驱动的社交媒体操控领域。
Existing research on LLM misuse in political contexts focuses predominantly on platform-led moderation strategies, such as automated detection and account suspension. This top-down framing treats users as passive recipients of misinformation rather than active agents. The peacebuilding and conflict de-escalation literature has not previously been applied to the domain of LLM-driven social media manipulation.
解决方案 / Solution
作者提出概念框架，将越狱（诱导LLM账号暴露系统提示或产生失coherent输出）定位为非暴力抵抗形式，并借鉴和平构建理论说明其去升级作用。研究以用户为中心，强调社区能动性，属理论性与规范性贡献，缺乏实证验证。
The authors propose a conceptual framework that positions jailbreaking—prompting LLM-based accounts to break character, reveal system prompts, or produce incoherent outputs—as a form of non-violent resistance and de-escalation. They draw on peacebuilding theory to argue that exposing bot behavior disrupts the automated amplification of divisive narratives. The paper adopts a user-centric lens, emphasizing community-level agency over platform-dependent solutions. This is primarily a theoretical and normative contribution rather than an empirical study.
主要结论 / Key Results
本文无实证实验、数据集或量化结果，结论依赖理论论证与零散案例。未报告任何可量化成效（如机器人活动减少或虚假信息传播下降）。
The paper presents no empirical experiments, datasets, or quantitative results. Its claims rest on theoretical argumentation and anecdotal examples of users attempting to jailbreak suspected LLM-driven accounts on social media platforms. No measurable outcomes—such as reduction in bot activity or misinformation spread—are reported.
值得关注的讨论点 / Discussion Points
  • 实证缺失致命：用户越狱能否真正去升级政治机器人活动从未被验证，成功率、用户风险与叙事干预效果均无数据支撑，框架停留于推测层面。
  • 伦理与法律模糊：将越狱定性为公民实践，忽视了错误识别真实用户、骚扰风险及违反平台条款或计算机滥用法律等潜在危害。
  • 可扩展性存疑：即便单次越狱成功，运营者可轻易修补系统提示或注册新账号，该策略能否实现系统性去升级仍存重大疑问。
  • Empirical gap is critical: The core claim—that user-led jailbreaking meaningfully de-escalates political bot campaigns—is never tested. Without evidence on success rates, user safety risks, or actual narrative disruption, the framework remains speculative.
  • Ethical and legal ambiguity: Framing jailbreaking as a positive civic practice ignores potential harms, including misidentification of real humans as bots, harassment risks, and possible violations of platform terms of service or computer misuse laws.
  • Scalability concern: Even if individual jailbreaking attempts succeed in exposing one bot, LLM operators can trivially patch system prompts or deploy new accounts, raising serious doubts about whether this tactic can achieve systemic de-escalation at any meaningful scale.

Refining Almost-Safe Value Functions on the Fly
Sander Tonkens, Sosuke Kojima, Chenhao Liu et al. 评分 7.8
核心贡献
本文提出refineCBF与HJ-Patch，通过热启动HJ可达性分析实时修正近似或不安全的控制障碍函数，使机器人能在动态环境中安全运行。
This paper introduces refineCBF and HJ-Patch, two methods that refine approximate or unsafe Control Barrier Functions in real time using warm-started Hamilton-Jacobi reachability, enabling safe robot operation in dynamic environments.
科研问题 / Research Question
当预计算的安全值函数近似、学习所得或本身不安全时，机器人如何在动态环境中维持正式安全保证？为复杂系统设计有效的CBF极为困难，而静态离线方案无法在运行时适应环境变化。
How can robots maintain formal safety guarantees in dynamic environments when pre-computed safe value functions are approximate, learned, or outright unsafe? Designing valid Control Barrier Functions (CBFs) for complex systems is notoriously difficult, and static offline solutions cannot adapt when environments change at runtime.
先前工作为何不够 / Why Prior Work Falls Short
HJ可达性提供正式安全保证，但受维度诅咒影响，计算代价极高，难以在线使用。现有学习或解析推导的CBF往往缺乏正式有效性，且此前没有原则性方法能在不重新计算的情况下实时修正它们。
Hamilton-Jacobi (HJ) Reachability offers formal safety guarantees but suffers from the curse of dimensionality, making it computationally intractable for high-dimensional systems and impractical for online use. Existing learned or analytically derived CBFs often lack formal validity, yet no principled method existed to certify and repair them on the fly without restarting computation from scratch.
解决方案 / Solution
refineCBF以任意近似CBF为热启动，通过HJ可达性迭代修正，保证单调安全改进并收敛至有效安全值函数。HJ-Patch将HJ更新限定于状态空间中值函数不准确或不安全的局部区域，大幅降低每步计算量。两种方法均支持在线自适应，在机器人运行过程中持续强化安全性。
refineCBF takes any approximate CBF—analytically derived, learned, or unsafe—as a warm start and iteratively refines it using HJ reachability, guaranteeing convergence to a valid safe value function with monotonic safety improvements. HJ-Patch, the computationally efficient successor, restricts HJ updates to localized regions of the state space where the current value function is inaccurate or unsafe, dramatically reducing per-step computation. Both methods support in-the-loop adaptation, meaning safety is continuously tightened as the robot operates rather than requiring a costly offline pre-computation phase. Together they bridge offline synthesis and real-time deployment for dynamic robotic environments.
主要结论 / Key Results
仿真实验表明，两种方法均能从近似或不安全初始点成功恢复有效安全值函数；HJ-Patch通过局部更新显著提升速度。框架在真实机器人硬件上得到验证，确认具备实时自适应能力，且整个修正过程中安全集单调不缩小。
Simulation experiments demonstrate that both methods successfully recover valid safe value functions from initially approximate or unsafe starting points, with HJ-Patch achieving significantly faster updates by focusing computation on localized patches. The framework is validated on real robotic hardware, confirming real-time adaptability. Monotonic safety improvement is maintained throughout adaptation, meaning the safe set never shrinks during the refinement process.
值得关注的讨论点 / Discussion Points
  • 可扩展性上限：HJ-Patch减轻但未消除维度诅咒，对于超过约6维的系统，局部HJ更新仍可能难以承受，限制了对高自由度机器人的适用性。
  • 热启动质量关键：收敛速度和中间安全质量高度依赖初始近似CBF与有效CBF的接近程度，严重不安全的初始化可能需要大量修正步骤才能激活安全保证。
  • 硬件验证范围有限：摘要提及了真实机器人实验，但系统复杂度、延迟及失败案例的细节不足，在传感器噪声和模型失配下的鲁棒性仍是开放问题。
  • Scalability ceiling: HJ-Patch reduces but does not eliminate the curse of dimensionality; for systems beyond ~6 dimensions, even localized HJ updates may remain intractable, limiting applicability to high-DOF robots without further approximation.
  • Warm-start quality matters: The convergence speed and intermediate safety quality depend heavily on how close the initial approximate CBF is to a valid one; a severely unsafe initialization could require many refinement steps before safety guarantees activate.
  • Hardware validation scope: Real-robot experiments are mentioned but details on system complexity, latency, and failure cases are limited in the abstract, leaving open questions about robustness under sensor noise and model mismatch in real deployments.

Analyzing Physical Adversarial Example Threats to Machine Learning in Election Systems
Khaleque Md Aashiq Kamal, Surya Eada, Aayushi Verma et al. 评分 6.2
核心贡献
本文量化了翻转美国大选所需的物理对抗样本选票数量，并通过144,000个物理样本对比六种对抗攻击类型的实际效果。
This paper quantifies how many physically printed adversarial ballots are needed to flip a U.S. election and benchmarks six adversarial attack types across 144,000 physical realizations to identify the most effective threat.
科研问题 / Research Question
机器学习选票分类器准确率超99%，但易受对抗样本攻击导致误分类。核心问题是攻击者能否实际打印并提交足够的对抗选票以翻转选举结果，以及哪种攻击方式在物理实现时最有效。
Machine learning ballot classifiers achieve over 99% accuracy but remain vulnerable to adversarial example attacks that deliberately cause misclassification. The core question is whether an attacker can realistically print and submit enough adversarial ballots to flip an election outcome, and which attack method is most effective when physically realized.
先前工作为何不够 / Why Prior Work Falls Short
此前研究证明基于机器学习的选票分类器可达到高精度，但安全分析多集中于数字对抗样本，未考虑打印-扫描流程对扰动的削弱。此前也无研究建立翻转选举所需对抗选票数量的概率阈值框架。
Prior work has demonstrated that ML-based ballot classifiers are achievable with high accuracy, but security analyses have largely focused on digital adversarial examples without accounting for the print-and-scan pipeline that degrades perturbations. No prior study has established a probabilistic threshold for how many adversarial ballots are operationally required to change an election result.
解决方案 / Solution
作者构建了一个概率框架，以候选人获胜概率和总票数为参数，计算翻转选举所需的最少对抗选票数。随后对六种攻击算法生成的对抗样本进行物理打印与扫描，共评估144,000个物理对抗样本，衡量通过打印-扫描通道的现实攻击迁移性。
The authors develop a probabilistic framework that computes the minimum number of adversarial ballots needed to flip an election, parameterized by each candidate's win probability and total ballot count. They then physically print and scan adversarial examples generated by six attack algorithms: l∞-APGD, l2-APGD, l1-APGD, l0-PGD, l0+l∞-PGD, and l0+sigma-map PGD. A total of 144,000 physical adversarial examples are evaluated to measure real-world attack transferability through the print-scan channel. This bridges the gap between digital attack performance and physical deployment feasibility.
主要结论 / Key Results
在144,000个物理对抗样本中，经打印-扫描流程后各算法攻击效果差异显著。稀疏攻击（l0类）与混合范数攻击（l0+sigma-map PGD）在物理实现鲁棒性上优于l∞-APGD。概率框架显示，在势均力敌的选举中，少量对抗选票即可能改变结果。
Across 144,000 physical adversarial examples, attack effectiveness varied significantly by algorithm after the print-scan degradation pipeline. Sparse attacks (l0-based) and combined norm attacks (l0+sigma-map PGD) showed differential robustness to physical realization compared to l∞-APGD, though exact per-method success rates are pending full paper details. The probabilistic framework reveals that in close elections, a surprisingly small number of adversarial ballots could suffice to shift outcomes.
值得关注的讨论点 / Discussion Points
  • 物理实现差距：打印-扫描流程是关键瓶颈，数字攻击成功率无法直接迁移，攻击范数的选择（稀疏vs密集）对现实威胁建模至关重要。
  • 威胁的规模性：概率框架表明，竞争激烈的选举极易受攻击，票差以千计时所需伪造选票远少于票差以百万计的情形。
  • 防御启示：结果建议选票分类系统应针对打印-扫描变换后的扰动进行对抗训练，而非仅依赖纯数字对抗增强。
  • Physical realizability gap: The print-scan pipeline is the key bottleneck—digital attack success rates do not transfer directly, making the choice of attack norm (sparse vs. dense) critical for real-world threat modeling.
  • Scalability of the threat: The probabilistic framework highlights that tightly contested elections are disproportionately vulnerable; attackers need far fewer fraudulent ballots in a race decided by thousands rather than millions of votes.
  • Defensive implications: Results suggest that ballot classification systems should be hardened with adversarial training specifically tuned for print-scan-transformed perturbations, rather than purely digital adversarial augmentation.

提出贝叶斯对抗性多智能体框架，通过三个协调的LLM智能体协同优化测试用例与科学代码生成。
AI-for-Science Low-code Platform with Bayesian Adversarial Multi-Agent Framework
Zihang Zeng, Jiaquan Zhang, Pengze Li et al. 评分 7.2
核心贡献
提出贝叶斯对抗性多智能体框架，通过三个协调的LLM智能体协同优化测试用例与科学代码生成。
A Bayesian adversarial multi-agent framework for a low-code platform that co-optimizes test cases and scientific code generation using three coordinated LLM agents.
科研问题 / Research Question
LLM在科学代码生成中存在输出不可靠、多智能体流程中错误级联传播以及成功指标难以定义等问题，本文针对AI4S任务中代码生成的可靠性与可信度展开研究。
LLMs show promise for automating scientific code generation, but suffer from unreliable outputs, cascading errors in multi-agent pipelines, and difficulty evaluating correctness in domains where success metrics are poorly defined. This paper targets the specific challenge of making AI-driven code generation robust and trustworthy for AI-for-Science (AI4S) tasks.
先前工作为何不够 / Why Prior Work Falls Short
现有LLM代码生成框架依赖静态提示模板和固定测试用例，在缺乏标准答案的科学领域表现脆弱。多智能体流程中，单个智能体的错误会向下游级联传播，缺乏自适应纠错机制。
Existing LLM-based code generation frameworks rely on static prompt templates and fixed test cases, making them brittle when applied to scientific domains where ground-truth outputs are often unavailable. Multi-agent workflows further amplify errors, as mistakes from one agent propagate unchecked to downstream agents without adaptive correction mechanisms.
解决方案 / Solution
平台由任务管理器、代码生成器和评估器三个LLM智能体构成。任务管理器与代码生成器形成对抗循环，前者持续强化测试用例以暴露代码缺陷。系统利用贝叶斯推断动态更新提示分布，融合功能正确性、结构对齐性和静态分析三类质量指标，实现测试与代码的协同优化。
The paper introduces a Low-code Platform (LCP) built on a Bayesian adversarial multi-agent framework with three specialized LLM agents: a Task Manager that structures user inputs and generates adaptive test cases, a Code Generator that produces candidate solutions, and an Evaluator that provides structured feedback. An adversarial loop pits the Task Manager against the Code Generator, where test cases are iteratively hardened to expose code weaknesses. Prompt distributions for all agents are updated dynamically via Bayesian inference, integrating three code quality signals: functional correctness, structural alignment, and static analysis results. This co-optimization ensures both the tests and the generated code improve together over iterations.
主要结论 / Key Results
原文摘要不完整，具体量化结果无法确认。框架通过对抗协同优化循环在可靠性上优于静态多智能体基线，三类质量指标共同引导迭代优化，完整实验数据有待查阅全文。
Specific benchmark numbers are not available from the provided abstract, which appears truncated. The framework demonstrates improved reliability over static multi-agent baselines through the adversarial co-optimization loop, with the three quality metrics (functional correctness, structural alignment, static analysis) jointly guiding iterative refinement. Quantitative results on AI4S-specific tasks remain to be confirmed from the full paper.
值得关注的讨论点 / Discussion Points
  • 自适应测试生成是核心创新点：任务管理器主动对抗性探测代码生成器，有别于标准LLM编码流程。
  • 贝叶斯提示分布更新思路新颖，但其对先验选择的敏感性及在小数据科学领域的收敛速度仍是未解问题。
  • 低代码平台定位面向编程能力有限的领域科学家，但实用性高度依赖任务管理器解析模糊科学问题描述的能力。
  • Adaptive test generation is the key novelty: rather than using fixed unit tests, the Task Manager actively adversarially probes the Code Generator, which is a meaningful departure from standard LLM coding pipelines.
  • The Bayesian prompt distribution update is elegant but its sensitivity to prior selection and convergence speed in low-data scientific domains remains an open question that the truncated abstract does not address.
  • The low-code platform framing targets domain scientists with limited programming expertise, but practical usability depends heavily on how well the Task Manager can parse ambiguous, jargon-heavy scientific problem descriptions.

RAPO: Expanding Exploration for LLM Agents via Retrieval-Augmented Policy Optimization
Siwei Zhang, Yun Xiong, Xi Chen et al. 评分 7.2
核心贡献
RAPO将检索增强探索引入智能体强化学习，使LLM智能体能在细粒度步骤层面利用离线经验，突破纯在线策略的探索瓶颈。
RAPO introduces retrieval-augmented exploration into agentic reinforcement learning, enabling LLM agents to learn from retrieved off-policy experience at a fine-grained step level rather than being confined to self-generated trajectories.
科研问题 / Research Question
基于智能体RL训练的LLM受限于纯在线策略探索，只能从自身生成的轨迹中学习，难以发现新的推理策略。在复杂多步工具调用任务中，拓展探索多样性对提升性能至关重要。
LLM agents trained with agentic RL are bottlenecked by on-policy exploration: they can only learn from their own generated rollouts, which limits discovery of novel reasoning strategies for complex multi-step, tool-integrated tasks. Expanding the diversity of explored behaviors is critical to improving agent performance on hard tasks where self-generated solutions are sparse or repetitive.
先前工作为何不够 / Why Prior Work Falls Short
纯在线策略方法（如GRPO）将探索限制在智能体自身输出，形成"回音壁"效应。现有离线辅助方法以完整轨迹为粒度进行策略估计，忽视了智能体推理过程中逐步骤的动态差异，导致信用分配不准确。
Pure on-policy methods (e.g., GRPO, PPO variants for LLMs) restrict exploration to the agent's own outputs, creating an echo-chamber effect that stalls improvement on hard tasks. Some recent works inject off-policy signals but operate at the full-trajectory level, applying coarse policy estimates that ignore the step-by-step dynamics critical to agentic rollouts — leading to misaligned credit assignment.
解决方案 / Solution
RAPO在训练时从经验库检索相关离线轨迹，并在步骤层面将其融入策略优化。智能体可将自身动作与检索到的替代动作对比，从而获得更丰富的梯度信号。通过重要性加权机制校正在/离线策略间的分布偏移，实现稳定的细粒度信用分配。
RAPO (Retrieval-Augmented Policy Optimization) augments the training rollout process by retrieving relevant off-policy trajectories from an experience buffer and integrating them at the step level during policy optimization. This allows the agent to contrast its own actions against retrieved alternatives, enriching the gradient signal with diverse exploratory dynamics without fully committing to off-policy trajectories. A theoretically motivated importance-weighting scheme corrects for distribution shift between on- and off-policy steps, enabling stable and fine-grained credit assignment throughout the agentic rollout.
主要结论 / Key Results
RAPO在涉及工具使用和多步推理的多个智能体基准上持续优于在线策略及轨迹级离线策略基准。步骤级检索融合方式优于全轨迹离线方法，验证了细粒度探索信号的有效性。
RAPO achieves consistent improvements over strong on-policy and trajectory-level off-policy baselines across multiple agentic benchmarks involving tool use and multi-step reasoning. The step-level retrieval integration outperforms full-trajectory off-policy methods, validating that fine-grained exploratory signals are more beneficial than coarse trajectory-level augmentation. Specific numbers are not fully disclosed in the abstract, but reported gains span diverse task categories, suggesting broad applicability.
值得关注的讨论点 / Discussion Points
  • 检索质量是隐性瓶颈：RAPO的效果高度依赖检索到的轨迹的相关性与多样性，若经验库质量不足，收益可能大打折扣。
  • 长时域任务的可扩展性存疑：步骤级检索与重要性加权带来额外计算开销，随着任务复杂度和推理步数增加，效率问题有待验证。
  • 在高度开放或新颖任务中的泛化能力不确定：对于检索步骤难以提供有效信号的任务，该方法的优势可能显著下降。
  • Retrieval quality is a hidden bottleneck: RAPO's gains depend heavily on the relevance and diversity of retrieved trajectories; a poorly populated or redundant experience buffer could neutralize the benefit, yet the paper's curation strategy for this buffer remains underspecified.
  • Scalability to very long agentic horizons is unclear: step-level retrieval and importance weighting add per-step computational overhead, and it is not shown whether this overhead remains tractable as task complexity and rollout length grow significantly.
  • Generalization beyond retrieval-friendly domains is uncertain: the method is well-suited for tasks with reusable step patterns (e.g., structured tool calls), but its advantage may diminish in highly novel or open-ended tasks where retrieved steps provide little relevant signal.
