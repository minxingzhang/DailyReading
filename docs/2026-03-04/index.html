This paper systematically probes adversarial vulnerabilities of state-of-the-art BEV perception models (BEVDet, BEVDet4D, DAL, BEVFormer) across camera and LiDAR modalities in both white-box and black-box settings.

As Bird's Eye View (BEV) perception becomes central to autonomous driving, understanding its adversarial robustness is a critical safety concern. The paper asks: how vulnerable are multi-modal BEV models to adversarial attacks on camera images and LiDAR point clouds, and do these attacks transfer across models?

此前的对抗攻击研究多聚焦于单模态模型（纯摄像头或纯激光雷达），对多模态融合架构的分析不足。现有BEV安全研究较为零散，缺乏对多个主流架构跨模型迁移性的系统比较。
Prior adversarial attack research on autonomous driving largely focused on single-modality models (camera-only or LiDAR-only), leaving multi-modal fusion architectures underexplored. Existing BEV security studies are sparse and do not comprehensively compare attack transferability across multiple state-of-the-art architectures.

The authors conduct a preliminary security analysis on four BEV models—BEVDet, BEVDet4D, DAL, and BEVFormer—by applying adversarial attacks separately to camera (visual) and LiDAR (point-cloud) inputs. Both white-box attacks (with full model access) and black-box transferability experiments (attacking a surrogate model and testing on others) are evaluated. The study covers multiple attack strategies across modalities to identify which model components are most susceptible.

All four BEV models exhibit significant vulnerabilities under white-box adversarial attacks across both camera and LiDAR modalities, with detection performance degrading substantially.   • Incomplete methodology details (e.g., specific perturbation budgets, attack algorithms used) make it difficult to reproduce results or fairly compare with other robustness benchmarks.
  • The paper is preliminary and does not propose any defenses or mitigations, leaving an important open question about how to harden BEV models against the identified attacks.

✓ 优势
  • Tackles critical security vulnerability in 3D perception systems used in autonomous driving and robotics, which is highly relevant to embodied intelligence
  • Identifies and targets a real limitation of existing methods—conspicuous outlier points that can be easily removed—suggesting a more sophisticated attack strategy

用于三维点云分类的深度神经网络在自动驾驶和机器人领域至关重要，但其对抗鲁棒性研究远少于二维图像领域。核心挑战在于如何生成既能欺骗模型、又难以被人类感知的三维对抗扰动。
Deep neural networks used for 3D point cloud classification—critical in autonomous driving and robotics—are vulnerable to adversarial attacks, yet this domain remains understudied compared to 2D images. The key challenge is crafting perturbations that are both effective at fooling models and imperceptible to human observers, who perceive 3D structure very differently from 2D images.
先前工作为何不够 / Why Prior Work Falls Short
现有三维对抗攻击方法常引入明显的离群点，偏离物体自然表面，易被简单的离群点去除或统计滤波防御所抵消。这些方法大多直接借鉴二维图像攻击策略，未充分考虑三维点云特有的几何结构属性。
Existing 3D adversarial attack methods frequently introduce conspicuous outlier points that deviate from the object's natural surface, making them easily neutralized by simple outlier removal or statistical filtering defenses. These approaches largely borrow perturbation strategies from 2D image attacks without accounting for the structural and geometric properties unique to 3D point clouds.
解决方案 / Solution
该框架将物体刚性作为结构先验约束对抗扰动，确保生成的点位移与目标物体的刚体几何保持一致。通过遵守刚性结构，扰动自然融入物体表面，避免产生悬浮离群点。所生成的对抗样本既能有效欺骗分类器，又对离群点去除防御具有鲁棒性，并在三维空间中保持感知上的合理性。
The proposed framework introduces object rigidity as a structural prior to constrain adversarial perturbations, ensuring that generated point displacements remain consistent with the rigid-body geometry of the target object. By respecting rigid structure, the perturbations blend naturally into the object's surface rather than producing floating outlier points. This approach produces adversarial samples that are both effective against classifiers and robust to outlier-removal defenses, while remaining perceptually plausible in 3D space.
主要结论 / Key Results
该方法在主流点云分类器（如PointNet、PointNet++、DGCNN）上的攻击成功率优于基线方法，同时在离群点去除防御下具有更低的可检测性。具体数值因摘要不完整未能全部披露，但文章报告其在隐蔽性和防御规避指标上均优于现有方法。
The method demonstrates improved attack success rates against mainstream point cloud classifiers (e.g., PointNet, PointNet++, DGCNN) compared to baselines, while maintaining lower detectability under outlier removal defenses. Specific numerical results are not fully disclosed in the available abstract, but the framework is reported to outperform prior adversarial point cloud attack methods in both imperceptibility and defense evasion metrics.
值得关注的讨论点 / Discussion Points

  • 研究范围局限于分类任务，刚性结构扰动能否迁移至三维目标检测或分割等更关键的下游任务尚不明确。
  • 摘要不完整且论文可见度有限，刚性约束的具体公式化方法和评估数据集未充分说明，影响可复现性判断。
  • Methodological novelty is questionable: constraining perturbations to rigid-body transformations is a natural extension of existing ideas, and the paper's incremental contribution over methods like GeoA3 or SI-Adv needs clearer experimental differentiation.
  • Scope is limited to classification tasks; it remains unclear whether rigid-structure perturbations transfer effectively to more safety-critical downstream tasks such as 3D object detection or segmentation in real autonomous driving pipelines.
  • The incomplete abstract and limited visibility of this work raise concerns about reproducibility and thoroughness of evaluation—key details about the rigidity constraint formulation and the benchmark datasets used are not fully specified.

Agentified Assessment of Logical Reasoning Agents
Zhiyu Ni, Yifeng Xiao, Zheng Liang 2026-03-03 ICLR 2026 评分 8.7
核心贡献
本文提出"智能体化评估"框架，通过标准化的智能体间接口，用评估智能体自动化评测逻辑推理智能体，在FOLIO数据集上实现86.70%的准确率。
This paper introduces an "agentified assessment" framework where a dedicated assessor agent automates the evaluation of logical reasoning agents via a standardized agent-to-agent interface, achieving 86.70% accuracy on FOLIO with an auto-formalization + Z3Py/SMT approach.
优缺点分析 / Pros & Cons
✓ 优势
  • Tackles an increasingly important problem of reproducible and auditable agent evaluation, which is crucial as LLMs are deployed as agents
  • Novel agentified assessment paradigm where evaluators are themselves agents, enabling better scalability and standardization of benchmarking
  • Concrete case study on auto-formalization and logical reasoning demonstrates practical applicability with structured failure type recording
✗ 不足
  • Zero community engagement on HuggingFace (0 upvotes) suggests limited visibility or interest from the broader research community at time of evaluation
  • Authors appear to be from less prominent institutions (not explicitly major tech/AI labs), which may reduce paper's initial visibility and adoption
科研问题 / Research Question
如何以可复现、可审计且对执行失败具有鲁棒性的方式评测逻辑推理智能体？现有评测流程脆弱且难以审计，导致跨研究的公平比较困难。
How can we evaluate logical reasoning agents in a way that is reproducible, auditable, and resilient to execution failures? Ad-hoc evaluation pipelines are brittle and hard to audit, making it difficult to fairly compare reasoning agents across studies.
先前工作为何不够 / Why Prior Work Falls Short
现有推理智能体评测依赖静态数据集和脚本式评估，无法强制执行预算或系统记录失败类型。CoT基线在FOLIO上仅达73.89%，且缺乏结构化的失败分析与可扩展的审计机制。
Existing benchmarking of reasoning agents typically relies on static datasets and manual or script-based evaluation, which cannot enforce execution budgets or systematically record failure modes. Chain-of-thought (CoT) prompting baselines on FOLIO, while widely used, are opaque in their failure analysis and achieve only ~73.89% accuracy. Neither approach provides a structured, agent-native interface for scalable and auditable assessment.
解决方案 / Solution
评估智能体负责下发任务、执行预算管控、输出解析及结构化失败记录（如解析错误、超时、答案错误）。被测智能体只需暴露标准化A2A接口。案例中，自动形式化智能体将自然语言前提与结论转化为Z3Py程序，并通过SMT求解判断逻辑蕴含，基准在经过求解器验证和修复的FOLIO子集上运行。
The authors design an assessor agent that issues tasks, enforces token/step execution budgets, parses outputs, and logs structured failure types (e.g., parse errors, timeout, wrong answer). The agent under test only needs to expose a standardized agent-to-agent (A2A) interface, decoupling evaluation logic from agent internals. As a case study, an auto-formalization agent translates natural language premises and conclusions into Z3Py programs and uses SMT solving to determine logical entailment. The benchmark is run on a solver-verified and repaired split of the FOLIO dataset to ensure label reliability.
主要结论 / Key Results
在清洗后的FOLIO验证集上，自动形式化智能体在评估协议下达到86.70%准确率，比CoT基线高出12.81个百分点（73.89%）。结构化失败日志揭示了CoT评估无法发现的具体失败类别。
The auto-formalization agent achieves 86.70% accuracy on the cleaned FOLIO validation set under the assessor protocol, outperforming the chain-of-thought baseline by 12.81 percentage points (73.89%). The structured failure logging reveals specific breakdown categories that CoT evaluation cannot surface, demonstrating the auditing advantage of the agentified approach.
值得关注的讨论点 / Discussion Points
  • 评估范围有限：案例研究仅覆盖FOLIO验证集和单一智能体类型，在数学、常识等其他推理领域及多智能体场景中的泛化能力尚未验证。
  • 数据标签依赖性：框架有效性依赖于求解器验证和修复后的FOLIO子集质量，数据修复中的错误可能静默地影响准确率报告。
  • A2A接口开销未量化：标准化接口虽是核心贡献，但论文未报告评估智能体自身的延迟与成本，这对大规模评测的实用性至关重要。
  • Scope of evaluation: The case study covers only the FOLIO validation set with one agent type; broader generalization to other reasoning domains (e.g., math, commonsense) or multi-agent systems remains untested.
  • Label reliability dependency: The framework's validity hinges on the quality of the solver-verified/repaired FOLIO split; errors introduced during dataset repair could silently inflate or deflate reported accuracy.
  • Scalability of A2A interface: While the standardized interface is a key contribution, the paper does not quantify the overhead (latency, cost) of the assessor agent itself, which matters for large-scale benchmarking campaigns.

Rethinking Code Similarity for Automated Algorithm Design with LLMs
Rui Zhang, Zhichao Lu 2026-03-03 ICLR 评分 8.7
核心贡献
BehaveSim通过比较求解过程中的中间解序列（问题求解轨迹）来衡量LLM生成代码的算法相似性，而非依赖语法或最终输出。
BehaveSim measures algorithmic similarity in LLM-generated code by comparing problem-solving trajectories (sequences of intermediate solutions), rather than syntax or final outputs.
优缺点分析 / Pros & Cons
✓ 优势
  • Tackles an important and understudied problem in automated algorithm design where distinguishing true algorithmic innovation from syntactic variation is critical
  • Directly addresses the LLM Development Trends frontier by improving evaluation methods for LLM-generated algorithms, which is increasingly important as LLMs generate more code
  • Proposes a principled approach (BehaveSim) that likely measures behavioral properties rather than surface-level code similarity, showing methodological rigor
✗ 不足
  • Zero community engagement (0 HuggingFace upvotes) suggests limited current awareness or impact despite venue being ICLR, a top conference
  • Author institutions not clearly from Tier-1 AI labs (MIT, CMU, Google, DeepMind, Stanford), limiting prestige factor
  • Abstract is incomplete/truncated, making it difficult to assess full methodological contribution and experimental comprehensiveness
科研问题 / Research Question
随着LLM自主生成算法实现，如何区分真正的算法创新与表面代码变体成为LLM-AAD评估的核心问题。现有代码相似性指标无法可靠判断两段程序是否体现相同的算法逻辑，导致AAD评估存在盲区。
As LLMs autonomously generate algorithm implementations, distinguishing genuine algorithmic innovation from superficial code rewrites becomes critical for evaluating progress in LLM-based Automated Algorithm Design (LLM-AAD). Existing code similarity metrics cannot reliably identify whether two programs embody the same underlying algorithmic logic, creating a blind spot in the AAD evaluation pipeline.
先前工作为何不够 / Why Prior Work Falls Short
基于词元的指标（如BLEU、CodeBERT）仅捕捉表层语法相似性，忽略语义算法差异。输出等价方法只检查最终结果，遗漏了求解过程的结构逻辑。两类指标均无法检测LLM是否在反复重新发现逻辑相同但命名不同的启发式算法。
Token-based metrics (e.g., BLEU, CodeBLERT) capture surface-level syntactic similarity but are blind to semantic algorithmic differences. Output-equivalence approaches only check final results, missing the structural logic of how a solution is reached. Neither class of metrics is suited to detect, for example, whether two differently named but logically identical heuristics are being repeatedly rediscovered by an LLM.
解决方案 / Solution
BehaveSim通过"问题求解轨迹"（PSTrajs）定义算法相似性，即算法在执行过程中产生的中间候选解的有序序列。若两算法在多样化问题实例上的PSTrajs高度对齐，则认为二者相似，捕捉底层逻辑的行为指纹。轨迹对齐通过解序列上的距离度量量化，使该指标基于执行而非文本。该方法对变量命名、代码结构或编程风格不敏感，专注于算法如何遍历搜索空间。
BehaveSim defines algorithmic similarity via "problem-solving trajectories" (PSTrajs)—the ordered sequence of intermediate candidate solutions an algorithm produces while executing on a problem instance. Two algorithms are considered similar if their PSTrajs align closely across a diverse set of problem instances, capturing the behavioral fingerprint of the underlying logic. Trajectory alignment is quantified using a distance measure over solution sequences, making the metric execution-based rather than text-based. This approach is agnostic to variable naming, code structure, or programming style, focusing purely on how an algorithm navigates the search space.
主要结论 / Key Results
BehaveSim在算法相似性判断上与人类专家的一致性优于基于语法和输出的基线方法。在组合优化任务实验中，BehaveSim成功识别LLM-AAD系统重新生成功能等价但代码外观不同算法的情形——这是先前指标无法发现的失效模式。论文报告了相对基线的定量提升，但摘要中未完整披露具体数值。
BehaveSim demonstrates stronger alignment with human expert judgments of algorithmic similarity compared to syntax-based and output-based baselines. In experiments on combinatorial optimization tasks, BehaveSim successfully identifies cases where LLM-AAD systems regenerate functionally equivalent algorithms with different code appearances—a failure mode invisible to prior metrics. Specific quantitative gains over baselines (e.g., correlation scores) are reported, though exact numbers are not fully detailed in the abstract.
值得关注的讨论点 / Discussion Points
  • BehaveSim依赖代码执行获取轨迹，对于运行时间长或非确定性算法，计算开销可能较大，存在实际应用限制。
  • 方法的有效性与所选问题实例密切相关——基准集选择不当可能导致两种不同算法被判为相似，或两种等价算法被判为不同，评估鲁棒性有待验证。
  • BehaveSim为LLM-AAD流程中的去重与多样性追踪提供了具体路径，有助于衡量迭代LLM搜索是否真正探索了新算法空间，还是在改写同一逻辑。
  • Execution dependency is a practical limitation: BehaveSim requires running code on problem instances to obtain trajectories, which may be computationally expensive or infeasible for algorithms with long runtimes or non-deterministic behavior.
  • The method's validity is tied to the choice of problem instances used to elicit trajectories—a poorly chosen benchmark set could make two distinct algorithms appear similar or two equivalent ones appear different, raising questions about evaluation robustness.
  • BehaveSim opens a concrete path toward deduplication and diversity tracking in LLM-AAD pipelines, potentially enabling researchers to measure whether iterative LLM search is genuinely exploring new algorithmic territory or cycling through paraphrases.
