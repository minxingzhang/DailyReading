VideoAgent: Long-form Video Understanding with Large Language Model as Agent
Xiaohan Wang, Yuhui Zhang, Orr Zohar et al. 2024-03-15 ECCV 评分 9.3
核心贡献
VideoAgent以大语言模型为核心智能体，迭代选取关键帧并调用视觉工具，以平均不足9帧实现具有竞争力的零样本视频问答性能。
VideoAgent uses an LLM as a central reasoning agent that iteratively selects and queries only the most relevant frames, achieving competitive zero-shot video QA accuracy with fewer than 9 frames on average.
优缺点分析 / Pros & Cons
✓ 优势
  • Novel agent-based architecture that cleverly uses LLMs as central coordinators for iterative video reasoning, directly aligning with agent-based LLM development trends
  • Impressive efficiency metrics (8.4 and 8.2 frames on average) demonstrate practical value for real-world deployment and computational efficiency
  • Strong empirical results on challenging benchmarks (EgoSchema 54.1%, NExT-QA 71.3% zero-shot) with clear methodology combining vision-language models as tools

科研问题 / Research Question
长视频理解需要对跨越数分钟乃至数小时的多模态序列进行推理，端到端模型在计算上代价极高。核心挑战在于无需处理每一帧，即可定位与问题相关的关键视觉片段，这与人类选择性注意的认知机制相似。
Long-form video understanding requires reasoning over extended multi-modal sequences spanning minutes or hours, which is computationally prohibitive for standard end-to-end models. The core challenge is identifying which visual moments are relevant to a given question without processing every frame, mirroring how humans selectively attend to video content.
先前工作为何不够 / Why Prior Work Falls Short
现有方法要么对密集帧输入的大型视频语言模型进行微调（计算代价极高），要么采用均匀帧采样（对无关内容浪费算力）。两种策略均未能主动规划视频的检索区域，导致长视频场景下效率与精度受限。
Existing approaches either fine-tune large video-language models on dense frame inputs—incurring enormous computational costs—or apply uniform frame sampling that wastes capacity on irrelevant content. Neither strategy explicitly plans which parts of the video to inspect, limiting both efficiency and accuracy on long-form content.
解决方案 / Solution
VideoAgent将长视频问答构建为迭代智能体循环：LLM（如GPT-4）担任中央规划器，在每一步判断信息是否充足或是否需要额外视觉查询。视觉语言工具（包括字幕模型和时序定位模型）按需调用，将视觉内容转化为LLM可推理的文本。LLM综合检索到的字幕与时序片段生成最终答案，仅使用其主动请求的帧，无需任务特定微调。
VideoAgent frames long-form video QA as an iterative agent loop: an LLM (e.g., GPT-4) acts as the central planner, deciding at each step whether enough information has been gathered or whether additional visual queries are needed. Vision-language tools—including a captioning model and a temporal grounding model—are called on demand to translate visual content into text the LLM can reason over. The LLM then synthesizes retrieved captions and temporal segments to produce a final answer, using only the frames it explicitly requested. This tool-augmented, question-driven design avoids processing the full video and requires no task-specific fine-tuning.
主要结论 / Key Results
VideoAgent在EgoSchema上达到54.1%的零样本准确率，在NExT-QA上达到71.3%，均超越此前零样本方法的最优水平。更关键的是，其平均仅使用8.4帧（EgoSchema）和8.2帧（NExT-QA），远低于消耗数十乃至数百帧的对比方法，在保持精度的同时显著提升效率。
VideoAgent achieves 54.1% zero-shot accuracy on EgoSchema and 71.3% on NExT-QA, surpassing prior state-of-the-art zero-shot methods on both benchmarks. Crucially, it uses only 8.4 frames on average for EgoSchema and 8.2 for NExT-QA, compared to methods that consume dozens or hundreds of frames, demonstrating strong efficiency gains without sacrificing accuracy.
值得关注的讨论点 / Discussion Points
  • 迭代查询循环的延迟随视频时长线性增长，LLM API调用成本在生产环境中可能难以承受，系统对超长视频（如小时级录像）的可扩展性仍是开放问题。
  • 系统推理质量与底层视觉语言工具的准确性高度耦合，字幕或时序定位的错误会直接传入LLM上下文并导致级联错误。
  • 基准测试仅覆盖多项选择题问答，可能高估真实场景性能；开放式生成或细粒度时序推理（如事件计数、动作排序）未经评估，极可能暴露更多局限性。
  • Scalability to very long videos (e.g., hours-long recordings) remains an open question, as the iterative query loop's latency scales with video length and LLM API call costs could become prohibitive in production settings.
  • The system's reasoning quality is tightly coupled to the accuracy of underlying vision-language tools; errors in captioning or temporal grounding propagate directly into the LLM's context and can cascade into wrong final answers.
  • The benchmark evaluations focus on multiple-choice QA, which may overstate real-world performance; open-ended generation tasks or fine-grained temporal reasoning (e.g., counting events, ordering actions) are not assessed and likely expose further limitations.

首篇系统综述多模态大语言模型数学推理的论文，涵盖200余项研究，聚焦基准、方法与五大AGI挑战。
The first comprehensive survey of mathematical reasoning in multimodal LLMs, reviewing 200+ studies across benchmarks, methods, and five key AGI challenges.

科研问题 / Research Question
现实数学问题天然包含图表、几何图形等多模态信息，但现有研究多局限于纯文本推理。本文聚焦：当前多模态大语言模型的数学推理能力如何，以及如何系统整合碎片化的基准与方法体系？
Mathematical reasoning is a critical benchmark for AGI, yet existing LLM research has largely treated it as a text-only problem, ignoring the multimodal nature of real-world math (diagrams, charts, geometric figures). This survey asks: how well do current multimodal LLMs (MLLMs) handle mathematical reasoning, and what systematic framework can unify the fragmented landscape of benchmarks and methods?
先前工作为何不够 / Why Prior Work Falls Short
早期综述仅关注文本数学推理（如GSM8K、MATH），忽视几何、图表等视觉输入。2021年以来多模态基准与方法快速涌现，但缺乏统一分类框架，视觉编码与数学验证的协同机制尚未被系统梳理。
Earlier surveys on LLM mathematical reasoning focused exclusively on text-based tasks (e.g., GSM8K, MATH), overlooking visual and diagrammatic inputs that are central to geometry, physics, and chart interpretation. Multimodal benchmarks and MLLM-specific methods have emerged rapidly since 2021 but lack a unified taxonomy. No prior work has systematically mapped the interplay between visual encoders, reasoning pipelines, and mathematical correctness verification in multimodal settings.
解决方案 / Solution
本文从三维度构建框架：①基准——按模态、任务类型（算术、几何、定理证明）分类200余个数据集；②方法——梳理从视觉感知到符号/神经推理再到答案验证的完整流程；③挑战——归纳制约AGI数学推理的五大瓶颈，重点分析思维链提示、工具增强推理及微调策略。
The authors organize the field along three dimensions: (1) benchmarks — categorizing 200+ datasets by modality, task type (arithmetic, geometry, theorem proving), and difficulty; (2) methodologies — mapping the multimodal math reasoning pipeline from visual perception through symbolic/neural reasoning to answer verification; and (3) challenges — identifying five core bottlenecks for AGI-level math reasoning. Special attention is given to the roles of visual encoders, chain-of-thought prompting, tool-augmented reasoning (code interpreters, symbolic solvers), and fine-tuning strategies for MLLMs.
主要结论 / Key Results
前沿模型（如GPT-4V、Gemini）在MathVista、GeoQA等基准上与人类仍存在显著差距，尤其在多步几何推理和定理证明上表现薄弱。工具增强方法（结合Python解释器或符号求解器）在结构化数学任务上持续优于纯神经网络方法。
The survey finds that state-of-the-art MLLMs (e.g., GPT-4V, Gemini) still struggle with complex multi-step geometric reasoning and theorem proving, with significant performance gaps versus humans on benchmarks like MathVista and GeoQA. Tool-augmented approaches (combining LLMs with Python interpreters or symbolic solvers) consistently outperform pure neural methods on structured mathematical tasks. Five major challenges are identified: robust visual-symbolic grounding, multi-step reasoning consistency, out-of-distribution generalization, formal proof generation, and evaluation metric reliability.
值得关注的讨论点 / Discussion Points
  • 基准饱和风险：200余个数据集中大量任务类型重叠（如初等算术），模型可能过拟合于狭窄技能，未来基准应优先覆盖证明验证、跨模态抽象等新型推理范式。
  • 工具增强的本质疑问：代码解释器增强方法虽提升显著，但这些系统常绕过真正的数学理解，依赖暴力计算，基准分数是否真实反映推理能力值得深思。
  • 评估指标的局限性：当前评估过度依赖精确匹配或选择题准确率，无法捕捉多步推理的部分正确性，亟需开发面向推理过程的评估方法。
  • Benchmark saturation risk: With 200+ datasets reviewed, many benchmarks share overlapping problem types (e.g., elementary arithmetic), risking overfitting to narrow skills rather than measuring genuine mathematical reasoning breadth — future benchmarks should prioritize novel reasoning paradigms like proof verification or cross-modal abstraction.
  • The tool-augmented reasoning gap: While code-interpreter-augmented MLLMs show strong gains, the survey reveals these systems often bypass true mathematical understanding by executing brute-force computation, raising questions about whether benchmark scores reflect real reasoning capability or sophisticated lookup.
  • Evaluation metric fragility: Current evaluation relies heavily on exact-match or multiple-choice accuracy, which fails to capture partial correctness in multi-step proofs or diagram interpretation; developing process-level evaluation (scoring reasoning chains, not just final answers) is a critical unresolved challenge.

CPath-Omni: A Unified Multimodal Foundation Model for Patch and Whole Slide Image Analysis in Computational Pathology

CPath-Omni是首个统一切片级与全切片图像分析的150亿参数多模态模型，在42个基准中的39个上达到最优性能。
CPath-Omni is the first 15B-parameter multimodal model unifying patch-level and whole-slide image analysis across seven pathology tasks, achieving state-of-the-art on 39 of 42 benchmarks.

✓ 优势

  • Comprehensive evaluation across 39/42 datasets with SOTA performance on diverse tasks (classification, VQA, captioning, referring prompting)

科研问题 / Research Question
计算病理学需同时分析图像切片和全切片图像（WSI），但现有模型将两者分开训练，导致知识无法跨层级迁移，产生冗余的任务专用模型，维护和部署成本高。
Computational pathology requires analyzing both small image patches and gigapixel whole-slide images (WSIs), yet existing models treat these as separate problems requiring separate training pipelines. This fragmentation prevents knowledge transfer between patch and WSI levels and leads to redundant, task-specific models that are costly to maintain and deploy.
先前工作为何不够 / Why Prior Work Falls Short
此前研究分别训练切片级和WSI级模型，限制了跨层级知识整合。现有病理视觉-语言模型依赖单一视觉编码器，难以捕捉多尺度组织特征，且各任务需单独微调，造成流程冗余。
Prior work trained patch-level and WSI-level models independently, limiting cross-level knowledge integration. Existing pathology vision-language models also relied on single vision encoders, constraining their ability to capture multi-scale histological features. Task-specific models further required separate fine-tuning for classification, VQA, captioning, and visual referring, creating redundancy across pipelines.
解决方案 / Solution
CPath-Omni基于统一架构，在单一框架内处理切片和WSI输入，支持分类、VQA、描述生成和视觉指代提示等任务。同时开发了CPath-CLIP，首次融合多种异构视觉模型并引入大语言模型增强视觉表征，作为CPath-Omni的视觉主干，实现多尺度特征提取。
CPath-Omni is a 15B-parameter large multimodal model built on a unified architecture that handles patch and WSI inputs within a single framework, supporting classification, VQA, captioning, and visual referring prompting. The authors also develop CPath-CLIP, a specialized pathology CLIP-based visual processor that, for the first time, integrates multiple heterogeneous vision models and incorporates a large language model to enrich visual representations. CPath-CLIP serves as the visual backbone for CPath-Omni, enabling robust multi-scale feature extraction critical for both patch and WSI analysis. Together, these components eliminate the need for separate task-specific models across seven distinct pathology tasks.
主要结论 / Key Results
CPath-Omni在涵盖七类任务的42个数据集中的39个上达到最优，超越或持平各任务专用模型。CPath-CLIP在病理视觉表征基准上也独立取得新的最优结果。
CPath-Omni achieves state-of-the-art performance on 39 out of 42 datasets spanning seven diverse pathology tasks, outperforming or matching task-specific models trained individually for each task. This is notable because task-specific models benefit from focused training, yet a single unified 15B model surpasses them in the vast majority of benchmarks. CPath-CLIP also independently sets new SOTA results on pathology visual representation benchmarks.
值得关注的讨论点 / Discussion Points
  • 全切片图像的可扩展性仍是挑战：150亿参数模型是否真正利用了长程空间上下文，还是依赖局部切片特征的事后聚合，尚不明确。
  • 150亿参数规模对GPU显存要求极高，HuggingFace社区参与度有限，暗示在资源受限的临床环境中部署存在现实障碍。
  • 单模型统一七类任务存在负迁移风险，尤其是细粒度切片分级与粗粒度WSI分类之间；需要更充分的任务干扰消融实验来验证结论。
  • Scalability to full WSIs remains a core challenge: gigapixel images require aggressive tiling and aggregation strategies, and it is unclear whether the 15B unified model truly exploits long-range spatial context or relies on local patch features aggregated post-hoc.
  • The limited HuggingFace community engagement noted in the score rationale suggests adoption barriers—likely due to the 15B parameter scale requiring substantial GPU memory, raising questions about clinical deployability in resource-constrained hospital settings.
  • Unifying seven tasks in one model risks negative transfer, especially between fine-grained patch-level grading tasks and coarse WSI-level classification; ablations on task interference and the contribution of CPath-CLIP versus the LMM backbone would strengthen the paper's conclusions.
