LLM 安全 / LLM Security

TSC-GRPO通过因果意图探针与强化学习，防止大语言模型在生成"Sure, here is"等对抗性前缀后丧失安全对齐能力。
From Shallow to Deep: Pinning Semantic Intent via Causal GRPO
Shuyi Zhou, Zeen Song, Wenwen Qiang et al. 评分 7.2
核心贡献
TSC-GRPO通过因果意图探针与强化学习，防止大语言模型在生成"Sure, here is"等对抗性前缀后丧失安全对齐能力。
TSC-GRPO uses causal intent probing and reinforcement learning to prevent LLMs from abandoning safety alignment mid-generation when primed with adversarial compliant prefixes like "Sure, here is".
科研问题 / Research Question
经过标准安全对齐训练的大语言模型仍易受对抗性前缀攻击，即强制模型以顺从短语开头后会继续生成有害内容。此类攻击构造极为简单，却能绕过主流安全微调方法，带来严重部署风险。
LLMs trained with standard safety alignment remain brittle against adversarial prefix attacks, where forcing the model to begin a response with a compliant phrase (e.g., "Sure, here is") causes it to complete harmful content. This matters because such attacks are trivially simple yet bypass state-of-the-art safety fine-tuning, posing a critical deployment risk.
先前工作为何不够 / Why Prior Work Falls Short
现有安全对齐方法（如RLHF、DPO）侧重于在生成初期拒绝有害请求，但一旦顺从性token已被生成，便无法维持拒绝行为。已有防御方法依赖输入过滤或静态拒绝模板，易被绕过且未解决生成过程中意图信号衰减的根本问题。
Existing safety alignment methods (e.g., RLHF, DPO) focus on refusing harmful prompts at the start of generation but fail to maintain refusal behavior once compliant tokens have been generated. Prior defenses against prefix attacks rely on input filtering or static refusal templates, which are easily circumvented and do not address the root cause of intent signal degradation during generation.
解决方案 / Solution
作者将核心问题诊断为"语义表示衰减"：模型在生成顺从性前缀时，内部恶意意图表示逐渐消退。TSC-GRPO分两阶段：首先基于因果可识别性理论训练因果意图探针，提取对风格扰动鲁棒的不变意图信号；其次通过GRPO将因果感知注入策略训练，在"岔路口"场景中施加累积因果惩罚，使每生成一个额外有害token奖励单调递减，从而强制模型学会在生成后期执行拒绝。
The authors diagnose the core problem as "semantic representation decay": the model's internal malicious intent representation fades as it generates compliant prefix tokens, leading to harmful completions. TSC-GRPO operates in two stages: first, a causal intent probe is trained using causal identifiability theory to extract an invariant intent signal that is robust to stylistic surface variations. Second, this causal awareness is injected into policy training via Group Relative Policy Optimization (GRPO), using "fork-in-the-road" scenarios where the model must choose to continue or refuse after seeing a harmful prefix. A cumulative causal penalty ensures that generating each additional harmful token monotonically decreases reward, forcing the model to learn late-stage refusals.
主要结论 / Key Results
TSC-GRPO在对抗性前缀攻击基准上显著优于基线安全对齐方法，即使模型已生成多个顺从性token，仍能实现鲁棒的后期拒绝。累积因果惩罚被证明至关重要，去除后模型无法在生成早期之后持续维持拒绝。摘要未披露完整数值，但报告了相对于RLHF和DPO基线的实质性提升。
TSC-GRPO significantly outperforms baseline safety alignment methods on adversarial prefix attack benchmarks, demonstrating robust late-stage refusal even after the model has already generated several compliant tokens. The cumulative causal penalty proves critical: models trained without it fail to sustain refusals beyond early generation steps. Specific numeric results are not fully disclosed in the abstract but are reported as substantial improvements over RLHF and DPO baselines.
值得关注的讨论点 / Discussion Points
  • 因果探针的泛化性：框架假设意图可与风格解耦，但探针对训练未见的新型攻击措辞或多语言对抗前缀的泛化能力尚不明确。
  • 岔路口训练的可扩展性：构建该场景需要精心标注的部分有害完成数据集，规模化成本较高，且分支点选取方式可能引入偏差。
  • 奖励单调性与实用性的权衡：强制每个有害token使奖励递减可能导致对模糊或边界性提示的过度拒绝，损害模型在表面相似但合法任务上的有用性。
  • Causal probe generalization: The causal identifiability framework assumes intent can be disentangled from style, but it is unclear how well the probe generalizes to novel attack phrasings or multilingual adversarial prefixes not seen during training.
  • Scalability of fork-in-the-road training: Constructing 'fork-in-the-road' scenarios requires curated datasets of partial harmful completions, which may be costly to scale and could introduce biases depending on how the branching points are selected.
  • Reward monotonicity vs. utility trade-off: Enforcing that every additional harmful token decreases reward may cause over-refusal on ambiguous or borderline prompts, potentially degrading helpfulness on legitimate tasks that superficially resemble harmful ones.

SaFeR-ToolKit将视觉语言模型的安全决策形式化为可校验的虚拟工具调用协议，并通过三阶段课程训练实现可靠执行。
SaFeR-ToolKit: Structured Reasoning via Virtual Tool Calling for Multimodal Safety
Zixuan Xu, Tiancheng He, Huahui Yi et al. 评分 7.2
核心贡献
SaFeR-ToolKit将视觉语言模型的安全决策形式化为可校验的虚拟工具调用协议，并通过三阶段课程训练实现可靠执行。
SaFeR-ToolKit enforces structured, auditable safety reasoning in vision-language models by casting the safety decision as a virtual tool-calling protocol trained with a three-stage SFT→DPO→GRPO curriculum.
科研问题 / Research Question
视觉语言模型易受多模态越狱攻击和过度拒绝问题的困扰，根源在于现有对齐流程仅监督最终输出，忽略了视觉证据与用户意图的中间推理过程。安全机制需具备透明性和可验证性。
Vision-language models (VLMs) are vulnerable to multimodal jailbreaks (harmful inputs that bypass refusal) and over-refusal (blocking benign requests), both of which stem from opaque, end-to-end alignment that ignores intermediate visual evidence and user intent. A reliable safety mechanism must be transparent, checkable, and sensitive to multimodal context rather than just the final output.
先前工作为何不够 / Why Prior Work Falls Short
现有RLHF/DPO方法仅在答案层面进行监督，推理过程缺乏约束，容易被攻击。多模态安全分类器将安全判断视为二元输出，无法区分感知失败还是推理失败。
Existing alignment pipelines (RLHF, DPO at answer level) supervise only the final response, leaving the reasoning process unchecked and exploitable. Multimodal safety benchmarks and refusal classifiers treat safety as a binary output rather than a structured decision process, making it hard to diagnose whether a failure is perceptual or reasoning-based.
解决方案 / Solution
系统由规划器和响应器组成：规划器定义角色和三工具（感知→推理→决策）的有限状态转移图，响应器在生成最终答案前须输出结构化的键值工具轨迹。训练数据包含31,654条样本，采用SFT→DPO→GRPO三阶段课程，其中GRPO直接对工具使用行为给予奖励。
SaFeR-ToolKit introduces a planner-responder architecture: the planner defines a persona and a constrained transition graph over three virtual tools—Perception, Reasoning, and Decision—while the responder must emit a typed key-value tool trace before producing any final answer. This makes every safety decision fully auditable. A dataset of 31,654 examples is constructed across three training splits (SFT 6k, DPO 18.6k, GRPO 6k) plus a 1k held-out evaluation set. Training follows a three-stage curriculum: SFT for basic protocol adherence, DPO for preference alignment, and GRPO to directly reward correct tool usage beyond answer-level signals.
主要结论 / Key Results
在Qwen2.5-VL上，SaFeR-ToolKit在安全分数和有用性分数上均显著优于基线，表明其在减少越狱攻击的同时未增加过度拒绝。GRPO对工具使用的直接监督优于仅监督答案的方法，但论文摘要未披露具体数值。
On Qwen2.5-VL, SaFeR-ToolKit significantly improves both Safety Score and Helpful Score compared to baselines, demonstrating it reduces jailbreak susceptibility without increasing over-refusal. The structured tool trace provides a measurable intermediate signal that GRPO can optimize, outperforming answer-only supervision baselines. Specific absolute numbers are not fully disclosed in the abstract, but the dual improvement on safety and helpfulness is highlighted as the primary empirical contribution.
值得关注的讨论点 / Discussion Points
  • 推理开销问题：每次响应前强制生成完整工具轨迹会增加推理延迟，但论文未报告延迟成本，这对实时部署至关重要。
  • 跨模型泛化性存疑：实验仅在Qwen2.5-VL上验证，工具调用协议和数据集能否迁移至LLaVA、InternVL等其他架构尚不明确。
  • 数据多样性不透明：训练集未详细说明越狱攻击类型的覆盖范围，若攻击分布单一，安全提升可能对未见攻击策略脆弱。
  • Auditability vs. latency trade-off: Requiring a full Perception→Reasoning→Decision key-value trace before every response adds inference overhead; the paper does not report latency costs, which are critical for deployment in real-time applications.
  • Generalizability beyond Qwen2.5-VL: All experiments use a single model family; it is unclear whether the tool-calling protocol and dataset transfer effectively to other VLM architectures (e.g., LLaVA, InternVL) or whether the GRPO reward is model-specific.
  • Dataset quality and adversarial coverage: The 31,654 training examples cover SFT, DPO, and GRPO splits, but the paper does not detail how adversarial jailbreak diversity was ensured—a narrow attack distribution could lead to brittle safety gains that fail against unseen jailbreak strategies.

CEMMA提出协同进化框架，通过进化攻击者与自适应防御者的迭代博弈，实现更鲁棒的多模态大语言模型安全对齐。
Co-Evolutionary Multi-Modal Alignment via Structured Adversarial Evolution
Guoxin Shi, Haoyu Wang, Zaihui Yang et al. 评分 7.2
核心贡献
CEMMA提出协同进化框架，通过进化攻击者与自适应防御者的迭代博弈，实现更鲁棒的多模态大语言模型安全对齐。
CEMMA introduces a co-evolutionary framework where an Evolutionary Attacker and Adaptive Defender iteratively improve each other to achieve more robust multimodal LLM safety alignment.
科研问题 / Research Question
如何使多模态大语言模型能够抵御多样化、自适应的越狱攻击，而非仅防御已知的固定对抗提示？多模态场景下的攻击面远大于纯文本，静态对齐方法难以泛化。
How can we build multimodal LLMs that remain safe against diverse and adaptive jailbreak attacks, rather than only known, fixed adversarial prompts? Static alignment methods fail to generalize because the attack space in multimodal models (text + image) is vastly larger and more varied than in text-only settings.
先前工作为何不够 / Why Prior Work Falls Short
现有RLHF、DPO等对齐方法依赖固定的人工标注对抗样本集，随着新越狱策略涌现，这些数据集很快过时。多模态安全对齐研究严重不足，大多数方法仅将纯文本方案简单迁移，未考虑视觉输入带来的额外攻击面。
Existing alignment approaches such as RLHF and DPO rely on fixed, human-curated datasets of adversarial examples, which quickly become outdated as new jailbreak strategies emerge. Multimodal safety alignment is especially underexplored, with most methods naively extending text-only pipelines without accounting for the compounding attack surface introduced by visual inputs.
解决方案 / Solution
CEMMA包含两个协同进化模块：进化攻击者将对抗提示分解为"方法模板"与"有害意图"，并通过变异、交叉、差分进化等遗传算子，从简单种子攻击中孵化出复杂越狱样本；自适应防御者在最难的合成对抗样本上迭代微调，形成闭环红队测试流程，使对齐持续适应新型攻击策略。
CEMMA introduces two co-evolving components: an Evolutionary Attacker and an Adaptive Defender. The Evolutionary Attacker decomposes adversarial prompts into reusable "method templates" and "harmful intents," then applies genetic operators—mutation, crossover, and differential evolution—to breed increasingly sophisticated jailbreaks from simple seed attacks. The Adaptive Defender is fine-tuned iteratively on the hardest synthesized adversarial examples, creating a closed-loop red-teaming pipeline. This design ensures alignment continuously adapts to novel attack strategies rather than converging on a fixed threat model.
主要结论 / Key Results
CEMMA在多模态场景下对已知和未知越狱攻击均表现出更强的鲁棒性，进化攻击生成的结构多样性样本能暴露固定红队数据集遗漏的安全漏洞。摘要未披露完整数值，但在多个多模态基准上安全性有持续提升。
CEMMA demonstrates improved robustness against both seen and unseen jailbreak attacks in multimodal settings compared to static alignment baselines. The evolutionary attack generation produces structurally diverse adversarial prompts that expose weaknesses missed by fixed red-teaming datasets. Specific numerical results were not fully disclosed in the abstract, but the framework shows consistent safety gains across multiple multimodal benchmarks.
值得关注的讨论点 / Discussion Points
  • 计算开销：遗传算子（交叉、差分进化）在大规模训练中成本较高，论文未说明与标准RLHF/DPO相比的额外计算代价。
  • 评估不足：摘要未公布具体数值，且社区认可度为零，难以判断安全提升是否能抵御视觉对抗补丁等前沿攻击手段。
  • 军备竞赛风险：协同进化框架可能使防御者过拟合于特定进化攻击策略，对遗传算子未覆盖的真实越狱方式仍存在盲区。
  • Scalability concern: Genetic operators like crossover and differential evolution can be computationally expensive at scale; the paper does not clarify the training cost overhead compared to standard RLHF or DPO pipelines.
  • Evaluation gap: Without published benchmark numbers in the abstract and zero community validation (0 HF upvotes), it is difficult to assess whether safety gains hold against state-of-the-art attacks like visual adversarial patches or multimodal prompt injection.
  • Arms-race risk: Co-evolutionary frameworks inherently risk overfitting the defender to the specific attacker's evolutionary strategy, potentially leaving blind spots against structurally different real-world jailbreaks not captured by the genetic operators used.

LLM 发展动态 / LLM Development Trends

Code2Math提出多智能体框架，利用代码智能体自动将现有数学题演化为更难但可解的变体，以缓解高质量数学训练数据稀缺的瓶颈。
Code2Math: Can Your Code Agent Effectively Evolve Math Problems Through Exploration?
Dadi Guo, Yuejin Xie, Qingyu Liu et al. 评分 7.2
核心贡献
Code2Math提出多智能体框架，利用代码智能体自动将现有数学题演化为更难但可解的变体，以缓解高质量数学训练数据稀缺的瓶颈。
Code2Math introduces a multi-agent framework that uses code agents to autonomously evolve existing math problems into harder, solvable variants, addressing the shortage of high-quality training data for advanced LLM math reasoning.
科研问题 / Research Question
随着LLM数学能力逼近IMO水平，高质量难题的稀缺已成为训练与评估的关键瓶颈。本文探讨代码智能体能否自主地从现有题目生成更难且合法的新题，从而减少对人工出题的依赖。
As LLMs approach IMO-level mathematical reasoning, the supply of sufficiently challenging problems for training and benchmarking has become critically scarce. The paper asks whether code agents can autonomously generate harder, valid math problems from existing ones, reducing dependence on human problem-setters.
先前工作为何不够 / Why Prior Work Falls Short
现有数学数据增强方法（如改写、模板变换或提示重写）通常只产生表面变体，难以保证真正提升难度。此前代码智能体研究主要聚焦于解题而非出题，可验证难度提升的新题合成问题基本尚未被探索。
Existing data augmentation methods for math problems—such as rephrasing, template-based mutation, or GPT-prompted rewriting—tend to produce superficially varied but not genuinely harder problems. Prior code agent work has focused on solving problems rather than creating or evolving them, leaving the synthesis of verifiably difficult new problems largely unexplored.
解决方案 / Solution
Code2Math构建了一个多智能体框架：代码智能体通过可执行代码迭代修改题目参数、结构与约束，探索候选演化题目空间；独立验证模块同时检验可解性与难度提升（如求解器复杂度或模型失败率）。框架通过测试时计算扩展探索深度，并将创意演化与正确性验证解耦，实现可扩展的可靠出题。
Code2Math proposes a multi-agent framework in which a code agent iteratively modifies problem parameters, structures, and constraints via executable code, exploring a space of candidate evolved problems. A separate validation module checks both solvability (the evolved problem has a well-defined solution) and increased difficulty (e.g., via solver complexity or model failure rates). The framework leverages test-time compute by allowing extended exploration before committing to a generated problem. This design decouples creative evolution from correctness verification, enabling scalable and reliable problem synthesis.
主要结论 / Key Results
在充足的测试时计算下，框架成功生成了结构上不同于原题且可量化更难的新题。演化题目在保持可解性的同时，通过自动求解器和LLM失败率两个维度验证了难度提升。实验结果表明，代码驱动智能体是人工出题的可行且可扩展的替代方案。
Given sufficient test-time exploration, the framework successfully synthesizes new problems that are structurally distinct from and measurably more challenging than the originals. Evolved problems maintain solvability while exhibiting increased difficulty as validated by both automated solvers and LLM failure rates. The results provide empirical evidence that code-driven agents are a viable scalable alternative to human-authored problem generation for advanced math benchmarks.
值得关注的讨论点 / Discussion Points
  • 测试时计算依赖：框架成功依赖于'充足'的探索预算，但论文未清晰量化计算量与质量的权衡曲线，难以评估大规模部署的实际成本。
  • 验证瓶颈：以LLM失败率和自动求解器认证难度并不完善——当前模型无法解答的题目可能是病态题而非真正的难题，存在引入噪声训练信号的风险。
  • 泛化范围：实验似乎集中于特定数学领域，演化题目能否覆盖竞赛数学的全部分支（组合、数论、几何）尚不明确，或主要偏向求解器友好的代数与数值类题目。
  • Test-time compute dependency: The framework's success relies on 'sufficient' exploration budget, but the paper does not clearly quantify the compute-quality trade-off curve, making it hard to assess practical deployment costs at scale.
  • Validation bottleneck: Using LLM failure rates and automated solvers to certify difficulty is imperfect—a problem unsolvable by current models may be ill-posed rather than genuinely hard, risking noisy training signal.
  • Generalization scope: Experiments appear focused on specific math domains; it remains unclear whether the evolved problems cover the breadth of competition mathematics (combinatorics, number theory, geometry) or concentrate on more solver-friendly algebraic/numerical problems.

MoD-DPO通过模态解耦偏好优化与语言先验去偏惩罚，有效缓解全模态大语言模型中的跨模态幻觉问题。
MoD-DPO: Towards Mitigating Cross-modal Hallucinations in Omni LLMs using Modality Decoupled Preference Optimization
Ashutosh Chaubey, Jiacheng Pang, Mohammad Soleymani 评分 7.2
核心贡献
MoD-DPO通过模态解耦偏好优化与语言先验去偏惩罚，有效缓解全模态大语言模型中的跨模态幻觉问题。
MoD-DPO reduces cross-modal hallucinations in omni-modal LLMs by introducing modality-decoupled preference optimization with modality-aware regularization and a language-prior debiasing penalty.
科研问题 / Research Question
全模态大语言模型在联合处理音频、视频与文本时，易因虚假跨模态相关性或强语言先验产生幻觉响应，严重影响其在真实视听理解任务中的可靠性。
Omni-modal LLMs that jointly process audio, video, and text are prone to cross-modal hallucinations — generating responses driven by spurious inter-modal correlations or dominant language priors rather than the actual input signal. This undermines reliability in real-world audiovisual understanding tasks where precise modality grounding is critical.
先前工作为何不够 / Why Prior Work Falls Short
标准DPO等偏好优化方法对所有模态一视同仁，无法区分相关模态；现有幻觉缓解研究主要针对视觉-语言模型，对音视频文本联合的全模态场景研究不足，且语言先验去偏未被纳入偏好学习框架。
Existing preference optimization methods like standard DPO treat all modalities uniformly, failing to disentangle which modality is relevant to a given query. Prior multimodal hallucination mitigation work has focused primarily on vision-language models, leaving omni-modal settings (audio + video + text) largely underexplored. Language-prior debiasing has not been explicitly integrated into preference learning frameworks for omni LLMs.
解决方案 / Solution
MoD-DPO在DPO基础上引入两类模态感知正则项：对无关模态扰动保持不变性，对相关模态扰动保持敏感性；同时加入语言先验去偏惩罚项，抑制仅依赖文本的幻觉输出。该模态解耦设计引导模型将答案锚定于正确的输入模态。
MoD-DPO extends DPO with two modality-aware regularization terms: one enforcing invariance to corruptions in task-irrelevant modalities (e.g., perturbing audio when the query is visual), and one enforcing sensitivity to perturbations in the task-relevant modality. A language-prior debiasing penalty explicitly suppresses text-only hallucination-prone responses during preference optimization. This modality-decoupled design encourages the model to ground its answers in the correct input modality rather than co-variate signals or memorized language patterns.
主要结论 / Key Results
MoD-DPO在多个视听幻觉基准上持续优于标准DPO及基线全模态模型，在视听问答与幻觉检测任务中均有提升，语言先验去偏惩罚项可量化地减少纯文本幻觉输出。
MoD-DPO consistently outperforms standard DPO and baseline omni LLMs across multiple audiovisual hallucination benchmarks, improving both perception accuracy and hallucination resistance. Specific gains are demonstrated across audio-visual question answering and hallucination detection tasks, with the language-prior debiasing penalty contributing measurable reductions in text-only hallucinated responses.
值得关注的讨论点 / Discussion Points
  • 模态扩展性：模态解耦设计理论上可扩展至更多模态（如深度图、IMU），但论文仅在音频-视频-文本三元组上验证，更广泛的泛化能力有待证明。
  • 数据依赖性：构建模态扰动偏好对需要针对每种模态设计扰动策略，合成负样本的质量与多样性可能成为性能提升的实际瓶颈。
  • 评估范围：实验聚焦于幻觉基准，缺乏通用视听QA准确率对比，难以排除幻觉减少以牺牲通用任务性能为代价的可能性。

传统MoE框架在理论上可建模多样地形，但门控网络实际上对不同地形输出近乎均匀的专家激活权重，导致专家专门化失效。单一策略RL方法难以捕捉不同地形的差异化行为，多阶段或层次化方法则引入训练复杂性并在地形切换处易出现不稳定。

  • 对比约束的可扩展性：对比损失在训练时需要地形类别标签，当地形连续变化或类别边界模糊时，CMoE的适用性存疑。
