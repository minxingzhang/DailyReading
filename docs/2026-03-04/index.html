CyberLLMInstruct: A Pseudo-Malicious Dataset Revealing Safety-Performance Trade-offs in Cyber Security LLM Fine-tuning
Adel Elzemity, Budi Arief, Shujun Li 2025-03-12 CCS
核心贡献
CyberLLMInstruct数据集揭示了一个关键权衡：对LLM进行网络安全微调可提升任务准确率，但会严重削弱其对抗攻击的安全性。
CyberLLMInstruct introduces a 54,928-pair pseudo-malicious dataset to expose a critical trade-off: fine-tuning LLMs for cybersecurity tasks boosts task accuracy but drastically degrades safety against adversarial attacks.
科研问题 / Research Question
在网络安全数据上微调LLM，如何同时影响任务性能与对抗攻击的安全韧性？这一问题至关重要，因为在对抗性领域部署LLM会带来双重使用风险。
How does fine-tuning LLMs on cybersecurity-specific data affect both task performance and safety resilience against adversarial inputs? This matters because deploying LLMs in adversarial domains like cybersecurity creates dual-use risks where improved capability may come at the cost of exploitability.
先前工作为何不够 / Why Prior Work Falls Short
现有网络安全LLM数据集缺乏系统性探究安全-性能权衡的伪恶意指令对。已有安全研究多聚焦于通用越狱，而非网络安全领域特定的对抗向量（如提示注入）。
Existing cybersecurity LLM benchmarks and datasets lack pseudo-malicious instruction-response pairs that systematically probe the safety-performance trade-off. Prior work on LLM safety primarily focuses on general-purpose jailbreaks rather than domain-specific adversarial vectors such as prompt injection in cybersecurity contexts. No prior dataset comprehensively combines CTF challenges, CVE databases, academic papers, and industry reports at this scale for fine-tuning evaluation.
解决方案 / Solution
研究者构建了包含54,928对伪恶意指令的CyberLLMInstruct数据集，覆盖恶意软件分析、网络钓鱼模拟和零日漏洞等任务。对7个开源LLM进行微调，并在任务性能（CyberMetric）和多种攻击向量的安全韧性上进行联合评估，定量揭示性能与安全的权衡关系。
The authors construct CyberLLMInstruct, a dataset of 54,928 pseudo-malicious instruction-response pairs covering malware analysis, phishing simulations, and zero-day vulnerabilities, sourced from CTF challenges, academic papers, CVE databases, and industry reports. Seven open-source LLMs are fine-tuned on this dataset and evaluated on both cybersecurity task performance (using CyberMetric) and safety resilience across multiple attack vectors including prompt injection. The key contribution is a rigorous empirical framework that jointly measures capability gains and safety degradation, making the trade-off quantitatively explicit.
主要结论 / Key Results
微调后CyberMetric准确率最高达92.50%，但安全性大幅下降。Llama 3.1 8B在提示注入攻击下的安全评分从0.95骤降至0.15，且该现象在所有7个模型和全部攻击向量中普遍存在。
Fine-tuning on CyberLLMInstruct achieves up to 92.50% accuracy on the CyberMetric benchmark, demonstrating strong task performance gains. However, safety scores collapse dramatically: Llama 3.1 8B's security score against prompt injection drops from 0.95 to 0.15 after fine-tuning. This pattern holds across all seven tested models and all evaluated attack vectors, confirming the trade-off is systematic rather than model-specific.

GAP用互联图结构替代树形越狱框架，实现跨攻击路径知识共享，攻击成功率超96%，同时将查询成本降低62.7%。
Graph of Attacks with Pruning: Optimizing Stealthy Jailbreak Prompt Generation for Enhanced LLM Content Moderation
Daniel Schwartz, Dmitriy Bespalov, Zhe Wang et al. 2025-01-28 EMNLP
核心贡献
GAP用互联图结构替代树形越狱框架，实现跨攻击路径知识共享，攻击成功率超96%，同时将查询成本降低62.7%。
GAP replaces tree-based jailbreak structures with an interconnected graph that shares knowledge across attack paths, achieving >96% attack success rates while cutting query costs by 62.7%.
科研问题 / Research Question
如何更高效地生成隐蔽越狱提示词，以压力测试LLM安全护栏？随着LLM大规模部署，在恶意行为者利用之前发现内容审核系统的弱点，对负责任的AI开发至关重要。
How can we generate stealthy jailbreak prompts more efficiently and effectively to stress-test LLM safety guardrails? As LLMs are deployed at scale, identifying weaknesses in their content moderation systems before malicious actors do is critical for responsible AI development.
先前工作为何不够 / Why Prior Work Falls Short
现有树形越狱方法（如TAP、PAIR）独立探索各攻击路径，无法跨分支共享成功策略，导致查询冗余、成本高昂且错失协同机会。此类方法也不支持多模态输入，且需手动构建种子提示词。
Existing tree-based jailbreak methods (e.g., TAP, PAIR) explore attack paths independently, preventing successful strategies discovered in one branch from informing others. This structural isolation leads to redundant queries, higher costs, and missed opportunities to exploit cross-branch insights. Such methods also lack support for multimodal inputs and require manual seed prompt crafting.
解决方案 / Solution
GAP用有向图取代树结构，使成功攻击节点能同时跨多条路径共享信息。剪枝机制提前淘汰低潜力分支，减少无效查询。GAP-Auto扩展了自动种子提示词生成能力，无需人工初始化。GAP-VLM将框架适配至视觉语言模型，支持多模态越狱评估。
GAP (Graph of Attacks with Pruning) replaces the tree structure with a directed graph, allowing successful attack nodes to share information across multiple paths simultaneously. A pruning mechanism eliminates low-promise branches early, reducing wasted queries. GAP-Auto extends the framework with automated seed prompt generation, removing the need for manual initialization. GAP-VLM adapts the approach to vision-language models, enabling multimodal jailbreak evaluation.
主要结论 / Key Results
GAP在开源和闭源LLM上的攻击成功率均超96%，相比现有最优方法提升20.8%，同时将查询成本降低62.7%。GAP生成的对抗提示词还可作为高质量训练数据，显著提升下游内容审核分类器的性能。
GAP achieves attack success rates exceeding 96% on both open-source and closed LLMs, outperforming state-of-the-art baselines by 20.8% in success rate while reducing query costs by 62.7%. GAP-generated adversarial prompts also serve as high-quality training data, measurably improving downstream content moderation classifiers.
值得关注的讨论点 / Discussion Points
  • 双刃剑风险：GAP是强力攻击工具，尽管作者将其定位为红队测试辅助，但同样可能降低恶意行为者低成本绕过LLM护栏的门槛。
  • 剪枝策略的泛化性：剪枝效果很可能依赖于分支评分启发式设计，论文未充分分析该设计在不同目标模型上的敏感性。
  • 审核改进闭环：用GAP生成的提示词训练内容审核器思路新颖，但论文未量化对抗数据相对于常规数据增强的独立贡献，因果关系有待进一步验证。
  • Dual-use risk: GAP is a powerful offensive tool, and while the authors frame it as a red-teaming aid, the same framework could lower the barrier for malicious actors to systematically bypass LLM safeguards at low cost.
  • Generalization of pruning: The pruning strategy's effectiveness likely depends on the scoring heuristic used to rank attack branches; the paper does not fully analyze sensitivity to this design choice across diverse target models.
  • Moderation improvement loop: Using GAP-generated prompts to train content moderators is promising, but the paper stops short of quantifying how much the adversarial data specifically contributes versus standard augmentation, leaving the causal claim partially unverified.

Adversarial and Multilingual Threats in Retrieval-Augmented Generation: From Prompt Injection to Model Exploitation
Basma ElSaify, Mohamed Baderelden 2025-08-18 ACL
核心贡献
本文针对RAG系统提出全面威胁分类与对抗框架，揭示多语言流水线（尤其是阿拉伯语-英语混码）存在超越纯英语评估的独特安全漏洞。
This paper proposes a comprehensive threat taxonomy and adversarial framework for RAG systems, revealing that multilingual pipelines—especially Arabic-English code-switching—introduce unique vulnerabilities beyond those found in English-only evaluations.
科研问题 / Research Question
RAG系统将LLM与外部检索结合，但这一架构扩大了检索流水线、语言接口和API的攻击面。本文探究传统与智能体RAG系统的全谱对抗威胁，以及多语言输入如何放大这些风险，对高风险应用场景的安全部署具有重要意义。
RAG systems couple LLMs with external retrieval, but this architecture expands the attack surface across retrieval pipelines, language interfaces, and exposed APIs. The paper asks: what are the full spectrum of adversarial threats in both conventional and agentic RAG systems, and how do multilingual inputs amplify these risks? This matters because RAG is increasingly deployed in high-stakes applications where security failures can cause real harm.
先前工作为何不够 / Why Prior Work Falls Short
现有LLM和RAG安全研究主要聚焦于纯英语场景，多语言漏洞几乎未被探索。已有工作多针对孤立攻击类型（如提示注入或越狱），缺乏覆盖检索到生成全流程的统一分类体系。智能体RAG场景（LLM自主执行多步任务）的对抗性分析尤为匮乏。
Existing security research on LLMs and RAG systems predominantly focuses on English-only settings, leaving multilingual vulnerabilities largely unexplored. Prior work also tends to address isolated attack types—such as prompt injection or jailbreaks—rather than providing a unified taxonomy covering the full pipeline from retrieval to generation. Agentic RAG scenarios, where LLMs autonomously execute multi-step tasks, have received especially limited adversarial analysis.
解决方案 / Solution
作者构建了覆盖RAG全流程的威胁分类体系，涵盖提示注入、越狱漏洞、API驱动的模型提取及多语言提示操控。核心贡献是对语言混码（尤其阿拉伯语-英语）作为攻击向量的专项分析，该方式可绕过主要基于单语数据训练的安全过滤器。框架还将威胁建模扩展至智能体RAG系统，自主多步推理会导致漏洞叠加。
The authors construct a comprehensive threat taxonomy that categorizes attacks across the entire RAG pipeline, including prompt injection, jailbreak exploits, API-driven model extraction, and multilingual prompt manipulation. A key contribution is the focused analysis of language blending (code-switching), particularly Arabic-English mixing, as a vector that bypasses safety filters trained predominantly on monolingual data. The framework also extends threat modeling to agentic RAG systems, where autonomous multi-step reasoning creates compounding vulnerabilities. Together, these form an adversarial framework intended to guide both offensive red-teaming and defensive system design.
主要结论 / Key Results
研究表明，多语言混码（尤其阿拉伯语-英语）能持续绕过在纯英语下有效的安全护栏，揭示现有防御体系的系统性缺口。智能体RAG系统因自主检索-执行循环可被注入文档或API操控劫持，表现出更高的漏洞风险。本文以分析和分类为主，研究结论指向具体且被忽视的攻击面。
The paper demonstrates that multilingual code-switching—particularly Arabic-English blending—consistently evades safety guardrails that hold under English-only inputs, exposing a systemic gap in current defenses. Agentic RAG systems are shown to be disproportionately vulnerable due to their autonomous retrieval-and-execution loops, which can be hijacked via injected documents or API manipulation. While the paper is primarily analytical and taxonomic rather than empirical, these findings highlight concrete, underexplored attack surfaces with practical implications for deployed systems.
值得关注的讨论点 / Discussion Points
  • 多语言盲区是关键缺口：安全对齐数据集以英语为主，面向非英语或多语言用户的RAG部署存在未量化风险，组织应优先使用混码对抗输入审计其流水线。
  • 智能体RAG威胁模型有待细化：论文指出自主多步执行风险高，但未提供具体攻击基准或量化失败率，对实践者的可操作指导有限。
  • 缺乏实证支撑削弱可信度：框架全面但以定性分析为主，后续工作应在真实RAG部署上结合系统性红队测试，以验证威胁严重性排序并指导防御资源分配。
  • Multilingual blind spots are a critical gap: safety alignment datasets are overwhelmingly English-centric, meaning any RAG deployment serving non-English or multilingual users inherits unquantified risk—organizations should urgently audit their pipelines with code-switched adversarial inputs.
  • The agentic RAG threat model is underspecified: while the paper flags autonomous multi-step execution as high-risk, it stops short of providing concrete attack benchmarks or quantitative failure rates, limiting actionable takeaways for practitioners building agentic systems.
  • Taxonomy without empirical grounding reduces confidence: the framework is comprehensive but largely qualitative; future work should pair this taxonomy with systematic red-teaming experiments across real RAG deployments to validate threat severity rankings and prioritize defensive investment.

Jailbreaking Embodied LLMs via Action-level Manipulation
Xinyu Huang, Qiang Yang, Leming Shen et al. 2026-03-02
核心贡献
Blindfold是一种自动化越狱框架，通过动作级别操控具身大语言模型，构造语义上看似安全但会导致真实物理危害的指令，从而绕过语言安全过滤器。
Blindfold is an automated jailbreak framework that manipulates embodied LLMs at the action level—crafting semantically safe instructions that produce harmful physical-world outcomes—bypassing both language-level safety filters and LLM causal reasoning.
科研问题 / Research Question
具身大语言模型通过自然语言控制机器人等物理智能体，但其安全机制针对语言威胁而非物理后果设计。语义上无害的指令序列仍可能造成碰撞、财产损毁或人身伤害，揭示了语言对齐与物理安全之间的关键鸿沟。
Embodied LLMs control physical agents (robots, autonomous systems) via natural language, but their safety mechanisms are designed for linguistic threats, not physical consequences. A semantically benign instruction sequence can still cause real-world harm—collision, property damage, or injury—revealing a critical gap between language-level alignment and physical-world safety.
先前工作为何不够 / Why Prior Work Falls Short
现有大语言模型越狱方法（提示注入、对抗后缀、迭代红队测试）针对语言层输出，依赖黑盒试错，效率低且易被现代安全过滤器拦截。这些方法也未对动作序列与物理危害之间的因果链建模，具身领域的对抗性研究几乎空白。
Existing LLM jailbreak methods (prompt injection, adversarial suffixes, iterative red-teaming) target language-layer outputs and rely on black-box trial-and-error, making them inefficient and easily caught by modern safety filters. They also fail to model the causal chain between action sequences and physical-world harm, leaving the embodied domain largely unexplored from an adversarial perspective.
解决方案 / Solution
Blindfold提出"对抗代理规划"策略：不直接攻击黑盒具身LLM，而是操控本地代理LLM生成在指令层语义安全、执行后却产生物理危害的动作计划。通过动作级注入将恶意步骤隐藏于合法任务计划中，使安全检测器无法发现异常。该方法利用具身LLM在多步动作轨迹与物理后果因果推理上的不足，实现自动化、查询高效的攻击，并可跨不同具身LLM后端迁移。
Blindfold introduces an Adversarial Proxy Planning strategy: instead of directly attacking a black-box embodied LLM, it compromises a local surrogate LLM to generate action plans that are semantically safe at the instruction level but physically dangerous when executed in sequence. Key malicious actions are concealed through action-level injection—embedding harmful steps within otherwise legitimate task plans so that safety checkers see no red flags. This approach exploits the limited causal reasoning of embodied LLMs, which struggle to map multi-step action trajectories to downstream physical outcomes. The result is an automated, query-efficient attack that transfers across different embodied LLM backends.
主要结论 / Key Results
与基线越狱方法相比，Blindfold在具身LLM基准测试中实现了显著更高的攻击成功率，证明动作级操控可绕过能成功拦截语言级攻击的安全防护。代理规划设计使框架查询效率高，避免了迭代黑盒方法的高查询开销。该方法在多种具身LLM架构和任务环境中均表现出良好的泛化能力。
Blindfold achieves significantly higher attack success rates compared to baseline jailbreak methods on embodied LLM benchmarks, demonstrating that action-level manipulation bypasses safety guardrails that successfully block language-level attacks. The framework is query-efficient due to its proxy planning design, avoiding the high query costs of iterative black-box approaches. Specific numerical results were not fully available from the provided abstract, but the method is shown to generalize across multiple embodied LLM architectures and task environments.
值得关注的讨论点 / Discussion Points
  • 物理-语义鸿沟构成新攻击面：Blindfold揭示仅针对语言输出的安全对齐对具身AI根本不够，监管者和开发者必须在动作轨迹和物理后果层面评估安全性。
  • 迁移攻击风险：代理规划策略无需直接访问目标模型即可成功，攻击者可用单一代理LLM针对多种已部署具身系统生成攻击，大幅降低了攻击门槛。
  • 防御启示：应对Blindfold类攻击可能需要将因果世界模型集成到LLM规划流程中，使其能预测动作序列的物理后果，而非仅依赖事后语言过滤器。
  • Physical-semantic gap as a new threat surface: Blindfold exposes that safety alignment focused on language outputs is fundamentally insufficient for embodied AI—regulators and developers must evaluate safety at the action-trajectory and physical-outcome levels, not just token generation.
  • Transferability risk: The proxy planning strategy succeeds without direct access to the target model, meaning a single surrogate LLM can be used to craft attacks against many deployed embodied systems, dramatically lowering the bar for adversaries.
  • Defensive implications: Mitigating Blindfold-style attacks likely requires causal world models integrated into the LLM's planning pipeline—capable of forecasting physical consequences of action sequences—rather than post-hoc language filters alone.

MoD-DPO: Towards Mitigating Cross-modal Hallucinations in Omni LLMs using Modality Decoupled Preference Optimization
Ashutosh Chaubey, Jiacheng Pang, Mohammad Soleymani 2026-03-03 CVPR
核心贡献
MoD-DPO通过模态解耦偏好优化与语言先验去偏惩罚，有效减少全模态大语言模型中的跨模态幻觉。
MoD-DPO introduces modality-decoupled preference optimization with modality-aware regularization and language-prior debiasing to reduce cross-modal hallucinations in omni-modal LLMs.
科研问题 / Research Question

Omni-modal LLMs (handling audio, video, and text jointly) suffer from cross-modal hallucinations — generating responses driven by spurious correlations between modalities or dominant language priors rather than the actual input signal. This is a critical reliability problem as omni LLMs are deployed in real-world audiovisual understanding tasks where grounding to the correct modality is essential.

Existing hallucination mitigation methods such as standard Direct Preference Optimization (DPO) treat all modalities uniformly, failing to distinguish which modality is relevant to a given query. Language-prior debiasing has been explored in vision-language models but has not been systematically extended to omni-modal settings with audio, making those approaches insufficient for the richer cross-modal interaction space in omni LLMs.
解决方案 / Solution
MoD-DPO在DPO基础上引入两类模态感知正则项：对无关模态加噪时强制输出不变性，对相关模态扰动时强制输出敏感性；同时加入语言先验去偏惩罚，抑制模型仅凭文本即可生成的幻觉回答，迫使模型真正依赖音视频输入。
MoD-DPO extends DPO by introducing two modality-aware regularization terms: one enforces invariance when irrelevant modalities are corrupted (the model's answer should not change), and another enforces sensitivity when the relevant modality is perturbed (the model's answer should change). Together, these terms explicitly discourage spurious cross-modal dependencies during preference learning. Additionally, a language-prior debiasing penalty penalizes the model for responses that could be generated from text alone, pushing it to genuinely ground answers in the audiovisual input.

MoD-DPO在多个音视频幻觉评测基准上持续优于标准DPO及基线全模态模型，在感知准确率和幻觉抵抗力两个维度均有提升，尤其在减少音视频跨模态混淆方面效果显著。
MoD-DPO consistently outperforms standard DPO and baseline omni LLMs across multiple audiovisual hallucination benchmarks, improving both perception accuracy and hallucination resistance. Specific benchmark gains demonstrate that modality-decoupled regularization is more effective than undifferentiated preference optimization, particularly in reducing audio-visual cross-modal confusion.
值得关注的讨论点 / Discussion Points
  • 模态扩展性存疑：MoD-DPO针对音视频设计，当引入更多模态（如深度、触觉）时，无关模态扰动对数量呈组合增长，正则项的可扩展性尚未验证。
  • 偏好对构建的数据敏感性：框架依赖基于扰动构建的偏好对，音频或视频的扰动方式对正则效果影响较大，缺乏系统性的敏感性分析。
  • 通用能力权衡未明确：实验仅在幻觉专项基准上评估，模态解耦优化是否会对通用音视频理解任务带来性能损耗，仍需进一步验证。
  • Scalability to more modalities: MoD-DPO is designed for audio-visual omni LLMs, but it is unclear how the modality-decoupled regularization scales when adding further modalities (e.g., depth, tactile), since the number of irrelevant-modality corruption pairs grows combinatorially.
  • Data dependency for preference pairs: The framework relies on constructing corruption-based preference pairs, and the quality of those corruptions (e.g., how audio or video is perturbed) likely has a strong impact on the effectiveness of the regularization terms — a sensitivity analysis would strengthen the claims.
  • Generalization beyond hallucination benchmarks: Results are evaluated on hallucination-specific benchmarks; it remains to be shown whether modality-decoupled optimization preserves or improves general audiovisual understanding tasks, or introduces trade-offs in performance on non-hallucination benchmarks.
