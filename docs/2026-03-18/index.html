本文将RESTA防御框架扩展至视觉语言模型，通过方向性嵌入噪声在JailBreakV-28K基准上有效降低越狱攻击成功率。
Directional Embedding Smoothing for Robust Vision Language Models
Ye Wang, Jing Liu, Toshiaki Koike-Akino 2026-03-16 ICLR 2026
核心贡献
本文将RESTA防御框架扩展至视觉语言模型，通过方向性嵌入噪声在JailBreakV-28K基准上有效降低越狱攻击成功率。
This paper extends the RESTA defense framework to vision-language models using directional embedding noise, demonstrating reduced jailbreak attack success rates on the JailBreakV-28K benchmark.
重要性与意义 / Significance
随着VLM在智能体系统中广泛部署，轻量级推理时防御方案具有重要实用价值。本文无需重新训练模型即可部署，方向性噪声的发现为未来嵌入式防御设计提供了具体原则。
As VLMs are increasingly deployed in agentic AI systems, robust inference-time defenses are critical for real-world safety. This work provides a lightweight, plug-in defense layer requiring no model retraining, making it practical for deployment. The directional noise finding offers a concrete design principle for future embedding-based defenses.
科研问题 / Research Question
视觉语言模型易受多模态越狱攻击，攻击者结合对抗图像与文本绕过安全对齐并诱导有害输出。VLM被集成进智能体系统，不安全输出会带来真实风险，因此这一问题至关重要。
Vision-language models are vulnerable to multi-modal jailbreaking attacks that combine adversarial images and text to bypass safety alignment and elicit harmful outputs. This is a critical problem because VLMs are being integrated into agentic systems where unsafe outputs can have real-world consequences. The paper asks whether RESTA, an existing text-domain defense, can be effectively adapted to the multi-modal setting.
先前工作为何不够 / Why Prior Work Falls Short
现有LLM越狱防御多针对纯文本输入，无法有效泛化至多模态场景。RESTA原为文本嵌入设计，未在VLM多样攻击类型下得到验证，也缺乏在JailBreakV-28K大规模基准上的系统评估。
Existing jailbreak defenses for LLMs often rely on text-only smoothing or adversarial training, which do not generalize well to multi-modal inputs. Methods like RESTA were originally designed for text-domain token embeddings and had not been validated against the diverse attack types in VLM settings. No lightweight inference-time defense had been systematically evaluated across the large-scale JailBreakV-28K multi-modal benchmark.
解决方案 / Solution
作者将RESTA适配至VLM，在推理时向token嵌入注入噪声并聚合多次带噪预测以平滑对抗扰动。核心创新在于引入方向性嵌入噪声——噪声向量与原始嵌入方向对齐，而非各向同性随机噪声，从而在保留语义的同时破坏对抗结构。
The authors adapt RESTA to VLMs by injecting noise into token embeddings at inference time and aggregating predictions across multiple noisy samples to smooth out adversarial perturbations. The key contribution is introducing directional embedding noise, where the noise vector is aligned with the original token embedding direction rather than being isotropic random noise. This directional alignment preserves semantic content while disrupting adversarial structure. The method is evaluated on JailBreakV-28K, a benchmark covering 28,000 multi-modal jailbreak prompts across diverse attack strategies.
主要结论 / Key Results
方向性嵌入噪声的RESTA在JailBreakV-28K基准上显著降低了攻击成功率，且在各攻击类别中均优于各向同性随机噪声，证明噪声与嵌入几何对齐是关键因素。该防御无需微调模型即可在推理时部署。
RESTA with directional embedding noise achieves a notable reduction in attack success rate (ASR) on the JailBreakV-28K benchmark compared to undefended VLMs. Directional noise consistently outperforms isotropic random noise across attack categories, suggesting that noise alignment with embedding geometry is a key factor. The defense operates at inference time with no model fine-tuning required, confirming its practicality as a lightweight security layer.
值得关注的讨论点 / Discussion Points
  • 方法新颖性有限：核心贡献是对RESTA的适配而非全新防御机制，方向性噪声是否为主要增益来源需进一步消融验证。
  • 对抗性评估不足：JailBreakV-28K涵盖静态攻击，但未评估了解防御机制的白盒自适应攻击者，这是平滑类防御的标准压力测试。
  • 推理开销未充分量化：多次带噪采样会增加延迟，对时延敏感的智能体系统而言，需更精确地报告计算开销以评估实际可部署性。
  • Incremental novelty: The core method is an adaptation of existing RESTA rather than a fundamentally new defense, raising questions about whether the gains are due to the VLM extension or the directional noise insight specifically.
  • Benchmark coverage vs. adaptive attacks: JailBreakV-28K covers diverse static attacks, but the paper does not evaluate against white-box adaptive adversaries who know the defense, which is the standard stress test for smoothing-based defenses.
  • Inference cost trade-off: Aggregating predictions over multiple noisy samples increases inference latency; the paper should quantify this overhead more precisely to assess deployment feasibility in latency-sensitive agentic systems.

SeqAR: Jailbreak LLMs with Sequential Auto-Generated Characters
Yan Yang, Zeguan Xiao, Xin Lu et al. 2024-07-02 NAACL
核心贡献
SeqAR通过在单次查询中串联多个自动生成的角色来绕过LLM安全护栏，无需人工模板，对GPT-3.5和GPT-4的攻击成功率分别达88%和60%。
SeqAR is an automated jailbreak framework that chains multiple AI-generated characters in a single query to bypass LLM safety guardrails, achieving 88% and 60% attack success rates on GPT-3.5 and GPT-4 without any human-crafted templates.
重要性与意义 / Significance
SeqAR表明，仅凭开源工具即可自动化突破LLM安全对齐，无需专有模型或专家知识，大幅降低红队测试门槛，同时警示当前基于RLHF的对齐机制亟需加固。
SeqAR demonstrates that LLM safety alignment is breakable with fully automated, open-source tooling — no proprietary models or expert prompt engineering required. This lowers the barrier for red-teaming, making it accessible to the broader safety research community. It also signals an urgent need for more robust guardrail mechanisms beyond current RLHF-based alignment.
科研问题 / Research Question
能否在无需专有LLM或人工设计模板的情况下自动生成并优化越狱提示？这对开源安全研究社区降低红队测试成本、提升可及性具有重要意义。
Can jailbreak prompts be generated and optimized automatically without relying on proprietary LLMs or human-designed seed templates? This matters because existing red-teaming methods are costly, hard to scale, and often inaccessible to open-source safety researchers.
先前工作为何不够 / Why Prior Work Falls Short
现有越狱方法依赖昂贵的专有模型或人工设计的种子模板，可扩展性差；GCG等自动方法生成的对抗后缀不自然，易被困惑度检测防御识别。
Prior jailbreak methods either depend on expensive proprietary models (e.g., GPT-4 as the attacker) or require manually curated seed templates crafted by human experts, both of which limit scalability and reproducibility. Automated approaches like GCG generate adversarial suffixes but produce unnatural text easily caught by perplexity-based defenses.
解决方案 / Solution
SeqAR分三阶段运行：①用开源LLM生成多样化越狱角色；②根据攻击成功反馈迭代优化每个角色；③将多个优化角色串联为单次查询以叠加绕过效果。全流程仅依赖开源LLM，无需种子模板或专有API。
SeqAR operates in three stages: (1) character generation, where an open-source LLM proposes diverse jailbreak persona descriptions; (2) character optimization, where each character is iteratively refined based on attack success feedback; and (3) sequential application, where multiple optimized characters are chained into a single query to compound their bypass effect. The entire pipeline runs in a cold-start setting — no seed templates, no proprietary APIs — using only open-source LLMs.
主要结论 / Key Results
SeqAR对GPT-3.5-1106和GPT-4的攻击成功率分别为88%和60%，优于依赖人工模板或专有攻击模型的基线；串联多角色策略始终优于单角色，验证了核心设计的有效性。
SeqAR achieves attack success rates of 88% on GPT-3.5-1106 and 60% on GPT-4, outperforming baselines that rely on human-crafted templates or proprietary attackers. The sequential character chaining strategy consistently outperforms using a single jailbreak character, validating the core design choice.
值得关注的讨论点 / Discussion Points
  • 评估范围有限：仅测试GPT-3.5和GPT-4，未涵盖LLaMA-3、Mistral等开源模型或Claude 3等护栏更强的系统，泛化性存疑。
  • 攻击成功率的判定标准不明确——未说明采用人工评判、分类器还是关键词匹配，影响结果的可复现性与横向比较。
  • 论文以红队安全工具为定位，却未提出或评估针对串联角色攻击的具体防御措施，攻击发现与实际缓解之间存在明显缺口。
  • Evaluation breadth is limited: results are reported only on GPT-3.5-1106 and GPT-4, leaving open how SeqAR performs against open-source targets (e.g., LLaMA-3, Mistral) or models with stronger guardrails like Claude 3.
  • The attack success rate metric is not clearly defined — it is unclear whether it uses human judgment, a classifier, or keyword matching, which affects reproducibility and comparability with prior work.
  • While SeqAR is framed as a red-teaming safety tool, the paper does not propose or evaluate concrete defenses against sequential character attacks, leaving a gap between attack discovery and practical mitigation.

VLFeedback: A Large-Scale AI Feedback Dataset for Large Vision-Language Models Alignment
Lei Li, Zhihui Xie, Mukai Li et al. 2024-10-12 EMNLP
核心贡献
VLFeedback构建了包含82K样本的AI标注多模态偏好数据集，通过DPO训练Silkie模型，无需人工标注即可提升大视觉语言模型的对齐性能。
VLFeedback introduces an 82K-sample AI-annotated multimodal preference dataset and trains Silkie via DPO, improving LVLM alignment in helpfulness, visual faithfulness, and safety without costly human labels.
重要性与意义 / Significance
VLFeedback证明AI反馈可替代昂贵的人工标注，为多模态对齐研究降低门槛。数据集与Silkie模型的公开为社区提供了具体基准，其对幻觉和安全性的关注对实际部署具有重要意义。
VLFeedback demonstrates that scalable AI feedback can replace expensive human annotation for aligning large vision-language models, lowering the barrier for alignment research. The release of this dataset and the Silkie model provides a concrete benchmark and baseline for the community. Its focus on hallucination reduction and safety resilience addresses critical deployment concerns for real-world multimodal AI systems.
科研问题 / Research Question
如何在不依赖昂贵人工标注的前提下，为大型视觉语言模型高效生成高质量对齐数据？随着LVLM快速落地，帮助性、视觉准确性和安全性的同步保障至关重要。
How can we efficiently generate high-quality alignment data for large vision-language models without relying on expensive and slow human annotation? This matters because LVLMs are rapidly being deployed in real-world settings where helpfulness, visual accuracy, and safety are all critical requirements.
先前工作为何不够 / Why Prior Work Falls Short
现有LVLM对齐方法依赖人工标注偏好数据，成本高且难以扩展。面向纯文本的AI反馈方法因缺乏视觉基础而无法直接迁移至多模态场景。大规模自动化多模态反馈生成在此前尚未被系统研究。
Existing LVLM alignment approaches rely heavily on human-annotated preference data, which is costly, slow to scale, and often limited in multimodal diversity. Prior AI feedback methods (e.g., RLHF variants for text-only LLMs) do not directly transfer to vision-language settings due to the added complexity of grounding language in visual inputs. Automated multimodal evaluation and feedback generation at scale had not been systematically explored before this work.
解决方案 / Solution
VLFeedback收集82K多模态指令，用现成模型生成多个候选回复，再由GPT-4V等强模型自动评分并提供详细理由，全程无需人工介入。基于这些AI生成的偏好对，作者使用DPO对Silkie模型进行微调，指令类型覆盖帮助性、视觉忠实性与安全性三个维度。
The authors construct VLFeedback by collecting 82K multi-modal instructions and using off-the-shelf vision-language models to generate multiple candidate responses, which are then scored and ranked by a capable AI annotator (e.g., GPT-4V) with comprehensive rationales—all without human involvement. They then fine-tune an LVLM called Silkie using Direct Preference Optimization (DPO) on these AI-generated preference pairs. The pipeline covers diverse instruction types to ensure broad coverage of helpfulness, visual faithfulness, and safety dimensions.
主要结论 / Key Results
Silkie在感知任务上超越基础模型6.9%，在认知任务上提升9.5%；在MMHal-Bench上幻觉现象显著减少，同时对红队对抗攻击的抵御能力也有所增强，验证了AI反馈的可扩展性。
Silkie outperforms its base model by 6.9% on perception tasks and 9.5% on cognition tasks. It also reduces hallucination as measured on MMHal-Bench and shows improved resilience against red-teaming adversarial prompts. These gains are achieved purely through AI-generated feedback, validating the scalability of the approach.
值得关注的讨论点 / Discussion Points
  • 标注质量瓶颈：整个流程依赖GPT-4V的标注质量，其系统性偏差将直接传导至偏好数据集并影响Silkie的行为。
  • 泛化边界：评估局限于特定基准（如MMHal-Bench），Silkie在开放式真实场景或非英语任务上的泛化能力尚不明确。
  • 安全与能力的权衡：论文未深入分析DPO对齐是否导致复杂推理或创造性任务性能下降，这是对齐研究中的已知矛盾。
  • Annotation quality bottleneck: The entire pipeline depends on the quality of the AI annotator (e.g., GPT-4V). Any systematic biases or errors in GPT-4V's scoring will propagate directly into the preference dataset and ultimately into Silkie's behavior.
  • Generalization scope: Results are reported on specific benchmarks (MMHal-Bench, perception/cognition tasks), but it is unclear how well Silkie generalizes to open-ended real-world multimodal tasks or languages beyond English.
  • Safety vs. capability trade-off: While Silkie shows improved safety resilience, the paper does not deeply analyze whether DPO-based alignment causes any regression in creative or complex reasoning capabilities—a known tension in alignment research.

AdaAnchor提出一种隐空间推理框架，通过迭代精化锚向量并自适应停止，无需手动调整推理步数。
Thinking in Latents: Adaptive Anchor Refinement for Implicit Reasoning in LLMs
Disha Sheshanarayana, Rajat Subhra Pal, Manjira Sinha et al. 2026-03-16 ICLR 2026
核心贡献
AdaAnchor提出一种隐空间推理框架，通过迭代精化锚向量并自适应停止，无需手动调整推理步数。
AdaAnchor introduces a latent reasoning framework for LLMs that iteratively refines anchor vectors in hidden space and halts adaptively, eliminating the need to manually tune the number of reasoning steps.
重要性与意义 / Significance
AdaAnchor解决了LLM部署中的实际瓶颈：冗长的思维链推理增加推理成本。通过将推理移入隐空间并自适应停止，该方法在不牺牲精度的前提下提升推理效率，对推理机制研究者和工程实践者均有参考价值。
AdaAnchor tackles a practical bottleneck in LLM deployment: verbose chain-of-thought traces inflate inference cost and latency. By moving reasoning into latent space with adaptive halting, it offers a path to more efficient inference without sacrificing accuracy. This is relevant to both researchers studying reasoning mechanisms and practitioners optimizing LLM serving costs.
科研问题 / Research Question
LLM如何在不生成冗长中间token序列、且无需固定隐推理步数的情况下完成多步推理？冗长的CoT输出和固定步数超参数均会带来效率与泛化瓶颈。
How can LLMs perform multi-step reasoning without generating lengthy intermediate token sequences, and without requiring a fixed, hand-tuned number of latent computation steps? This matters because both verbose CoT outputs and rigid latent-step hyperparameters create efficiency and generalization bottlenecks across diverse tasks and model sizes.
先前工作为何不够 / Why Prior Work Falls Short
标准CoT方法要求模型逐步输出推理过程，推理成本随之线性增加。已有隐推理方法（如Pause Tokens、Coconut）虽将计算移入隐状态，但依赖固定迭代步数，需针对不同数据集调参，迁移灵活性有限。
Standard CoT prompting forces models to verbalize every reasoning step, increasing output length and inference cost proportionally. Prior latent reasoning methods (e.g., Pause Tokens, Coconut) shift computation to hidden states but rely on a fixed number of refinement iterations, requiring per-dataset hyperparameter tuning. This rigidity limits their practicality when transferring across tasks or model families.
解决方案 / Solution
AdaAnchor在输入端附加一组可学习的隐锚向量，通过Transformer层迭代精化而不产生任何中间token。自适应停止机制监控锚向量跨迭代的稳定性，收敛后即停止，从而完全消除固定步数超参数。最终稳定的锚状态用于条件化答案生成，推理深度随输入自适应调整。
AdaAnchor appends a set of learnable latent anchor vectors to the input, which are iteratively refined through the model's transformer layers without emitting any intermediate tokens. An adaptive halting mechanism tracks the stability of anchor representations across iterations—stopping early when anchors converge—removing the fixed-step hyperparameter entirely. The final stable anchor states are used to condition the model's answer generation. This design decouples reasoning depth from output length and makes the computation budget input-dependent.
主要结论 / Key Results
AdaAnchor在数学文字题基准上与固定步数隐推理基准相比具有竞争力，对简单问题自动分配更少迭代步数，对难题则分配更多。自适应停止相较固定预算方法降低了平均推理步数，节约推理成本，同时在精度上与CoT基准持平或更优。
AdaAnchor demonstrates competitive accuracy on mathematical word problem benchmarks compared to fixed-step latent reasoning baselines, while dynamically allocating fewer iterations to simpler problems and more to harder ones. The adaptive halting reduces average inference steps versus always-on fixed-budget methods, yielding inference cost savings. Exact numbers are not fully visible from the abstract, but the method claims to match or exceed CoT baselines in accuracy with substantially shorter effective output length.
值得关注的讨论点 / Discussion Points
  • 锚向量的数量、初始化方式和附加位置等设计选择可能对性能影响显著，论文在不同模型规模下的指导尚不明确。
  • 基于锚向量稳定性的自适应停止机制直觉合理，但若锚向量出现噪声或振荡，可能导致过早停止或无法收敛，在分布偏移下的鲁棒性尚未得到验证。
  • 评测主要集中在数学推理基准上，AdaAnchor能否泛化至常识推理、代码生成、多跳问答等其他多步推理任务仍不明确，限制了结论的普适性。
  • Anchor vector design choices (number, initialization, and attachment position) are likely sensitive hyperparameters that could substantially affect performance, and the paper's guidance on these choices across different model scales is unclear.
  • The adaptive halting criterion based on anchor stability is intuitive but may be brittle: noisy or oscillating anchors could trigger premature stopping or prevent halting, and robustness under distribution shift has not been demonstrated.
  • Evaluation appears focused on mathematical reasoning benchmarks; it is unknown whether AdaAnchor generalizes to other multi-step reasoning domains (e.g., commonsense, coding, multi-hop QA), limiting conclusions about broad applicability.

Physics-informed fine-tuning of foundation models for partial differential equations
Vlad Medvedev, Leon Armbruster, Christopher Straub et al. 2026-03-16 ICLR
核心贡献
本文提出一种物理信息微调框架，将PDE残差和边界条件嵌入训练目标，使预训练PDE基础模型能在数据稀缺场景下高效适配。
This paper introduces a physics-informed fine-tuning framework that incorporates PDE residuals and boundary conditions into the training objective to adapt pre-trained PDE foundation models under data-scarce conditions.
重要性与意义 / Significance
本文为科学计算领域的PDE基础模型微调提供了系统性方法，有助于在标注数据昂贵的真实场景中降低部署门槛，对科学机器学习社区具有重要参考价值。
As foundation models for PDEs gain traction in scientific computing, establishing principled fine-tuning practices is critical for real-world deployment where labeled simulation data is expensive. This work bridges the gap between physics-informed learning and the foundation model paradigm, offering a practical recipe for adapting general-purpose PDE surrogates to specific physical tasks. It opens a systematic path for the community to leverage pre-trained models without requiring large domain-specific datasets.
科研问题 / Research Question
当任务数据稀缺且存在分布偏移时，PDE基础模型难以适配新物理系统。如何在微调中系统引入物理约束以提升精度和物理一致性，是核心研究问题。
Pre-trained PDE foundation models struggle to adapt to new physical systems when task-specific data is limited and distribution shifts exist. How can physical constraints be systematically integrated into fine-tuning to improve accuracy and physical consistency without large labeled datasets? This is critical because collecting high-fidelity simulation data for every new downstream PDE task is often prohibitively expensive.
先前工作为何不够 / Why Prior Work Falls Short
标准微调方法在NLP中效果显著，但直接用于PDE模型时数据不足会导致物理违规。PINN将PDE残差嵌入损失函数，但主要用于从头训练，尚未系统应用于预训练基础模型的适配。
Standard fine-tuning approaches transfer well in NLP but naively applying them to PDE models can violate physical laws when data is scarce. Physics-informed neural networks (PINNs) embed PDE residuals into training losses, but have primarily been used to train models from scratch rather than to adapt pre-trained foundation models. The combination of physics-informed objectives with large pre-trained PDE surrogates has not been systematically explored before this work.
解决方案 / Solution
框架在标准监督损失基础上增加PDE残差项和边界条件惩罚项，物理约束部分无需额外标注数据。该方法与模型无关，可适用于任意预训练PDE基础模型，并通过超参数平衡数据驱动与物理约束两类损失。
The authors propose a physics-informed fine-tuning framework that augments the standard supervised loss with PDE residual terms and boundary condition penalties evaluated at collocation points, requiring no additional labeled solution data for the physics terms. The framework is model-agnostic and can be applied on top of any pre-trained PDE foundation model. Fine-tuning is performed on a downstream task defined by a specific PDE family, allowing the model to specialize while preserving knowledge from pre-training. The balance between data-driven and physics-based loss components is controlled via weighting hyperparameters.
