GAP用互联图结构替代树形越狱框架，实现跨攻击路径知识共享，攻击成功率超96%，同时降低62.7%查询成本。
Graph of Attacks with Pruning: Optimizing Stealthy Jailbreak Prompt Generation for Enhanced LLM Content Moderation
Daniel Schwartz, Dmitriy Bespalov, Zhe Wang et al. 2025-01-28 EMNLP
核心贡献
GAP用互联图结构替代树形越狱框架，实现跨攻击路径知识共享，攻击成功率超96%，同时降低62.7%查询成本。
GAP replaces tree-based jailbreak structures with an interconnected graph to share knowledge across attack paths, achieving >96% attack success rates while cutting query costs by 62.7%.
重要性与意义 / Significance
GAP大幅提升对抗性提示生成的效率与可扩展性，有助于系统性安全评估。其多模态变体GAP-VLM与自动化种子生成模块GAP-Auto将应用范围扩展至多模态模型。GAP生成的提示可直接增强内容审核系统，将攻防研究有效结合。
GAP advances LLM red-teaming by making adversarial prompt generation significantly more efficient and scalable, lowering the barrier for systematic safety evaluation. Its multimodal variant (GAP-VLM) and automated seed generation (GAP-Auto) extend applicability beyond text-only models. Crucially, GAP-generated prompts are shown to directly improve content moderation systems, bridging offensive and defensive security research.
科研问题 / Research Question
如何使越狱提示生成更高效，以严格测试LLM安全防护？现有方法的攻击路径相互孤立，存在冗余计算且无法复用成功策略。需要更强大的红队工具提前发现漏洞。
How can jailbreak prompt generation be made more effective and query-efficient to rigorously stress-test LLM safety guardrails? Existing methods leave attack paths isolated, wasting redundant computation and missing opportunities to reuse successful strategies. A more powerful red-teaming tool is needed to expose vulnerabilities before malicious actors do.
先前工作为何不够 / Why Prior Work Falls Short
TAP等树形方法沿独立分支生成攻击，无法跨分支共享知识且查询成本高。PAIR等单路径迭代方法易陷入局部最优。两类方法均难以扩展至多模态场景或自动化种子生成。
Tree-based methods like TAP (Tree of Attacks with Pruning) generate attack candidates along independent branches, preventing cross-branch knowledge transfer and inflating query costs. Pair-based methods (e.g., PAIR) use single-path iterative refinement, which is computationally wasteful and prone to getting stuck in local optima. Neither approach scales well to multimodal settings or automated seed creation.
解决方案 / Solution
GAP构建有向攻击图，节点为候选越狱提示，边编码精化关系，使任意分支的成功策略均可影响其他路径。剪枝机制提前移除低分节点，将计算集中于最优攻击路径。GAP-Auto利用LLM自动生成多样化种子提示，免去人工初始化。GAP-VLM将框架扩展至视觉语言模型，将对抗文本嵌入图像中。
GAP constructs a directed graph of attack prompts where nodes represent candidate jailbreaks and edges encode refinement relationships, allowing successful strategies from any branch to inform other paths. A pruning mechanism removes low-scoring nodes early, concentrating compute on the most promising attack trajectories. GAP-Auto extends this by using an LLM to automatically generate diverse seed prompts, removing the need for manual initialization. GAP-VLM adapts the framework for vision-language models by embedding adversarial text within images.
主要结论 / Key Results
GAP在开源与闭源LLM上的攻击成功率均超96%，较最优基线提升20.8%。与树形方法相比，查询成本降低62.7%。GAP生成的对抗提示作为数据增强，可显著提升内容审核分类器性能。
GAP achieves attack success rates exceeding 96% on both open-source and closed LLMs, a 20.8% improvement over state-of-the-art baselines. Query costs are reduced by 62.7% compared to existing tree-based methods, making large-scale red-teaming practical. GAP-generated adversarial prompts also demonstrably improve downstream content moderation classifier performance when used as augmentation data.
值得关注的讨论点 / Discussion Points
  • 双重用途风险：GAP是强力攻击工具，尽管定位为红队辅助，但低成本系统性越狱生产LLM的潜力值得关注，论文的责任披露机制需审视。
  • 评估局限：攻击成功率由LLM裁判而非人工标注判定，可能高估实际违规率；在Claude 3、Gemini Ultra等更多安全微调模型上的结果将增强结论可信度。
  • 审核提升方法细节不足：GAP提示改善内容审核的主张缺乏分类器架构、训练数据量等关键细节，GAP贡献与标准数据增强的区别未被量化。
  • Ethical dual-use tension: GAP is a powerful offensive tool, and while the authors frame it as a red-teaming aid, the same framework could be misused to systematically jailbreak production LLMs at low cost — the paper's responsible disclosure and access controls deserve scrutiny.
  • Evaluation scope: Attack success rate is measured by a judge LLM rather than human raters, which may overestimate true policy violations; results on a broader set of safety-tuned models (e.g., Claude 3, Gemini Ultra) would strengthen generalizability claims.
  • Moderation improvement methodology: The claim that GAP prompts improve content moderation is promising but underspecified — the paper does not detail the classifier architecture, training data size, or how much of the gain is attributable to GAP versus standard data augmentation baselines.

Adversarial and Multilingual Threats in Retrieval-Augmented Generation: From Prompt Injection to Model Exploitation
Basma ElSaify, Mohamed Baderelden 2025-08-18 ACL
核心贡献
本文提出针对RAG系统的对抗性威胁分类框架，证明多语言提示操控（尤其是阿拉伯语-英语混码）可绕过仅针对英语的安全防御。
This paper presents a comprehensive adversarial threat taxonomy for RAG systems, demonstrating that multilingual prompt manipulation—especially Arabic-English code-switching—exposes critical security gaps missed by English-only defenses.
重要性与意义 / Significance
本文首次系统性地覆盖RAG检索与生成两层的安全威胁，并针对多语言攻击提出实用防御参考，对企业级RAG部署具有直接指导价值。
As RAG systems become production infrastructure for enterprise AI, this work is among the first to systematically map threats across both retrieval and generation layers, including agentic RAG pipelines. The multilingual attack angle is particularly novel and practically urgent, as most deployed safety filters are English-centric. Practitioners building RAG applications gain a concrete threat checklist and researchers gain a structured taxonomy to benchmark future defenses.
科研问题 / Research Question
RAG系统将攻击面从生成层扩展至检索管道和语言接口，现有研究未能全面建模这些威胁。本文重点探讨多语言混码输入如何绕过现有安全机制，对高风险部署场景至关重要。
RAG systems introduce a fundamentally expanded attack surface compared to standalone LLMs, as adversaries can compromise the retrieval pipeline, inject malicious content into knowledge bases, or exploit language interfaces. The paper asks: what is the full spectrum of adversarial threats specific to RAG, and how do multilingual inputs—particularly code-switching between Arabic and English—undermine existing defenses? This matters because RAG is now widely deployed in high-stakes domains where security failures carry real consequences.
先前工作为何不够 / Why Prior Work Falls Short
现有研究主要聚焦英语单语提示注入与越狱攻击，对RAG检索层及智能体配置的威胁建模不足，多语言混码攻击几乎未被系统研究。
Prior adversarial work on LLMs focuses primarily on English-language prompt injection and jailbreaks, leaving retrieval-specific attack vectors underexplored. Existing RAG security analyses rarely address agentic configurations where the LLM autonomously executes multi-step tasks, multiplying the potential blast radius of a successful attack. Multilingual vulnerability research is sparse, and code-switching attacks—where adversaries blend languages mid-prompt—have received almost no systematic treatment.
解决方案 / Solution
本文构建涵盖提示注入、越狱利用、API模型提取和多语言操控四类的威胁分类体系，并扩展至智能体RAG场景，针对每类威胁提供具体攻击演示，框架可适用于主流RAG系统。
The authors construct a structured threat taxonomy covering four major attack classes: prompt injection (both direct and indirect via poisoned documents), jailbreak exploits targeting RAG-specific system prompts, API-driven model extraction attacks, and multilingual prompt manipulation including Arabic-English code-switching. They extend the taxonomy to agentic RAG, where an LLM plans and executes multi-step tool calls, showing that each autonomous action step compounds attack surface. Practical attack demonstrations are provided for each category, grounding the taxonomy in concrete, reproducible scenarios. The framework is designed to be system-agnostic and applicable across commercial and open-source RAG deployments.
主要结论 / Key Results
实验表明阿拉伯语-英语混码提示可有效绕过仅拦截英语攻击的安全过滤器；智能体RAG中单条注入指令可级联影响多步工具调用；API模型提取攻击在低查询预算下即可成功。
The paper demonstrates empirically that Arabic-English code-switching prompts successfully bypass safety filters that correctly block equivalent English-only adversarial inputs, confirming a concrete and exploitable vulnerability gap. Agentic RAG systems are shown to be disproportionately vulnerable because a single injected instruction can cascade across multiple autonomous tool-use steps. API-based model extraction attacks are shown to be feasible against RAG-exposed endpoints with relatively low query budgets, raising practical IP and data-leakage concerns.
值得关注的讨论点 / Discussion Points
  • 多语言盲点的泛化性：论文聚焦阿拉伯语-英语混码，但同类漏洞可能存在于更多低资源语言对，需更广泛的跨语言基准测试。
  • 智能体攻击放大效应：论文指出多步工具调用会放大攻击面，但未量化成功率与链长度的关系，缺乏形式化分析。
  • 攻防不对称：论文侧重威胁建模，防御措施较为有限，建议为每类攻击配套经过评估的具体缓解方案（如多语言输入归一化、检索结果沙箱化）以提升实用性。
  • Multilingual blind spot scalability: The paper demonstrates Arabic-English code-switching attacks, but the same logic applies to any low-resource or morphologically complex language pair. Future work should benchmark defenses across a broader typological range of languages to assess how generalizable this vulnerability class truly is.
  • Agentic RAG threat amplification: In agentic settings, the authors note that attack surface grows with each autonomous step, but do not quantify this relationship. A formal model of how attack success probability scales with chain length and tool diversity would significantly strengthen the threat framework.
  • Defense-offense asymmetry: The paper is primarily offensive/taxonomic and offers limited concrete mitigation strategies beyond identifying the threats. Pairing each attack category with evaluated countermeasures—such as input normalization for multilingual inputs or retrieval-result sandboxing—would make this directly actionable for practitioners.

基于激活值引导的MCMC采样框架实现无梯度提示注入攻击，在五个主流LLM上达到49.6%的攻击成功率。
Transferable Direct Prompt Injection via Activation-Guided MCMC Sampling
Minghui Li, Hao Zhang, Yechao Zhang et al. 2025-09-09 EMNLP
核心贡献
基于激活值引导的MCMC采样框架实现无梯度提示注入攻击，在五个主流LLM上达到49.6%的攻击成功率。
A gradient-free, activation-guided MCMC framework for Direct Prompt Injection achieves 49.6% attack success rate with strong cross-model transferability across five mainstream LLMs.
重要性与意义 / Significance
本文揭示了一种无需梯度访问的实用可迁移攻击路径，降低了现实世界对抗攻击的门槛，促使研究界重新审视LLM部署安全防护。激活值与攻击成功率的相关性发现也为攻防研究提供了新视角。
This work exposes a practical, transferable attack vector against LLMs that requires no gradient access, lowering the bar for real-world adversarial exploitation. The 34.6% improvement over human-crafted prompts demonstrates that automated, black-box attacks can be highly effective, urging the community to rethink current LLM deployment safeguards. The interpretability findings linking internal activations to attack success also open new directions for both offense and defense research.
科研问题 / Research Question
直接提示注入攻击通过在用户输入中嵌入恶意指令操控LLM，威胁已部署AI系统的完整性。核心挑战是在无法访问模型权重或梯度的黑盒场景下，构造能跨模型有效迁移的对抗提示。
Direct Prompt Injection (DPI) attacks manipulate LLMs by embedding malicious instructions into user inputs, threatening the integrity of deployed AI systems. The core challenge is crafting adversarial prompts that transfer effectively across different LLMs without requiring access to model weights or gradients. This matters because real-world attackers typically operate in a black-box setting, yet existing black-box methods suffer from poor cross-model transferability.
先前工作为何不够 / Why Prior Work Falls Short
基于梯度的白盒/灰盒方法（如GCG）需访问模型内部，实际部署中不可行；现有黑盒方法依赖启发式搜索，跨模型迁移性差。两类方法均无法同时满足实用性与强迁移性的需求。
White-box and gray-box DPI methods (e.g., GCG-based gradient optimization) require access to model internals, making them impractical in real deployments. Existing black-box methods rely on heuristic or random token search, yielding adversarial prompts with weak transferability across different model architectures and task settings. Neither category simultaneously satisfies the constraints of practicality and strong transferability.
解决方案 / Solution
首先利用白盒代理模型的激活值训练能量模型（EBM）对候选对抗提示打分，再以EBM能量值为引导，通过token级MCMC采样迭代替换token，实现无梯度优化。该框架将攻击与特定目标模型解耦，从而提升跨模型迁移性，并在五个主流LLM的已见与未见任务场景中进行了评估。
The authors first train an Energy-based Model (EBM) using the internal activations of a white-box surrogate model to score the quality of candidate adversarial prompts. Guided by EBM energy scores, they apply token-level Markov Chain Monte Carlo (MCMC) sampling to iteratively propose and accept token substitutions, enabling gradient-free optimization. This decouples the attack from any specific target model, promoting transferability. The framework is evaluated on five mainstream LLMs (e.g., LLaMA, Vicuna, GPT variants) under both seen and unseen task scenarios.
主要结论 / Key Results
所提方法在五个主流LLM上平均攻击成功率为49.6%，比人工构造的对抗提示高出34.6%；在未见任务场景中仍保持36.6%的ASR，展现出良好的泛化性。可解释性分析证实代理模型激活值与目标模型攻击成功率存在可量化的相关性。
The proposed method achieves a 49.6% average attack success rate (ASR) across five mainstream LLMs, outperforming human-crafted adversarial prompts by 34.6%. On unseen task scenarios, it maintains a 36.6% ASR, demonstrating robust generalization beyond the training distribution. Interpretability analysis further reveals a measurable correlation between surrogate model activations and the attack success rate on target models.
值得关注的讨论点 / Discussion Points
  • 代理模型依赖性：EBM基于特定代理模型的激活值训练，若代理模型与目标模型架构差异过大，性能可能下降，代理模型的选择是一个需系统研究的隐含超参数。
  • MCMC计算开销：token级MCMC对长提示的计算成本较高，论文未充分对比其与梯度方法的计算代价，影响实际采用的可行性评估。
  • 防御启示：激活值与攻击成功率的相关性表明基于激活的异常检测是潜在防御方向，但论文未提出或评估具体的对抗防御措施。
  • Surrogate dependency: The EBM is trained on a specific surrogate model's activations, so performance may degrade if the surrogate is architecturally very distant from the target LLM — the choice of surrogate is a hidden hyperparameter that needs systematic study.
  • Scalability of MCMC: Token-level MCMC sampling can be computationally expensive for long prompts; the paper does not fully characterize the compute cost relative to gradient-based baselines, which matters for practical adoption.
  • Defense implications: The correlation between activations and ASR suggests that activation-based anomaly detectors could be a promising defense direction, but the paper stops short of proposing or evaluating concrete countermeasures.

具身智能安全 / Embodied Intelligence Security 2 篇
VisionGuard通过挖掘运动状态的时空不一致性，检测并缓解自动驾驶车辆感知模块中的物理对抗样本攻击。
VisionGuard: Secure and Robust Visual Perception of Autonomous Vehicles in Practice
Xingshuo Han, Haozhao Wang, Kangqiao Zhao et al. 2024-12-02 CCS
核心贡献
VisionGuard通过挖掘运动状态的时空不一致性，检测并缓解自动驾驶车辆感知模块中的物理对抗样本攻击。
VisionGuard detects and mitigates physical adversarial attacks on autonomous vehicle perception by exploiting spatiotemporal inconsistency in motion states across camera and LiDAR inputs.
重要性与意义 / Significance
物理对抗攻击对自动驾驶安全构成致命威胁，VisionGuard提供了一种无需重新设计底层DNN模型即可集成的通用防御方案，对学术界和工业界均具有重要价值。
Physical adversarial attacks on AV perception are a life-critical threat, yet no prior defense handles diverse attack types across real-world scenarios. VisionGuard offers a practical, generalizable defense layer that can be integrated into existing AV pipelines without redesigning underlying DNN models.
科研问题 / Research Question
自动驾驶车辆如何在真实驾驶场景中可靠地检测并消除针对摄像头和LiDAR感知模型的多样化物理对抗样本？该问题直接关系到行人与乘客的生命安全。
How can autonomous vehicles reliably detect and neutralize diverse physical adversarial examples (PAEs) — such as adversarial patches or 3D objects — that fool camera and LiDAR-based perception models in real driving conditions? This matters because PAEs can cause misdetection of obstacles or traffic signs, directly threatening passenger and public safety.
先前工作为何不够 / Why Prior Work Falls Short
现有防御方法（如对抗训练、输入预处理）通常针对特定攻击类型，难以泛化至不同PAE形式或传感器模态，且大多不适用于实时部署场景。
Existing defenses such as adversarial training and input preprocessing are largely attack-specific and fail to generalize across different PAE types (e.g., patches vs. 3D-printed objects) or sensor modalities. Most methods also operate offline or assume white-box knowledge of the attacker, making them impractical for real-time AV deployment.
解决方案 / Solution
VisionGuard构建时空一致性校验框架，通过追踪目标历史运动状态并预测当前状态，将感知异常标记为对抗攻击。系统融合摄像头与LiDAR数据进行交叉验证，并在异常触发时对感知输出进行修正，整个过程与底层DNN模型无关。
VisionGuard introduces a spatiotemporal consistency checking framework: it tracks historical motion states of surrounding objects and predicts expected current states, then flags discrepancies caused by adversarial manipulation as anomalies. The system fuses information from both camera and LiDAR to cross-validate perception outputs, reducing single-modality blind spots. Detected anomalies trigger mitigation strategies that suppress or correct the corrupted perception outputs before they influence driving decisions. This design is attack-agnostic and model-agnostic, requiring no modification to underlying DNN architectures.
主要结论 / Key Results
VisionGuard在摄像头与LiDAR两种模态下，对多种PAE攻击类型均展现出高检测率，同时保持低计算开销，并通过仿真与真实驾驶场景验证了其实际可用性。
VisionGuard demonstrates high detection rates against multiple PAE attack types including adversarial patches and 3D adversarial objects across both camera and LiDAR modalities. The system maintains robust driving performance under attack while incurring minimal computational overhead suitable for real-time AV operation. Experiments span both simulated and physical driving scenarios, validating practical applicability.
值得关注的讨论点 / Discussion Points
  • 时空建模假设的局限：该方法假设攻击者无法操控多帧历史数据；若攻击者逐帧缓慢引入扰动，一致性检测可能被绕过。
  • 传感器融合依赖：交叉验证依赖摄像头与LiDAR同时可用且未受攻击，协同攻击两种模态的场景仍是潜在盲区。
  • 防御层级局限：VisionGuard仅覆盖感知层，对规划层或LLM驱动决策层的攻击无能为力，难以作为全栈安全方案。
  • Scope of spatiotemporal modeling: The approach assumes attackers cannot simultaneously manipulate multi-frame historical data; a sophisticated attacker who gradually introduces perturbations over time could potentially evade the consistency check.
  • Sensor fusion dependency: VisionGuard's cross-validation relies on both camera and LiDAR being available and uncorrupted simultaneously; attacks targeting both modalities in a coordinated fashion remain a potential gap.
  • Perception-layer vs. decision-layer defense: VisionGuard operates at the perception layer, but threats at the planning or LLM-driven decision layer of embodied AV systems are out of scope, limiting its completeness as a full-stack security solution.

提出基于梯度校准与去相关的物理对抗伪装框架，提升跨距离、多视角下对目标检测器的攻击效果。
Physical Adversarial Camouflage through Gradient Calibration and Regularization
Jiawei Liang, Siyuan Liang, Jianjie Huang et al. 2025-08-07 IJCAI
核心贡献
提出基于梯度校准与去相关的物理对抗伪装框架，提升跨距离、多视角下对目标检测器的攻击效果。
A new physical adversarial camouflage framework uses gradient calibration and decorrelation to improve texture-based attacks on object detectors across variable real-world conditions.
重要性与意义 / Significance
该研究直接威胁自动驾驶等安全关键系统的感知层，系统性地解决了物理世界对抗攻击中的核心挑战，为后续防御研究提供了重要基准。
This work directly threatens the perception layer of safety-critical systems like autonomous vehicles, where fooling object detectors with physical textures could have life-threatening consequences. The gradient calibration and decorrelation techniques offer a more systematic treatment of real-world physical constraints than prior art, setting a new benchmark for physical adversarial attacks. Security researchers and autonomous driving engineers need to be aware of these advances to build more robust defenses.
科研问题 / Research Question
如何在真实环境中跨距离、多视角地可靠欺骗目标检测器？这对自动驾驶安全至关重要，因为物理纹理攻击无需数字入侵即可造成危险的漏检。
How can physical adversarial camouflage reliably deceive deep object detectors across varying distances and viewing angles in real-world environments? This matters because autonomous driving systems rely heavily on visual object detectors, and a successful physical attack on texture appearance could cause dangerous misdetections without any digital intrusion.
先前工作为何不够 / Why Prior Work Falls Short
现有方法（如DAS、CAMOU、FCA）依赖可微渲染优化纹理，但存在远距离采样点稀疏导致梯度不一致，以及多视角梯度冲突导致优化不稳定的问题。
Existing physical adversarial camouflage methods (e.g., DAS, CAMOU, FCA) optimize textures via differentiable rendering but suffer from inconsistent gradient signal quality across distances, as sampling point density drops sharply for far-away objects. Multi-angle texture updates also produce conflicting gradients, destabilizing optimization and weakening attack transferability to new viewpoints.
解决方案 / Solution
提出梯度校准策略，将稀疏采样点的梯度传播至未采样邻域，保证跨距离一致性；并设计梯度去相关方法，对多视角梯度去冲突加权，形成统一的物理对抗伪装优化框架。
The paper proposes two complementary modules. WoCoCo将含序列接触的人形机器人任务分解为接触阶段，以统一强化学习框架实现鲁棒的仿真到现实迁移，且仅需极少任务特定调参。
WoCoCo decomposes sequential-contact humanoid tasks into contact stages, enabling a unified RL framework that achieves robust sim-to-real transfer with minimal task-specific tuning.
重要性与意义 / Significance
WoCoCo为人形机器人全身序列接触控制提供了通用可扩展方案，无需昂贵的模型规划或大量任务特定工程设计，降低了复杂场景部署门槛，为接触密集型技能学习提供了可复用的框架模板。
WoCoCo offers a general and scalable solution to one of humanoid robotics' hardest open problems—coordinating whole-body motion through contact sequences—without expensive model-based planning or heavy per-task engineering. Its task-agnostic reward and sim-to-real design could lower the barrier for deploying humanoids in complex manipulation and locomotion scenarios. This work advances the embodied intelligence frontier and provides a reusable template for future contact-rich humanoid skill learning.
科研问题 / Research Question
如何让人形机器人在不依赖缓慢的模型规划或大量逐任务调参的情况下，学习需要全身协调序列接触的技能（如攀爬、穿越狭窄空间）？这对在非结构化真实环境中运作的人形机器人至关重要。
How can a humanoid robot learn to perform tasks requiring coordinated sequences of whole-body contacts—such as climbing, crawling through gaps, or object manipulation—without relying on slow model-based planners or exhaustive per-task RL tuning? This problem matters because contact-rich activities are essential for humanoids operating in unstructured real-world environments, yet remain extremely challenging for both classical and learning-based controllers.
先前工作为何不够 / Why Prior Work Falls Short
模型规划计算耗时且依赖简化动力学模型；现有无模型RL需大量任务特定奖励设计和状态机，且在稀疏接触转换的长程探索中表现欠佳，两类方法均难以跨任务泛化。
Model-based motion planning for contact sequences is computationally expensive and depends on simplified dynamics models that poorly reflect real-world contact physics. Existing model-free RL approaches require extensive task-specific reward shaping, hand-crafted state machines, and struggle with long-horizon exploration when contact transitions are sparse. Neither paradigm generalizes well across diverse contact-rich tasks without significant re-engineering.
解决方案 / Solution
WoCoCo将序列接触任务分解为以接触事件为锚点的有序子阶段，提供任务无关的策略学习流程，每个任务只需指定一到两个任务相关奖励项。阶段结构作为自然课程引导跨接触转换的探索，并通过统一的域随机化实现仿真到现实迁移。
WoCoCo introduces a contact-stage decomposition that breaks any sequential-contact task into ordered sub-phases, each anchored by a specific contact event. This decomposition enables a task-agnostic policy learning pipeline where only one or two task-specific reward terms need to be specified per task, while all other reward components and sim-to-real designs are shared. The framework leverages whole-body RL with the stage structure acting as a natural curriculum, guiding exploration across contact transitions. Sim-to-real transfer is achieved through domain randomization and robust reward designs applied uniformly across stages.

WoCoCo在真实人形机器人上成功完成箱体攀爬、低间隙爬行、两阶段推物等多项挑战性全身接触任务，每任务仅需一至两个奖励项，相比非分解RL基线显著提升了探索效率和任务成功率。
WoCoCo successfully learns and transfers multiple challenging whole-body contact tasks to a real humanoid robot, including box climbing, crawling through low gaps, and two-stage object pushing, demonstrating robust real-world performance. The framework requires specifying only one or two task-related reward terms per task, significantly reducing engineering effort compared to prior RL baselines. Experiments show improved exploration efficiency and success rates over non-decomposed RL approaches on long-horizon contact-sequence tasks.

  • The sim-to-real gap for contact-rich tasks remains a core challenge; WoCoCo addresses it with domain randomization, but contact dynamics (e.g., friction, deformation) are notoriously hard to model, and performance on surfaces not covered by randomization ranges is unclear.
  • WoCoCo is evaluated on a fixed humanoid platform; it is unknown how well the framework generalizes to humanoids with different morphologies, actuator characteristics, or degrees of freedom, which is critical for broad adoption across the robotics community.
