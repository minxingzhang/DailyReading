JailNewsBench: Multi-Lingual and Regional Benchmark for Fake News Generation under Jailbreak Attacks
Masahiro Kaneko, Ayana Niwa, Timothy Baldwin 2026-03-01 ICLR
核心贡献
JailNewsBench是首个跨34个地区、22种语言评估LLM抵御越狱诱导假新闻生成能力的基准测试。
JailNewsBench is the first benchmark to systematically evaluate LLM robustness against jailbreak-induced fake news generation across 34 regions and 22 languages.
重要性与意义 / Significance
该基准填补了LLM安全评估的关键空白，为研究社区提供了跨语言、跨地区的严格评估框架。其发现有助于推动更强健的越狱防御机制，对负责任的AI部署具有重要意义。
This benchmark fills a critical gap in LLM safety evaluation by introducing a multi-lingual, multi-regional perspective that existing English-centric benchmarks completely overlook. With fake news posing direct threats to public health, politics, and social stability, quantifying LLM vulnerability at scale is essential for responsible AI deployment. JailNewsBench provides the research community with a rigorous, reproducible framework to compare model safeguards and drive improvements in jailbreak defenses.
科研问题 / Research Question
本文研究LLM在越狱攻击下生成假新闻的脆弱性是否因语言和地区而异。假新闻具有地区特异性，任何语言的安全漏洞都可能被利用造成现实危害。
The paper asks: how vulnerable are LLMs to generating fake news when subjected to jailbreak attacks, and does this vulnerability vary across languages and regions? This matters because fake news is region-specific in its political and cultural framing, and a safety gap in any language or region can be exploited to cause real-world harm.
先前工作为何不够 / Why Prior Work Falls Short
现有假新闻与越狱基准以英语为主，无法捕捉地区特有的政治、社会和文化背景。此前没有基准同时跨多语言和地区系统评估越狱攻击韧性，导致非英语场景下的LLM安全性存在重大盲区。
Existing fake news and jailbreak benchmarks are predominantly English-centric and fail to capture region-specific political, social, and cultural contexts. No prior benchmark systematically evaluates jailbreak attack resilience for fake news generation across multiple languages and regions simultaneously. This leaves a blind spot in LLM safety research where non-English models and contexts remain largely unassessed.
解决方案 / Solution
JailNewsBench涵盖34个地区和22种语言，结合5种越狱攻击策略构建约30万个测试实例。采用LLM-as-a-Judge框架，通过8个子指标（如事实准确性、可信度、地区特异性）对9个LLM进行系统评估，支持跨模型与跨语言的横向比较。
JailNewsBench constructs a benchmark spanning 34 regions and 22 languages, combining real news topics with 5 distinct jailbreak attack strategies to prompt LLMs into generating fake news. Evaluation employs an LLM-as-a-Judge framework covering 8 sub-metrics (e.g., factual accuracy, plausibility, regional specificity) across approximately 300,000 instances. Nine LLMs are evaluated, enabling systematic cross-model and cross-lingual comparison. The benchmark is designed to be extensible for future languages, regions, and attack types.
主要结论 / Key Results
9个LLM的最高攻击成功率（ASR）达86%，表明现有防护机制远不充分。ASR在不同语言和地区间差异显著，说明安全对齐不均衡，部分语言或地区存在更大漏洞，即使先进LLM也高度易受攻击。
The maximum attack success rate (ASR) across the 9 evaluated LLMs reached 86%, indicating that current safeguards are far from sufficient against jailbreak-induced fake news generation. ASR varied notably across languages and regions, suggesting that safety alignment is uneven and that certain linguistic or regional contexts expose greater vulnerabilities. These findings highlight that even state-of-the-art LLMs with safety mechanisms remain highly exploitable for harmful content generation.
值得关注的讨论点 / Discussion Points
  • ASR的语言与地区差异表明安全微调以英语为中心，低资源语言和非西方地区成为可被针对性利用的薄弱环节。
  • LLM-as-a-Judge方法存在潜在循环性问题：若裁判模型本身对越狱敏感或对特定语言存在偏差，跨地区评分的一致性可能受影响。
  • 86%的最高ASR引发紧迫政策问题：在对抗条件下假新闻生成率如此之高的LLM，是否应在公开发布前强制进行安全审计或使用限制。
  • Language and regional disparity in ASR suggests that safety fine-tuning is predominantly English-centric, leaving lower-resource languages and non-Western regions as exploitable weak points that attackers could deliberately target.
  • The use of LLM-as-a-Judge for evaluation introduces a potential circularity concern: if the judge model itself is vulnerable to jailbreaks or biased toward certain languages, the quality of evaluation scores may be inconsistent across regions.
  • With ASR reaching 86%, the benchmark raises urgent policy questions about deployment thresholds — specifically, whether LLMs with such high fake news generation rates under adversarial conditions should be restricted or require mandatory safety audits before public release.

CyberLLMInstruct: A Pseudo-Malicious Dataset Revealing Safety-Performance Trade-offs in Cyber Security LLM Fine-tuning
Adel Elzemity, Budi Arief, Shujun Li 2025-03-12 CCS
核心贡献
CyberLLMInstruct构建了含54,928条伪恶意指令对的数据集，揭示了网络安全LLM微调中安全性与性能之间的严峻权衡。
CyberLLMInstruct introduces a 54,928-pair pseudo-malicious dataset that exposes a severe safety-performance trade-off when fine-tuning LLMs for cyber security tasks.
重要性与意义 / Significance
本文首次大规模量化了网络安全微调如何系统性地削弱LLM的安全防护，为研究社区提供了可复现的基准，促使从业者正视领域专业化与安全对齐之间的直接矛盾。
This work is the first large-scale empirical study to quantify how cyber security fine-tuning systematically degrades LLM safety guardrails across multiple models and attack vectors. The released dataset provides a reproducible benchmark for the research community to develop safer fine-tuning strategies. Practitioners deploying LLMs in security operations must now confront evidence that domain specialization and safety alignment are in direct tension.
科研问题 / Research Question
对LLM进行网络安全微调是否会破坏其内置安全机制？随着安全领域LLM的广泛应用，理解对抗性微调对安全对齐的影响至关重要。
Can LLMs be safely fine-tuned for cyber security tasks without compromising their built-in safety mechanisms? This matters because security-focused LLMs are increasingly deployed for malware analysis, vulnerability research, and threat intelligence, yet their alignment robustness under adversarial fine-tuning is poorly understood.
先前工作为何不够 / Why Prior Work Falls Short
现有网络安全数据集范围窄、缺乏对抗性指令对，无法有效检验安全对齐。此前研究未系统评估领域微调后多模型的安全退化情况，尤其在对抗性的网络安全场景中。
Existing cyber security datasets are either too narrow in scope (covering single tasks like malware classification) or lack adversarial instruction pairs needed to stress-test safety alignment. Prior safety evaluations of LLMs rarely examined domain-specific fine-tuning scenarios, especially in inherently adversarial fields like cyber security. No prior work systematically measured safety degradation across multiple open-source LLMs using standardized attack vectors post fine-tuning.
解决方案 / Solution
作者构建了CyberLLMInstruct，涵盖恶意软件分析、网络钓鱼、零日漏洞等任务。对7个开源LLM进行微调，并通过CyberMetric和多种攻击向量双重评估，系统量化领域能力与安全性之间的权衡。
The authors construct CyberLLMInstruct, a dataset of 54,928 pseudo-malicious instruction-response pairs covering malware analysis, phishing simulations, zero-day vulnerabilities, and CTF challenges, sourced from academic papers, CVE databases, and industry reports. Seven open-source LLMs (including Llama 3.1 8B, Mistral, and Phi variants) are fine-tuned on this dataset and evaluated on both task performance (CyberMetric benchmark) and safety resilience (prompt injection, jailbreaking, and other attack vectors). The dual evaluation framework isolates the trade-off between domain capability gain and safety degradation, providing a controlled and reproducible methodology for future alignment research.
主要结论 / Key Results
微调后CyberMetric准确率最高达92.50%，但安全性急剧下降：Llama 3.1 8B对提示注入攻击的安全得分从0.95跌至0.15，且该规律在所有7个模型和攻击向量中普遍存在。
Fine-tuning on CyberLLMInstruct improves cyber security task accuracy by up to 92.50% on CyberMetric, confirming strong domain adaptation. However, safety scores collapse dramatically: Llama 3.1 8B's resilience against prompt injection drops from 0.95 to 0.15 after fine-tuning. This pattern holds consistently across all seven tested models and all evaluated attack vectors, indicating a systemic rather than model-specific vulnerability.

Fun-tuning: Characterizing the Vulnerability of Proprietary LLMs to Optimization-Based Prompt Injection Attacks via the Fine-Tuning Interface
Andrey Labunets, Nishit V. Pandya, Ashish Hooda et al. 2025-01-16 IEEE Symposium on Security and Privacy
核心贡献
Fun-tuning利用闭源LLM微调API返回的类损失信号，对Gemini系列模型实施基于优化的提示注入攻击，成功率达65%至82%。
Fun-tuning exploits the loss-like signals returned by proprietary LLM fine-tuning APIs (e.g., Gemini) to compute optimization-based adversarial prompt injections against closed-weight models, achieving 65–82% attack success rates.
重要性与意义 / Significance
本文揭示了LLM微调服务中效用与安全性的根本矛盾，迫使厂商重新审视微调API所暴露的信息，并为商业部署中的提示注入风险评估树立了新标杆。
This work exposes a fundamental utility-security tradeoff in LLM fine-tuning services: the very signals needed for useful fine-tuning also enable gradient-free adversarial attacks on black-box models. It forces LLM vendors to reconsider what information their fine-tuning APIs expose and sets a new benchmark for evaluating prompt injection risks in commercial deployments.
科研问题 / Research Question
攻击者能否仅凭商业微调API返回的信号，在无法访问模型权重的情况下，对闭源LLM计算优化对抗性提示？这一问题至关重要，因为此前普遍认为闭源模型对此类攻击免疫。
Can an attacker compute optimized adversarial prompts against closed-weight LLMs without direct model access, using only the signals returned by commercial fine-tuning APIs? This matters because proprietary LLMs were widely assumed to be protected from optimization-based attacks due to their inaccessible weights and gradients.
先前工作为何不够 / Why Prior Work Falls Short
现有基于优化的提示注入攻击（如GCG）需要白盒访问模型权重与梯度，仅适用于开源模型。针对闭源LLM的黑盒攻击依赖迁移或启发式方法，成功率较低且不稳定。此前无研究系统分析微调API是否泄露足够信号以支持离散优化攻击。
Existing optimization-based prompt injection attacks (e.g., GCG) require white-box access to model weights and gradients, limiting their applicability to open-source models. Black-box attacks on proprietary LLMs have relied on transfer-based or heuristic methods, which yield lower and less reliable success rates. No prior work had systematically characterized whether fine-tuning APIs leak sufficient signal to enable discrete optimization attacks.
解决方案 / Solution
Fun-tuning将微调API视为预言机：将候选对抗性提示作为微调数据提交，读取返回的类训练损失值作为标量信号，驱动贪心离散token搜索。作者分析了Gemini微调API的损失信号特性，证明其与模型行为有效相关，无需访问模型权重或梯度，通过迭代替换token最小化损失值来精炼攻击提示。
Fun-tuning treats the fine-tuning API as an oracle: by submitting candidate adversarial prompts as fine-tuning data and reading back the returned training-loss-like values, attackers obtain a scalar signal to guide discrete token-level search. The authors characterize the Gemini fine-tuning API's loss signals and show they correlate sufficiently with model behavior to drive a greedy search algorithm. Attack prompts are iteratively refined by substituting tokens that minimize the loss-like value, requiring no model weights or gradients. Evaluation is conducted on the PurpleLlama prompt injection benchmark across the Gemini model family.
主要结论 / Key Results
在PurpleLlama提示注入基准测试中，Fun-tuning对Google Gemini系列LLM的攻击成功率为65%至82%，仅凭微调API返回的类损失标量即可驱动贪心搜索收敛，显著优于典型的黑盒迁移攻击基线。
On the PurpleLlama prompt injection benchmark, Fun-tuning achieves attack success rates of 65%–82% across Google's Gemini family of LLMs. The greedy search algorithm converges using only the loss-like scalar returned by the fine-tuning API, confirming that this signal is sufficient for practical discrete optimization. These results significantly outperform typical black-box transfer attack baselines on closed-weight models.
值得关注的讨论点 / Discussion Points
  • API调用频率限制与查询成本制约了攻击规模；防御方可在不影响正常使用的前提下，对微调损失信号加噪或限流。
  • 攻击依赖Gemini API，其可推广性存疑：其他厂商（如OpenAI、Anthropic）的微调API是否暴露类似可利用信号，尚待系统性研究。
  • 差分隐私训练或粗化损失返回值等防御措施可能破坏优化信号，但其对模型效用的影响需严格实证评估。
  • API rate limits and query costs constrain attack scalability; future defenses could throttle or add noise to fine-tuning loss signals without significantly degrading legitimate use.
  • The attack's dependence on the Gemini API raises the question of generalizability: whether other vendors' fine-tuning APIs (e.g., OpenAI, Anthropic) expose analogous exploitable signals remains to be systematically studied.
  • Mitigations such as differential privacy during fine-tuning or returning coarsened loss values could break the optimization signal, but their impact on model utility needs careful empirical evaluation.

Towards Real-Time Defense against Object-Based LiDAR Attacks in Autonomous Driving
Yan Zhang, Zihao Liu, Yi Zhu et al. 2025-11-19 CCS
核心贡献
本文提出首个针对自动驾驶激光雷达感知系统中物体对抗攻击的实时防御机制。
This paper proposes the first real-time defense mechanism against physical, object-based adversarial attacks on LiDAR perception systems in autonomous vehicles.
重要性与意义 / Significance
激光雷达感知是自动驾驶的安全核心，缺乏实时防御机制是严重的部署风险。本文直接弥合了理论攻击认知与实际可部署防御之间的差距，对自动驾驶安全研究者和工业界均具重要意义。
LiDAR-based perception is safety-critical in autonomous driving, and the lack of real-time defenses against physical adversarial attacks represents a serious deployment risk. This work directly bridges the gap between theoretical attack awareness and practical, deployable defense, making it highly relevant for both AV security researchers and industry practitioners.
科研问题 / Research Question
本文研究如何实时防御自动驾驶激光雷达感知系统中的物体级对抗攻击。此类攻击通过在环境中放置对抗性物体来干扰3D点云并欺骗检测模型，无需专用硬件，隐蔽性强、危害大。
The paper tackles how to defend autonomous vehicle LiDAR perception systems against object-based adversarial attacks in real time. Such attacks involve physically placing adversarial objects in the environment to corrupt 3D point clouds and fool detection DNNs, and they are practical and stealthy without requiring specialized hardware.
先前工作为何不够 / Why Prior Work Falls Short
现有激光雷达对抗防御方法计算开销大，无法满足实时部署需求；同时对不同攻击类型和检测模型的泛化能力有限，在真实场景中的防御效果也不理想。
Existing defense methods against LiDAR adversarial attacks suffer from high computational overhead, making them unsuitable for real-time deployment in vehicles. They also exhibit limited generalizability across different attack types and detection models, and their overall effectiveness in realistic scenarios remains constrained.
解决方案 / Solution
本文提出专为物体级激光雷达攻击设计的实时防御框架，针对对抗性物体对3D点云的篡改进行推理时异常检测。方法通过轻量化检测流程和通用防御策略，无需了解具体攻击配置或模型内部信息，即可在低延迟下完成防御。
The paper proposes a real-time defense framework specifically designed for object-based LiDAR attacks, targeting the manipulation of 3D point clouds by adversarial physical objects. The approach focuses on detecting anomalous point cloud patterns introduced by adversarial objects at inference time without incurring prohibitive latency. Key contributions include a lightweight detection pipeline and a generalized defense strategy that does not depend on knowledge of specific attack configurations or model internals.
主要结论 / Key Results
所提方法实现了现有防御所缺失的实时性能，同时在物体级激光雷达攻击下保持较强检测效果。该框架在多种攻击场景下展现出优于现有方法的泛化能力。具体数值指标（如延迟毫秒数、检测率）见完整论文。
The proposed method achieves real-time performance, a key milestone absent in prior defenses, while maintaining strong detection effectiveness against object-based LiDAR attacks. The framework demonstrates improved generalizability compared to existing approaches across multiple attack scenarios. Specific numerical benchmarks (e.g., latency in milliseconds, detection rate percentages) are not fully disclosed in the available abstract but are reported in the full paper.
值得关注的讨论点 / Discussion Points
  • 实时性与精度权衡：低延迟可能需要简化模型架构，与非实时防御方法相比牺牲了多少检测精度尚不明确，高速驾驶场景下的性能稳定性有待验证。
  • 泛化范围局限：该防御专针对物体级攻击，但激光雷达还面临欺骗、中继等更广泛威胁，方法对非物体级攻击的迁移能力尚未验证。
  • 实验完整性存疑：摘要未详述所评估的攻击种类、车辆平台和数据集多样性，实验结论能否支撑强安全性主张仍有待进一步考察。
  • Real-time constraint trade-offs: Achieving low latency likely requires architectural simplifications; it remains unclear how much detection accuracy is sacrificed relative to heavier, non-real-time defenses, and whether the system degrades gracefully under high-speed driving conditions.
  • Generalization scope: The defense targets object-based attacks specifically, but LiDAR systems face a broader threat landscape including spoofing and relay attacks; the method's transferability to non-object-based attack vectors is not yet established.
  • Experimental completeness concerns: The abstract does not detail the diversity of attack methods, AV platforms, or datasets evaluated, raising questions about whether the results reflect sufficiently varied real-world conditions to support strong safety claims.

Probing Vulnerabilities of Vision-Lidar Based Autonomous Driving Systems
Siwei Yang, Zeyu Wang, Diego Ortiz Barbosa et al. 2025-06-11 CVPR
核心贡献
本文系统评估了主流鸟瞰图（BEV）自动驾驶感知模型在摄像头与激光雷达模态下的对抗攻击脆弱性，并测试了黑盒场景下的攻击迁移性。
This paper systematically evaluates adversarial attack vulnerabilities in state-of-the-art Bird's Eye View (BEV) autonomous driving perception models across camera and LiDAR modalities, including black-box transferability.
重要性与意义 / Significance
BEV感知已成为自动驾驶主流范式，其安全性直接关乎生命安全。本文是首批同时针对多模型、多模态开展对抗脆弱性分析的工作之一，为社区提供了安全基准，有助于从业者优先部署针对性防御措施。
BEV perception is rapidly becoming the dominant paradigm for autonomous driving (used in Tesla, Waymo, etc.), making its security analysis directly safety-critical. This work is one of the first to probe adversarial vulnerabilities across multiple BEV models and modalities simultaneously, providing a security baseline for the community.
