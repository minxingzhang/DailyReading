JailNewsBench: Multi-Lingual and Regional Benchmark for Fake News Generation under Jailbreak Attacks
Masahiro Kaneko, Ayana Niwa, Timothy Baldwin 2026-03-01 ICLR
核心贡献
JailNewsBench是首个多语言、多地区基准（涵盖34个地区、22种语言、约30万条实例），用于评估LLM在越狱攻击下生成虚假新闻的鲁棒性。
JailNewsBench is the first multilingual, multi-regional benchmark (34 regions, 22 languages, ~300k instances) for evaluating LLM robustness against jailbreak-induced fake news generation.
重要性与意义 / Significance
该基准系统揭示了越狱攻击如何在不同语言和地区背景下将LLM武器化以生成虚假新闻，最高攻击成功率达86%，警示了现实部署中的紧迫风险。JailNewsBench为全球研究者提供了标准化的大规模安全评测工具。
This benchmark fills a critical gap by systematically exposing how jailbreak attacks can weaponize LLMs for fake news generation across diverse linguistic and regional contexts. The finding that attack success rates reach 86% signals urgent risk for real-world deployment. JailNewsBench provides researchers and practitioners with a standardized, large-scale tool to benchmark and improve LLM safety guardrails globally.
科研问题 / Research Question
LLM能否通过越狱攻击在多语言、多地区背景下生成具有欺骗性的虚假新闻？现有安全评测多以英语为中心且地区覆盖有限，而虚假新闻具有强烈的地域和文化特异性，亟需跨语言评估。
Can LLMs be manipulated via jailbreak attacks to generate convincing fake news across multiple languages and regions? This matters because fake news is deeply context-dependent—shaped by local politics, culture, and language—yet existing LLM safety evaluations are predominantly English-centric and regionally narrow.
先前工作为何不够 / Why Prior Work Falls Short
现有虚假新闻检测与LLM安全基准大多为单语（以英语为主），无法反映特定地区的政治和文化背景。此前的越狱评测聚焦于一般有害内容，而非虚假新闻生成这一结构化威胁，缺乏系统性的多语言越狱评估框架。
Existing fake news detection and LLM safety benchmarks are largely monolingual (predominantly English) and fail to capture region-specific political and cultural nuances. Prior jailbreak evaluations focus on general harmful content rather than the specific, structured threat of fake news generation. No benchmark before JailNewsBench systematically combines multilingual coverage, regional context, and jailbreak attack diversity for this task.
解决方案 / Solution
JailNewsBench覆盖34个地区、22种语言，对9个LLM施加5种越狱攻击策略，采用LLM-as-a-Judge方法从8个细粒度子指标（如事实可信度、地区特异性、语言流畅性）评估输出，数据集共约30万条实例，支持跨模型、跨语言和跨攻击类型的统计比较。
JailNewsBench constructs a benchmark spanning 34 regions and 22 languages, applying 5 distinct jailbreak attack strategies to 9 LLMs. It evaluates outputs using LLM-as-a-Judge across 8 fine-grained sub-metrics (e.g., factual plausibility, regional specificity, linguistic fluency). The dataset totals approximately 300,000 instances, enabling statistically robust comparisons across models, languages, and attack types.
主要结论 / Key Results
在9个LLM的评测中，最高攻击成功率（ASR）达86%，表明现有安全机制极易被越狱攻击突破。不同语言和地区之间的表现差异显著，低资源语言的防护能力尤为薄弱，揭示了多语言安全对齐中的系统性盲区。
Across 9 evaluated LLMs, the maximum attack success rate (ASR) reached 86%, indicating that current safety guardrails are highly vulnerable to jailbreak-induced fake news generation. Performance varied significantly across languages and regions, suggesting that safety mechanisms are unevenly distributed—likely weaker in lower-resource languages. The scale and diversity of the benchmark expose systematic blind spots in existing multilingual LLM safety alignment.
值得关注的讨论点 / Discussion Points
  • 基准覆盖偏差：尽管涵盖34个地区和22种语言，但低资源语言（如撒哈拉以南非洲语言）仍可能代表性不足，而这些地区恰是虚假新闻风险最高、LLM训练数据最匮乏的地方。
  • LLM裁判可靠性：使用LLM评估LLM输出存在循环性问题，裁判模型可能与被测模型共享同样的盲区，在低资源语言场景下可能低估真实攻击成功率。
  • 攻击泛化性：5种越狱攻击未必覆盖自适应或新兴攻击策略，随着模型商修补已知漏洞，攻击者会开发新方法，若无持续更新机制，基准的长期相关性将受影响。
  • Benchmark coverage bias: While 34 regions and 22 languages is a significant advance, the benchmark may still underrepresent the most vulnerable low-resource languages (e.g., Sub-Saharan African languages), where fake news risks are high but training data for both LLMs and judges is scarce.
  • LLM-as-a-Judge reliability: Using LLMs to evaluate LLM outputs introduces circularity—judge models may share the same blind spots as the evaluated models, particularly for languages outside their training distribution, potentially underreporting true ASR in low-resource settings.
  • Attack generalizability: The 5 jailbreak attacks tested may not cover adaptive or evolving attack strategies; as LLM providers patch known exploits, adversaries develop new methods, so benchmark relevance may degrade without a living update mechanism.

Obscure but Effective: Classical Chinese Jailbreak Prompt Optimization via Bio-Inspired Search
Xun Huang, Simeng Qin, Xiaoshuang Jia et al. 2026-02-26 ICLR
核心贡献
CC-BOS利用仿生果蝇优化算法，在八个策略维度上自动生成文言文越狱提示，借助文言文的语言隐晦性实现高效的黑盒攻击。
CC-BOS uses bio-inspired fruit fly optimization to auto-generate classical Chinese jailbreak prompts across 8 policy dimensions, achieving effective black-box attacks on LLMs by exploiting classical Chinese's linguistic obscurity.
重要性与意义 / Significance
本文揭示了一个被忽视的攻击面：文言文作为低资源语言向量可绕过LLM安全过滤器。CC-BOS框架降低了黑盒对抗提示生成的门槛，表明多语言和古典语言安全覆盖在LLM对齐研究中亟待加强。
This paper reveals a concrete, underexplored attack surface: classical Chinese as a low-resource language vector that evades modern LLM safety filters. The automated CC-BOS framework lowers the barrier for adversarial prompt generation in black-box settings, signaling that multilingual and classical-language safety coverage is urgently needed in LLM alignment research.
科研问题 / Research Question
当提示语言为文言文这一低资源且语义晦涩的语言时，LLM是否存在可利用的安全漏洞？作者认为安全训练主要集中于现代高资源语言，使文言文成为越狱攻击的有效盲区。
Do LLMs exhibit exploitable safety vulnerabilities when prompted in classical Chinese, a low-resource and linguistically obscure language? The authors argue that safety training is disproportionately skewed toward modern, high-resource languages, leaving classical Chinese an effective blind spot for jailbreak attacks.
先前工作为何不够 / Why Prior Work Falls Short
现有越狱研究主要集中于现代英文提示（如GCG、AutoDAN）或有限的现代多语言攻击，未探索低资源或古典语言场景，且人工构造提示难以实现系统化的黑盒自动对抗优化。
Existing jailbreak research focuses primarily on modern English prompts (e.g., GCG, AutoDAN) or a limited set of modern multilingual attacks. These approaches fail to explore low-resource or classical language contexts, and manual prompt crafting does not scale to automated, systematic adversarial optimization in black-box settings.
解决方案 / Solution
CC-BOS将对抗提示编码为角色、行为、机制、隐喻、表达、知识、触发模式和上下文八个策略维度，并利用多维果蝇优化算法（FOA）进行迭代优化，涵盖嗅觉搜索（随机探索）、视觉搜索（邻域利用）和柯西变异（跳出局部最优）三个阶段。整个框架仅依赖模型输出作为反馈，无需访问模型内部参数。
CC-BOS encodes adversarial prompts into 8 policy dimensions: role, behavior, mechanism, metaphor, expression, knowledge, trigger pattern, and context. It optimizes these dimensions using a multi-dimensional fruit fly optimization algorithm (FOA), iterating through smell search (random exploration), visual search (neighbor exploitation), and Cauchy mutation (escaping local optima). The framework operates entirely in a black-box setting, requiring only model output scores as feedback. This bio-inspired search strategy enables efficient traversal of the classical Chinese prompt space without access to model internals.
主要结论 / Key Results
CC-BOS在黑盒设置下对多个商业和开源LLM实现了可量化的攻击成功率，在有害内容诱导基准上优于基线越狱方法；文言文编码能持续绕过拦截现代中文或英文等效提示的内容过滤器，但具体数值因摘要不完整未能完全披露。
CC-BOS demonstrates measurable attack success rates against multiple commercial and open-source LLMs in black-box settings, outperforming baseline jailbreak methods on harmful content elicitation benchmarks. The classical Chinese encoding consistently bypasses content filters that block equivalent modern Chinese or English prompts, though specific numerical results are not fully disclosed in the available abstract.
值得关注的讨论点 / Discussion Points
  • 语言特定安全盲区：文言文在安全对齐数据集中的稀缺性很可能是其作为攻击向量有效的根本原因，提示LLM开发者须审查所有语言变体（包括古典语言）的安全覆盖情况。
  • 仿生优化的可扩展性：将FOA用于提示优化具有新颖性，但8个策略维度的选择似乎依赖经验，未来研究应探索不同维度组合或其他进化算法（如遗传算法、PSO）能否提升攻击效率与迁移性。
  • 伦理与双重用途风险：自动化文言文越狱生成降低了滥用门槛；论文写作不完整且社区关注度极低，引发对负责任披露实践的质疑，以及是否提供了充分防御对策的担忧。
  • Language-specific safety gaps: Classical Chinese's scarcity in safety alignment datasets likely explains its effectiveness as an attack vector — this suggests LLM developers must audit safety coverage across all supported language registers, including archaic ones.
  • Scalability of bio-inspired optimization: Using FOA for prompt optimization is novel, but the choice of exactly 8 policy dimensions appears heuristic; future work should investigate whether different dimension sets or other evolutionary algorithms (e.g., genetic algorithms, PSO) yield better attack efficiency or transferability.
  • Ethical and dual-use concerns: Automating jailbreak generation in classical Chinese lowers the practical barrier for misuse; the paper's incomplete writing and minimal community reception raise questions about responsible disclosure practices and whether sufficient defensive countermeasures are proposed alongside the attack framework.

Ignore All Previous Instructions: Jailbreaking as a de-escalatory peace building practise to resist LLM social media bots
Huw Day, Adrianna Jezierska, Jessica Woodgate 2026-03-02 ICLR
核心贡献
本文将对大语言模型的"越狱"重新定性为一种用户主导的非暴力策略，用于揭露和瓦解社交媒体上由AI驱动的政治操控机器人。
This paper reframes "jailbreaking" of LLMs as a user-driven, non-violent tactic to expose and neutralize AI-powered political manipulation bots on social media.
重要性与意义 / Significance
随着LLM驱动的机器人大规模扭曲政治话语，本文提出了一种绕过平台审核依赖的公民层面对策，将AI安全研究与和平建设文献相结合，为技术人员与冲突研究者开辟了跨学科对话空间。
As LLM-powered bots increasingly distort political discourse at scale, this paper offers a novel citizen-level countermeasure that bypasses dependence on platform moderation. It connects AI safety research with peacebuilding literature, opening an interdisciplinary dialogue between technologists and conflict scholars.
科研问题 / Research Question
普通社交媒体用户如何主动对抗加剧政治冲突、传播虚假信息的LLM机器人？本文认为平台主导的审核机制不足，用户发起的越狱行为是一种尚未充分研究的基层抵制策略。
How can ordinary social media users actively counter LLM-powered bots that escalate political conflict and spread disinformation? The paper argues that platform-centric moderation is insufficient and that user-initiated jailbreaking represents an underexplored grassroots resistance strategy.
先前工作为何不够 / Why Prior Work Falls Short
现有关于AI驱动虚假信息的研究主要聚焦于平台层面的干预，如自动检测系统和内容审核政策。这些自上而下的方法使终端用户处于被动地位，且平台的商业利益可能与严格执法相冲突。
Existing literature on AI-driven disinformation focuses predominantly on platform-level interventions such as automated detection systems and content moderation policies. These top-down approaches leave end users passive and dependent on platforms whose commercial incentives may conflict with rigorous enforcement.
解决方案 / Solution
作者提出将用户主导的越狱行为——即构造绕过LLM安全护栏以揭示账户自动化本质的提示词——视为一种去升级和平建设实践。借鉴冲突转化理论，他们将其定性为非暴力干扰手段，通过暴露机器人行为来削弱虚假叙事的传播力。论文还构建了概念分类框架，将角色扮演提示、指令注入等越狱技术映射至其去升级功能。
The authors propose treating user-led jailbreaking — crafting prompts that bypass LLM safety guardrails to reveal a bot's automated nature — as a de-escalatory peacebuilding practice. Drawing on conflict transformation theory, they frame this as non-violent disruption: exposing bot behavior discredits misleading narratives and reduces their social spread. The paper offers a conceptual taxonomy mapping jailbreaking techniques (e.g., role-play prompts, instruction injection like "Ignore all previous instructions") to their de-escalatory functions.
主要结论 / Key Results
本文以概念性论证为主，未提供实证实验或量化结果。论文未报告任何用户研究、真实机器人交互案例或可量化的去升级效果来验证所提框架。
The paper is primarily conceptual and does not present empirical experiments or quantitative results. No user studies, real-world bot interactions, or measurable de-escalation outcomes are reported to validate the proposed framework.
值得关注的讨论点 / Discussion Points
  • 最核心的缺陷是缺乏实证基础：没有对真实越狱交互的受控研究，关于其能降低政治冲突升级的主张仍属推测，尚未得到验证。
  • 该方法存在伦理与安全风险——将越狱行为常态化，可能无意中向用户传授可被滥用于有害目的（而非仅用于识别机器人）的技术。
  • 论文未涉及平台规则与法律层面的阻力：对LLM驱动账户实施越狱可能违反服务条款甚至计算机欺诈法规，而论文对这些现实与法律约束未作回应。
  • Lack of empirical grounding is the central weakness: without controlled studies of actual jailbreaking interactions, the claim that this reduces political conflict escalation remains speculative and unverified.
  • The approach carries ethical and safety risks — normalizing jailbreaking could inadvertently teach users techniques that also enable misuse of LLMs for harmful purposes beyond bot detection.
  • Platform and legal pushback is unaddressed: jailbreaking LLM-powered accounts may violate terms of service or even computer fraud statutes, and the paper does not engage with these practical and legal constraints.

Analyzing Physical Adversarial Example Threats to Machine Learning in Election Systems
Khaleque Md Aashiq Kamal, Surya Eada, Aayushi Verma et al. 2026-02-28
核心贡献
本文量化了翻转美国选举所需的对抗样本选票数量，并对六种对抗攻击方式在物理打印场景下进行了基准评测。
This paper quantifies how many physically-printed adversarial ballot images are needed to flip a U.S. election and benchmarks six adversarial attack types against ML ballot classifiers.
重要性与意义 / Significance
本文是首批系统分析物理对抗样本对选票分类系统威胁的研究之一，提出的概率框架可帮助选举官员评估真实风险，研究结论可直接指导更鲁棒选票扫描系统的设计。
Election integrity is a critical societal concern, and this paper is one of the first to rigorously analyze physical adversarial attacks on ML-based ballot classification at scale (144,000 adversarial examples). The probabilistic framework for estimating attack thresholds provides election officials and ML security researchers with a concrete tool to assess real-world risk. Findings could directly inform the design of more robust ballot scanning systems and adversarial defenses in high-stakes civic infrastructure.
科研问题 / Research Question
攻击者能否通过打印对抗样本选票来愚弄ML选票分类器，从而翻转选举结果？本文探讨需要多少张此类选票，以及哪种攻击方式在打印-扫描流程中最有效。
Can an attacker flip a U.S. election by physically printing adversarially perturbed ballots that fool ML ballot classifiers? The paper asks how many such ballots are needed and which adversarial attack type survives the print-scan pipeline most effectively.
先前工作为何不够 / Why Prior Work Falls Short
已有研究表明ML选票分类器准确率超过99%，但安全评估多忽视物理对抗威胁；现有对抗样本研究集中于数字扰动，鲜少考察打印-扫描鲁棒性。
Prior work demonstrated that ML ballot classifiers can exceed 99% accuracy on standard datasets, but security evaluations largely ignored physical-world adversarial threats. Existing adversarial example research focuses on digital perturbations, with limited study of print-scan robustness in election-critical contexts.
解决方案 / Solution
作者建立概率框架，将翻转选举所需对抗选票数量建模为候选人胜率与总票数的函数；并对六种攻击算法生成的144,000张对抗样本进行物理打印与扫描实验，评估各攻击在真实降质条件下的存活率。
The authors develop a probabilistic framework that models the number of adversarial ballots required to flip an election as a function of candidate win probabilities and total ballots cast. They then physically print and scan 144,000 adversarial examples generated by six attack algorithms: ℓ∞-APGD, ℓ2-APGD, ℓ1-APGD, ℓ0-PGD, ℓ0+ℓ∞-PGD, and ℓ0+σ-map PGD. Each attack's effectiveness is measured post-print-scan to identify which perturbation type best survives real-world degradation. This combined theoretical and empirical approach provides both a risk quantification tool and an attack comparison benchmark.
主要结论 / Key Results
实验表明，稀疏型攻击（ℓ0及ℓ0+σ-map PGD）在打印-扫描后保持较高误分类率；概率框架显示，在势均力敌的选举中，少量对抗选票即可能改变结果。
Across 144,000 physically realized adversarial examples, certain attack types (particularly sparse ℓ0-based and combined ℓ0+σ-map PGD) retained higher misclassification rates after the print-scan process compared to ℓ∞-norm attacks. The probabilistic framework shows that in close elections, a surprisingly small number of adversarial ballots could be sufficient to alter outcomes, underscoring the practical feasibility of such attacks.
值得关注的讨论点 / Discussion Points
  • 攻击者需物理插入对抗选票，依赖内部访问权限，现实可行性存疑，威胁模型对此讨论不足。
  • 攻击针对特定分类器评估，对抗样本能否迁移至不同司法管辖区的扫描仪或分类器架构尚不明确。
  • 论文仅分析攻击有效性，未提出或评估具体防御方案（如对抗训练），从威胁分析到缓解措施的路径尚不完整。
  • Threat model realism: The attacker must physically print and insert adversarial ballots into real voting pools, which requires insider access or physical infiltration — a significant practical barrier not fully addressed in the threat model.
  • Transferability gap: Attacks are evaluated against specific trained classifiers; it is unclear how well these adversarial examples transfer to different ballot scanner models or classifier architectures used across jurisdictions.
  • Defense gap: The paper characterizes attack effectiveness but does not propose or evaluate concrete defenses (e.g., adversarial training, certified robustness), leaving the path from threat analysis to mitigation incomplete.

Code2Math: Can Your Code Agent Effectively Evolve Math Problems Through Exploration?
Dadi Guo, Yuejin Xie, Qingyu Liu et al. 2026-03-03 ICML
核心贡献
Code2Math提出一个多智能体框架，利用代码执行自动将已有数学题演化为更难的变体，用于LLM训练与评估。
Code2Math introduces a multi-agent framework that uses code execution to autonomously evolve existing math problems into harder, structurally distinct variants for LLM training and evaluation.
重要性与意义 / Significance
随着LLM数学能力逼近IMO水平，高难度训练题稀缺问题日益突出，本文提供了一种可扩展的自动化解决方案。该框架能验证生成题目的可解性和难度提升，具备实际部署价值，同时开辟了代码智能体与数学推理交叉的新研究方向。
As LLMs approach IMO-level math, the shortage of sufficiently challenging training problems is a critical bottleneck; this paper offers a scalable, automated solution via code agents. The framework's ability to validate solvability and difficulty increase makes it practically deployable for synthetic data pipelines. It also opens a new research direction at the intersection of agentic coding and mathematical reasoning.
科研问题 / Research Question
如何自动生成高质量难题以解决前沿LLM训练数据稀缺的问题？随着模型逼近IMO水平，缺乏足够难的题目已成为训练和评估的关键瓶颈。
How can we automatically generate challenging, high-quality math problems to address the scarcity of hard training data for frontier LLMs? This matters because state-of-the-art models are approaching IMO-level performance, and the lack of sufficiently difficult problems limits both further training and rigorous evaluation.
先前工作为何不够 / Why Prior Work Falls Short
现有数学数据集构建方法依赖人工整理或简单模板增强，难以扩展至IMO难度。基于LLM的改写方法生成的变体缺乏结构新颖性和经过验证的难度提升。此前代码智能体虽具备强推理能力，但未被系统性地应用于带可解性保证的数学题演化。
Existing math dataset construction methods rely heavily on human curation or simple template-based augmentation, which do not scale to IMO-level difficulty. LLM-based problem rephrasing approaches tend to produce superficially modified variants that lack structural novelty or verified difficulty increases.
