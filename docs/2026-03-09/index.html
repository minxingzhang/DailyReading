Ignore All Previous Instructions: Jailbreaking as a de-escalatory peace building practise to resist LLM social media bots
Huw Day, Adrianna Jezierska, Jessica Woodgate 2026-03-02 ICLR
核心贡献
本文将用户主动对疑似LLM社交媒体机器人实施"越狱"重新定义为一种去中心化的非暴力降级手段，用以对抗AI驱动的政治操控。
This paper reframes user-initiated jailbreaking of suspected LLM social media bots as a grassroots, non-violent de-escalation tactic against AI-driven political manipulation.
重要性与意义 / Significance
本文提出了一个新颖视角：普通用户而非平台，可成为对抗LLM虚假信息的主动力量。若得到验证，该框架可为社区层面的反影响力策略提供依据，但其实践与伦理影响尚待深入探讨。
The paper opens a novel conceptual lens by positioning ordinary users — not platforms — as active resistors of LLM-powered disinformation. If validated, this framework could inform community-level counter-influence strategies alongside top-down moderation. However, its practical and ethical implications remain underexplored.
科研问题 / Research Question
LLM正被大规模用于操控社交媒体上的政治话语，加剧冲突与虚假叙事传播。现有对策依赖平台主导的内容审核，往往滞后且不透明。本文探讨普通用户能否通过越狱技术独立干预LLM机器人。
LLMs are being deployed at scale to manipulate political discourse on social media, accelerating conflict escalation and the spread of misleading narratives. Existing countermeasures rely heavily on platform-led moderation, which is slow, opaque, and often reactive rather than preventive. The paper asks whether ordinary users can independently disrupt LLM bots through jailbreaking techniques.
先前工作为何不够 / Why Prior Work Falls Short
现有研究主要聚焦于平台层面的干预，如自动检测、账号封禁和内容审核政策，用户几乎没有主动应对空间。将普通用户视为具备技术能力的反虚假信息参与者的研究极为匮乏。
Existing literature on combating LLM-driven social media manipulation focuses predominantly on platform-level interventions such as automated detection, account suspension, and content moderation policies. These approaches are centralized and leave users with little agency in resisting coordinated inauthentic behavior. There is minimal prior work treating end-users as active, technically capable participants in disrupting AI-generated disinformation.
解决方案 / Solution
本文提出以用户为中心的框架，将越狱（通过对抗性提示覆盖LLM系统指令）重新定位为和平建构实践。用户通过构造如"忽略所有先前指令"等提示来揭露机器人行为，打断操控性内容传播。作者借助冲突降级理论为该行为赋予合法性，贡献主要在概念层面。
The paper proposes a user-centric framework in which jailbreaking — prompting LLM-powered accounts with instructions designed to override their system prompts — is recast as a de-escalatory peace-building practice. Users who suspect an account is bot-operated craft adversarial prompts (e.g., "Ignore all previous instructions") to expose automated behavior and interrupt the spread of manipulative content. The authors draw on peacebuilding and conflict de-escalation theory to legitimize this user behavior as a form of non-violent resistance. The contribution is primarily conceptual, situating jailbreaking within a socio-political rather than purely technical frame.
主要结论 / Key Results
本文未提供实验数据或量化结果，结论停留在概念层面：越狱被认为可揭露机器人身份、干扰叙事传播并赋权用户。论文未提供成功率、可量化案例或与平台审核的对比评估。
The paper does not present empirical experiments or quantitative results. Its findings are conceptual: jailbreaking is argued to serve three functions — exposing bot identity, disrupting narrative circulation, and empowering users as non-violent actors. No success rates, case studies with measurable outcomes, or comparative evaluations against platform moderation are provided.

Beyond Input Guardrails: Reconstructing Cross-Agent Semantic Flows for Execution-Aware Attack Detection
Yangyang Wei, Yijie Xu, Zhenyuan Li et al. 2026-03-04
核心贡献
本文提出一种面向多智能体LLM系统的执行感知防御框架，通过重构跨智能体语义流检测间接提示注入及复合攻击，节点级F1达85.3%，路径级达66.7%。
This paper proposes an execution-aware defense framework for multi-agent LLM systems that reconstructs cross-agent semantic flows to detect indirect prompt injection and compound attacks, achieving F1-scores of 85.3% (node-level) and 66.7% (path-level).
重要性与意义 / Significance
随着多智能体LLM系统成为复杂任务编排的主流范式，其安全防护至关重要。本文将防御范式从静态输入过滤转向动态行为分析，为检测绕过传统防护的复合攻击提供了具体机制，对实际部署多智能体系统的从业者具有重要参考价值。
As multi-agent LLM systems become the standard for complex task automation, securing their inter-agent communication channels is critical yet underexplored. This framework shifts the security paradigm from static input filtering to dynamic behavioral analysis, offering a more robust defense against sophisticated attacks that exploit agent autonomy. Practitioners deploying multi-agent pipelines gain a concrete mechanism to detect compound attack vectors that bypass conventional guardrails.
科研问题 / Research Question
多智能体LLM系统依赖自主执行和非结构化的智能体间通信，产生了传统输入过滤无法应对的间接提示注入等严重安全风险。攻击可分散在多个智能体和执行步骤中，使单点过滤失效。本文核心问题是：如何通过分析多智能体系统的完整行为轨迹来检测此类攻击？
Multi-agent LLM systems rely on autonomous execution and unstructured inter-agent communication, creating severe security risks—particularly indirect prompt injection—that conventional input-level guardrails cannot address. The core challenge is that attacks can be distributed across agents and execution steps, making them invisible to any single-point filter. This paper asks: how can we detect such attacks by analyzing the full behavioral trajectory of a multi-agent system?
先前工作为何不够 / Why Prior Work Falls Short
现有防御主要依赖静态输入过滤，针对单个智能体入口进行检查，无法应对通过工具输出或智能体间消息间接注入的恶意指令。此前工作未考虑跨越完整执行图的多跳复合攻击，也缺乏将跨智能体碎片化操作原语整合为统一可审计行为视图的机制。
Existing defenses primarily rely on static input guardrails—filters applied at the entry point of individual agents—which fail when malicious instructions are injected indirectly through tool outputs or inter-agent messages. Prior work does not account for the compound, multi-hop nature of attacks that span the full execution graph of a multi-agent pipeline. There is also no established mechanism to correlate fragmented operational primitives across agents into a unified, auditable behavioral view.
解决方案 / Solution
该框架提取并重构"跨智能体语义流"，将碎片化的操作原语（单个智能体动作与消息）合成为连续行为轨迹。随后，监督LLM从数据流违规、控制流偏差和意图不一致三个维度对轨迹进行审查。这种整体性的执行感知分析能够检测在单个节点上不可见的攻击，覆盖超过10种复合攻击向量。
The proposed framework (referred to as SysName) extracts and reconstructs "Cross-Agent Semantic Flows" by synthesizing fragmented operational primitives—individual agent actions and messages—into contiguous behavioral trajectories. A Supervisor LLM then scrutinizes these trajectories across three dimensions: data flow violations, control flow deviations, and intent inconsistencies. This holistic, execution-aware analysis enables detection of attacks that are invisible at any single node. The framework covers over ten distinct compound attack vectors spanning both node-level and path-level attack patterns.
主要结论 / Key Results
框架在节点级端到端攻击检测中F1分数达85.3%，路径级达66.7%，成功识别超过10种复合攻击向量。路径级F1较低（66.7%）表明，跨多个智能体重构完整攻击链比检测单节点异常更具挑战性。
The framework achieves an F1-score of 85.3% at the node level and 66.7% at the path level for end-to-end attack detection. It successfully identifies over ten distinct compound attack vectors across both detection granularities. The lower path-level F1 (66.7%) suggests that reconstructing full attack chains across multiple agents remains a harder problem than detecting anomalies at individual nodes.
值得关注的讨论点 / Discussion Points
  • 节点级（85.3%）与路径级（66.7%）F1之间18.6个百分点的差距揭示了核心难点：跨完整执行路径关联异常远比标记单个可疑动作困难，提示监督LLM需要更强的时序与因果推理能力。
  • 以LLM作为监督者本身存在潜在风险——监督LLM可能被专门设计用于混淆轨迹分析的对抗性输入所攻击，形成元级别的攻击面，而论文似乎尚未对此进行讨论。
  • 评估涵盖十种复合攻击向量，但测试场景的多样性与真实性尚不明确；现实多智能体部署中的攻击面可能更广且更嘈杂，框架在基准之外的泛化能力有待验证。
  • The 18.6-point gap between node-level (85.3%) and path-level (66.7%) F1-scores reveals a core difficulty: correlating anomalies across a full execution path is significantly harder than flagging individual suspicious actions, pointing to a need for stronger temporal and causal reasoning in the Supervisor LLM.
  • Using an LLM as the supervisor introduces potential failure modes—the Supervisor LLM itself could be targeted by adversarial inputs designed to confuse its trajectory analysis, creating a meta-level attack surface that the paper does not appear to address.
  • The evaluation covers ten compound attack vectors, but the diversity and realism of the test scenarios are unclear from the abstract; real-world multi-agent deployments may exhibit a much wider and noisier attack surface, raising questions about generalization beyond the evaluated benchmark.

SaFeR-ToolKit: Structured Reasoning via Virtual Tool Calling for Multimodal Safety
Zixuan Xu, Tiancheng He, Huahui Yi et al. 2026-03-03
核心贡献
SaFeR-ToolKit将多模态安全决策形式化为结构化工具调用协议，通过三阶段课程训练显著降低视觉语言模型的越狱率和过度拒绝率。
SaFeR-ToolKit formalizes multimodal safety as a structured tool-calling protocol with a three-stage curriculum (SFT→DPO→GRPO), reducing both jailbreaks and over-refusal in vision-language models.
重要性与意义 / Significance
本文通过可审计的工具调用链同时解决越狱和过度拒绝两大难题，填补了多模态安全对齐数据集的空白，为可解释安全流水线提供了可复现的范式。
This paper tackles a dual failure mode in VLM safety—susceptibility to jailbreaks and excessive refusal—by making the reasoning process itself checkable and auditable, not just the final output. The publicly released dataset of 31,654 tool-based safety examples fills a notable gap in multimodal alignment resources. The structured tool-trace approach offers a reproducible blueprint for interpretable safety pipelines that practitioners can adapt.
科研问题 / Research Question
视觉语言模型既易受图像嵌入恶意意图的越狱攻击，又存在对无害请求过度拒绝的问题。现有方法仅对最终回复进行监督，缺乏对中间视觉感知与意图推理步骤的约束，难以同时应对两类失效。
Vision-language models (VLMs) are vulnerable to multimodal jailbreaks—where harmful intent is embedded in images—and simultaneously prone to over-refusal of benign requests, degrading utility. This dual failure stems from aligning only on final responses without supervising intermediate visual perception and intent-reasoning steps. A robust solution must handle both threats jointly while remaining interpretable.
先前工作为何不够 / Why Prior Work Falls Short
主流对齐方法（RLHF、DPO）仅监督最终输出，中间推理步骤无法验证；现有多模态安全数据集缺乏可审计的决策分解；工具增强推理此前未系统应用于VLM安全决策。
Most alignment pipelines (RLHF, DPO) supervise only the final answer token, leaving intermediate reasoning uncontrolled and vulnerable to adversarial visual inputs. Existing multimodal safety datasets rarely decompose the decision process into verifiable steps, making it hard to diagnose whether a failure is perceptual or reasoning-based. Tool-augmented reasoning has been explored for capability tasks but not systematically applied to safety decision-making in VLMs.
解决方案 / Solution
系统由规划器和响应器构成：规划器通过约束状态图定义感知→推理→决策三类虚拟工具，响应器在给出最终答案前输出可验证的键值工具链。训练采用SFT（6k）→DPO（18.6k）→GRPO（6k）三阶段课程，GRPO直接对工具使用正确性奖励，超越了答案级监督。
SaFeR-ToolKit defines a planner-responder architecture: the planner specifies a persona and a constrained transition graph over three virtual tools—Perception, Reasoning, and Decision—while the responder emits a typed key-value tool trace before producing the final answer. This trace is checkable, enabling supervision at intermediate steps rather than only at the output. Training follows a three-stage curriculum: SFT on 6k examples for trace format acquisition, DPO on 18.6k preference pairs for response quality, and GRPO on 6k examples to directly reward correct tool usage beyond answer-level signals. The full dataset (31,654 training + 1k evaluation examples) is the first tool-based safety reasoning dataset for multimodal models.
主要结论 / Key Results
在Qwen2.5-VL上，SaFeR-ToolKit的安全分和有用分均显著优于基线；三阶段课程优于仅SFT或SFT+DPO的消融实验，验证了GRPO对工具使用合规性的关键作用。
On Qwen2.5-VL, SaFeR-ToolKit significantly improves both the Safety Score and the Helpful Score compared to baseline alignment methods, demonstrating that structured tool traces reduce jailbreak success rates while limiting over-refusal. The three-stage curriculum outperforms ablations using only SFT or SFT+DPO, confirming GRPO's role in enforcing tool-usage compliance. The 1k held-out evaluation set provides a standardized benchmark for future multimodal safety work.
值得关注的讨论点 / Discussion Points
  • 泛化性：协议和数据集围绕Qwen2.5-VL构建，约束状态图与工具链格式能否迁移至其他VLM架构（如LLaVA、InternVL）尚未验证。
  • 工具链忠实性：键值工具链由模型自身生成，无法保证其反映真实内部计算，对抗输入仍可能在输出合规外观的同时操纵推理过程。
  • 数据集覆盖度：31,654条样本规模有限，有害类别、图像类型及语言分布未详细说明，长尾或特定文化安全风险的覆盖情况存疑。
  • Generalizability: The protocol and dataset are built around Qwen2.5-VL; whether the constrained transition graph and tool-trace format transfer effectively to other VLM architectures (e.g., LLaVA, InternVL) remains untested.
  • Trace faithfulness: Key-value tool traces are generated by the model itself, so there is no guarantee the trace reflects actual internal computation—adversarial inputs could still manipulate reasoning while producing a compliant-looking trace.
  • Dataset scale vs. diversity: With 31,654 examples, the dataset is a meaningful start, but the distribution of harmful categories, image types, and languages is not detailed in the abstract, raising questions about coverage of long-tail or culturally specific safety risks.

$\nabla$-Reasoner: LLM Reasoning via Test-Time Gradient Descent in Latent Space
Peihao Wang, Ruisi Cai, Zhen Wang et al. 2026-03-05 ICLR
核心贡献
∇-Reasoner通过在隐空间对token logits执行梯度下降，绕过离散搜索，实现推理时的可微优化以提升LLM推理能力。
∇-Reasoner improves LLM reasoning at inference time by applying gradient descent directly over token logits in latent space, bypassing discrete search with differentiable optimization guided by a reward model.
重要性与意义 / Significance
该论文在理论上证明推理时梯度下降与KL正则强化学习对偶，为无需重训练即可提升推理能力提供了新范式，对部署阶段的计算效率具有重要价值。
This paper bridges inference-time scaling and reinforcement learning theory, showing gradient-based decoding is dual to KL-regularized RL alignment — a conceptually elegant and practically impactful result. It opens a new paradigm for improving LLM reasoning without retraining, which is critical for deployment-time compute efficiency. Practitioners can potentially plug ∇-Reasoner into existing pipelines to boost performance on complex reasoning tasks.
科研问题 / Research Question
如何在推理时无需依赖低效离散搜索或试错提示，就能系统性地提升LLM推理质量？现有推理扩展方法缺乏原则性优化信号，计算代价高昂。
How can LLMs improve their reasoning quality at inference time without relying on slow, suboptimal discrete search or trial-and-error prompting? This matters because current inference-time scaling methods (e.g., beam search, Monte Carlo Tree Search, self-refinement) are computationally expensive and lack principled optimization signals.
先前工作为何不够 / Why Prior Work Falls Short
Best-of-N采样、MCTS等方法依赖离散组合搜索，无法利用梯度信号；自我纠错类方法受限于模型自身盲点，缺乏外部反馈，均未在解码过程中直接优化可微目标。
Existing inference-time scaling methods such as best-of-N sampling, MCTS, and self-refinement prompting rely on discrete, combinatorial search over token sequences, which scales poorly and offers no gradient signal. Prompt-based self-correction methods are further limited by the model's own blind spots and lack external feedback grounding. None of these approaches directly optimize a differentiable objective during decoding.
解决方案 / Solution
DTO在解码时对token logits执行梯度下降，结合LLM自身似然与外部奖励模型的梯度信号，优化后投影回离散token继续生成。同时引入拒绝采样和加速设计提升鲁棒性与效率，并从理论上证明该过程与KL正则RL对偶。
∇-Reasoner introduces Differentiable Textual Optimization (DTO), which performs gradient descent over token logits (in continuous latent space) during decoding, using gradients from both the LLM's own likelihood and an external reward model. The optimized logits are projected back to discrete tokens to continue generation, making the process compatible with standard autoregressive decoding. Rejection sampling is incorporated to filter low-quality candidates, and acceleration techniques reduce the overhead of repeated gradient steps. The authors also prove a theoretical duality: inference-time gradient ascent on reward in sample space is equivalent to KL-regularized RL policy optimization.
主要结论 / Key Results
∇-Reasoner在推理基准测试上优于标准解码和现有推理扩展基线，DTO优化结果与RL训练策略输出高度一致，验证了理论对偶性；具体数值在摘要中未完全披露。
∇-Reasoner achieves empirical gains over standard decoding and existing inference-time scaling baselines on reasoning benchmarks, demonstrating that differentiable optimization at test time yields measurable quality improvements. The theoretical duality result is validated empirically, showing that DTO-refined outputs align with what RL-trained policies would produce. Specific benchmark scores and percentage improvements are not fully disclosed in the abstract, but the paper reports consistent gains across evaluated tasks.
值得关注的讨论点 / Discussion Points

解决方案 / Solution
以人手抓取主成分（本征抓取）构建低维通用动作空间，通过预计算映射将其转化为各机械手关节动作；观测空间仅使用指尖与手掌位置，屏蔽手型差异。单一RL策略在仿真中训练后，可零样本迁移至四款不同灵巧手。
The authors introduce a universal action space defined by human hand eigengrasps — low-dimensional principal components of human grasp motions — which are retargeted to each specific robot hand's joint space via a precomputed mapping. Observations are unified by using only fingertip and palm positions, abstracting away hand-specific joint configurations. A single RL policy is trained in simulation to output eigengrasp actions, enabling zero-shot transfer to four distinct dexterous hands without retraining.
主要结论 / Key Results
方法在YCB数据集物体上对四款机械手实现了80%的抓取成功率，在仿真环境中验证了单一通用策略跨形态泛化的有效性，无需针对每款手重新训练。
The proposed approach achieves an 80% success rate in grasping objects from the YCB dataset across four distinct robot hand embodiments. This result is obtained in simulation and demonstrates that a single universal policy can generalize across diverse hand morphologies without per-hand retraining.
