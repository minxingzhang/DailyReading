Benign Samples Matter! Fine-tuning On Outlier Benign Samples Severely Breaks Safety
Zihan Guan, Mengxuan Hu, Ronghang Zhu et al. 2025-05-11 ICML
核心贡献
仅用100个精心筛选的"离群"良性样本微调大语言模型，即可严重破坏其安全对齐，揭示了微调流程中一个被严重低估的安全漏洞。
Fine-tuning LLMs on just 100 carefully selected "outlier" benign samples can severely break safety alignment, exposing a critical and underappreciated vulnerability in the fine-tuning pipeline.
重要性与意义 / Significance
本文表明，微调阶段的安全威胁比已知的更为精准高效——攻击者无需恶意数据，仅凭策略性筛选的良性数据即可奏效。这一发现在七个主流大模型上均可迁移，迫使研究界重新审视良性微调数据的安全假设。
This paper demonstrates that safety threats during fine-tuning are far more targeted and efficient than previously recognized — an attacker needs no malicious data, only strategically selected benign data. With high transferability across seven mainstream LLMs, the findings challenge the assumption that benign fine-tuning datasets are inherently safe, urging the community to rethink fine-tuning safety protocols.
科研问题 / Research Question
攻击者能否仅使用无害的良性数据在微调阶段破坏大语言模型的安全对齐？这一问题至关重要，因为许多部署流程默认良性数据集是安全的，缺乏针对性防护。
Can an attacker degrade LLM safety alignment using only benign, innocuous data during fine-tuning, without injecting any explicitly harmful content? This matters because many LLM deployment pipelines allow user-provided fine-tuning datasets that are assumed safe as long as they contain no overtly malicious samples.
先前工作为何不够 / Why Prior Work Falls Short
已有研究表明，包含少量恶意样本的混合数据集会破坏安全对齐；近期研究也发现纯良性数据集同样存在风险，但均将其视为被动现象，未将其作为可利用的攻击面加以研究。
Prior work has shown that fine-tuning on mixed datasets containing even a few malicious samples can compromise LLM safety. More recent studies found that purely benign datasets can also degrade safety, but treated this as a passive, uncontrolled phenomenon rather than an exploitable attack surface. No prior work systematically identified which benign samples are most harmful or proposed a method to extract them for targeted attack.
解决方案 / Solution
作者将问题建模为离群点检测：良性数据集中某些统计异常样本对安全降级贡献最大。提出Self-Inf-N方法，结合自影响分数与嵌入空间最近邻距离对样本打分排序，并仅用排名靠前的离群样本进行微调，构成高效红队攻击，无需任何恶意数据。
The authors frame the problem as outlier detection: within a benign dataset, certain samples are statistically anomalous and disproportionately responsible for safety degradation during fine-tuning. They propose Self-Inf-N, a method combining self-influence scores (measuring how much a sample affects the model's own loss) with nearest-neighbor distance in embedding space to score and rank samples by their safety-degrading potential. The top-ranked outlier samples are then used exclusively for fine-tuning, forming a targeted red-teaming attack. This approach requires no access to harmful data and is computationally lightweight compared to full dataset fine-tuning.
主要结论 / Key Results
仅使用Self-Inf-N筛选的100个离群样本进行微调，即可在七个主流大模型上造成严重的安全对齐降级，显著优于随机良性样本基线，且在不同架构间具有高度可迁移性。
Fine-tuning on only 100 outlier samples selected by Self-Inf-N leads to severe safety alignment degradation across seven mainstream LLMs, significantly outperforming random benign sample selection baselines. The attack demonstrates high transferability across different model architectures, meaning outliers identified on one model can compromise others. The method also remains effective in practical deployment scenarios where full model access may be limited.
值得关注的讨论点 / Discussion Points
  • 仅需100个样本即可实现强力攻击，说明当前大模型的安全对齐极为脆弱，易受训练数据分布边缘区域的影响。
  • 跨架构的可迁移性意味着针对单一模型的防护不足，平台级的数据集筛查机制迫在眉睫。
  • 离群样本在内容上并无明显恶意，极难被现有内容审核工具识别，这对提供微调服务的API平台（如OpenAI）构成严峻挑战。
  • Outlier-based targeting is highly efficient: achieving strong safety degradation with only 100 samples suggests that the safety alignment of current LLMs is brittle and concentrated in a small, identifiable region of the training data distribution.
  • The transferability finding is particularly alarming — if outlier samples identified on one LLM architecture can break safety in another, defenders cannot rely on model-specific hardening alone, and platform-level dataset screening becomes essential.
  • The work raises an unresolved tension: outlier samples are, by definition, unusual but not overtly harmful, making them extremely difficult to filter using content-based moderation tools, posing a serious challenge for API-based fine-tuning service providers like OpenAI and Hugging Face.

Improved Techniques for Optimization-Based Jailbreaking on Large Language Models
Xiaojun Jia, Tianyu Pang, Chao Du et al. 2024-05-31 ICLR
核心贡献
本文针对GCG优化式越狱攻击提出三项改进：多样化目标模板、自适应多坐标更新策略及重启机制，显著提升攻击收敛速度与成功率。
This paper proposes three concrete improvements to GCG-style optimization-based jailbreaking attacks on LLMs: diverse target templates, adaptive multi-coordinate token replacement, and a restarting strategy, collectively achieving faster convergence and higher attack success rates.
重要性与意义 / Significance
本文揭示了主流对齐机制的脆弱性，为红队测试提供更高效工具，推动社区开发更鲁棒的安全防御。所提技术具有普适性，可扩展至其他优化式攻击方法。
As LLMs are increasingly deployed in sensitive contexts, understanding and exposing weaknesses in alignment mechanisms is crucial for building more robust safety systems. This work advances the red-teaming toolkit by making optimization-based jailbreaks substantially more efficient, pressuring the community to develop stronger defenses. The techniques introduced are broadly applicable beyond GCG, offering a systematic blueprint for future adversarial attack research on aligned LLMs.
科研问题 / Research Question
如何提升优化式越狱攻击的效率与成功率？现有GCG方法收敛慢、成功率有限，难以充分暴露对齐LLM的真实脆弱性，亟需更强的红队测试工具。
The paper asks: how can optimization-based jailbreaking attacks on aligned LLMs be made more efficient and effective? This matters because existing methods like GCG are slow to converge and have limited success rates, leaving the true vulnerability of aligned LLMs underexplored. Faster, stronger attacks are necessary red-teaming tools for probing and ultimately improving LLM safety.
先前工作为何不够 / Why Prior Work Falls Short
GCG依赖单一目标模板"Sure"，极大限制了攻击多样性；每步固定替换单个token导致收敛缓慢且易陷入局部最优。其他越狱方法（如模板注入）缺乏梯度优化方法的泛化性与迁移性。
GCG (Greedy Coordinate Gradient) is the dominant optimization-based jailbreak, but it relies on a single, rigid target template ("Sure"), which severely limits its ability to steer model outputs. Its fixed single-token replacement strategy per step also leads to slow convergence and frequent stagnation. Other jailbreaks (e.g., prompt injection, template-based) lack the generalizability and transferability of gradient-based approaches.
解决方案 / Solution
提出三项改进：①用包含有害自我暗示和引导语的多样化模板替代单一"Sure"目标；②自适应多坐标更新策略，每步动态决定替换token数量以加速收敛；③引入重启机制，在攻击停滞时跳出局部最优，提升搜索鲁棒性。
The authors introduce three targeted improvements to GCG. First, they replace the single "Sure" target template with diverse templates incorporating harmful self-suggestion and guidance phrases, broadening the optimization landscape. Second, they propose an adaptive multi-coordinate update strategy that automatically decides how many tokens to replace at each step, accelerating convergence by escaping flat loss regions. Third, they add a restarting mechanism to escape local optima when the attack stagnates, further improving robustness of the search process.
主要结论 / Key Results
改进后的攻击在LLaMA-2、Vicuna等多个模型上相比原始GCG显著提升攻击成功率（ASR）并加快收敛；多样化模板单独使用即带来可观ASR提升，自适应更新策略大幅减少所需优化步数，三项技术组合达到优化式越狱的新最优性能。
The improved attack (referred to as I-GCG or similar) achieves notably higher attack success rates (ASR) and faster convergence compared to vanilla GCG across multiple aligned LLMs including LLaMA-2 and Vicuna. The diverse target templates alone provide significant ASR gains, while the adaptive update strategy reduces the number of steps required to find successful adversarial suffixes. Combined, the three techniques set a new state-of-the-art for optimization-based jailbreaking efficiency.
值得关注的讨论点 / Discussion Points
  • 目标模板多样化是高杠杆干预：仅替换模板即带来大幅ASR提升，说明此前GCG评估可能严重低估了对齐LLM的真实攻击面。
  • 自适应token替换策略揭示了对抗后缀搜索的优化地形问题，未来可探索二阶或动量方法进一步超越贪心坐标下降。
  • 更高效的越狱工具具有双重用途风险，降低了滥用门槛，迫切需要针对优化式后缀攻击的鲁棒防御（如对抗训练、输入认证）。
  • Target template diversity is a surprisingly high-leverage intervention: the finding that simply replacing 'Sure' with varied harmful-framing templates yields large ASR gains suggests that prior GCG evaluations may have significantly underestimated the true attack surface of aligned LLMs.
  • The adaptive token-replacement strategy raises a broader question about the optimization geometry of adversarial suffix search — future work could explore second-order or momentum-based methods to further accelerate convergence beyond greedy coordinate descent.
  • While the techniques improve attack efficiency, they also raise dual-use concerns: making jailbreaks faster and more accessible lowers the barrier for misuse, underscoring the urgency of developing defenses (e.g., adversarial training, input certification) that are robust to optimized suffix attacks.

Audio Is the Achilles' Heel: Red Teaming Audio Large Multimodal Models
Hao Yang, Lizhen Qu, Ehsan Shareghi et al. 2024-10-31 NAACL
核心贡献
本文对五个音频大型多模态模型进行首次系统性红队测试，揭示其平均69.14%的攻击成功率等严重安全漏洞。
This paper conducts the first comprehensive red-teaming study of five audio Large Multimodal Models (LMMs), revealing critical safety vulnerabilities with an average 69.14% attack success rate against harmful audio inputs.
重要性与意义 / Significance
本文首次对音频LMM进行系统性安全审计，填补了视觉模型研究忽视音频模态的空白。研究结果对现实部署中的音频AI助手安全防护具有直接指导意义，所提出的攻击分类与基准可作为未来研究的标准评估框架。
This is the first systematic safety audit of audio LMMs, filling a critical gap left by prior work that focused almost exclusively on vision-language models. The findings directly inform practitioners deploying audio-capable AI assistants in real-world settings where adversarial audio inputs are a genuine threat. The benchmark and attack taxonomy introduced can serve as a standard evaluation framework for future audio LMM safety research.
科研问题 / Research Question
本文研究文本模态的安全对齐是否能迁移至音频输入，包括语音形式的有害问题和语音特有的越狱攻击。随着音频LMM在语音交互场景中的广泛应用，这一问题具有重要的现实意义。
The paper investigates whether safety alignment applied to the text modality of LMMs generalizes to audio inputs, including spoken harmful questions and speech-specific jailbreaks. This matters because audio LMMs are increasingly deployed in real-world voice interfaces, yet their safety properties are almost entirely unstudied compared to their text and vision counterparts.
先前工作为何不够 / Why Prior Work Falls Short
现有多模态安全研究主要聚焦于视觉LMM，音频模态几乎未被探索。文本红队方法因忽视语音特有的韵律、口音和非语音声音等特性，难以直接迁移至音频场景。此前也无研究针对多种攻击向量对音频LMM进行系统性基准测试。
Existing safety-alignment research for multimodal models has concentrated on vision LMMs, leaving audio modalities largely unexamined. Text-based red-teaming methods do not transfer directly to audio inputs due to speech-specific phenomena such as prosody, accent, and non-speech sounds. No prior work had benchmarked audio LMMs across diverse attack vectors spanning both harmful speech content and distracting audio contexts.
解决方案 / Solution
作者在三类攻击设置下对五个音频LMM进行红队测试：（i）音频与文本形式的有害问题，（ii）有害文本问题配合干扰性非语音音频，（iii）利用语音特性（如韵律或口音）的越狱攻击。研究系统评估各设置下的攻击成功率，并以GPT-4o作为商业模型基线，全面覆盖当前主流音频LMM。
The authors red-team five advanced audio LMMs (including both open-source and proprietary models) under three distinct attack settings: (i) harmful questions delivered in both audio and text formats, (ii) harmful text questions paired with distracting non-speech audio (e.g., background noise or music), and (iii) speech-specific jailbreaks that exploit audio-unique properties such as altered prosody or accent manipulation. They evaluate attack success rate (ASR) systematically across these settings and models. The study also includes GPT-4o as a proprietary baseline for comparison, providing a broad coverage of the current audio LMM landscape.
主要结论 / Key Results
面对音频形式有害问题时，开源音频LMM的平均攻击成功率高达69.14%，显著高于纯文本安全基线。非语音干扰音频亦导致模型安全性明显下降。语音特有越狱攻击揭示了文本安全评估无法捕捉的独特漏洞，GPT-4o等商业模型防御相对较强但仍不完善。
Open-source audio LMMs suffer an average attack success rate of 69.14% when faced with harmful questions in audio format, substantially higher than their text-only safety baselines. Models also show notable safety degradation when distracted by irrelevant non-speech audio accompanying harmful text queries. Speech-specific jailbreaks further expose unique vulnerabilities not captured by standard text-based safety evaluations, with proprietary models like GPT-4o showing comparatively stronger but still imperfect defenses.
值得关注的讨论点 / Discussion Points
  • 模态对齐缺口：文本安全微调无法有效迁移至音频输入，表明当前对齐流程未能实现跨模态一致的安全保障，这是一个根本性的架构问题。
  • 非语音干扰作为攻击向量：良性背景音频可削弱安全防护，说明模型将声学上下文与语义意图混淆，反映音频编码器对语言模型输出的条件化存在缺陷。
  • 基准泛化性：研究仅涵盖五个模型和特定越狱类别，随着音频LMM快速迭代（如实时语音API），攻击面将持续演变，需要动态更新的红队评估方法。
  • Modality alignment gap: Safety fine-tuning on text does not robustly transfer to audio inputs, suggesting that current alignment pipelines treat modalities independently rather than enforcing cross-modal safety consistency — a fundamental architectural concern.
  • Non-speech distraction as an attack vector: The finding that benign background audio can degrade safety guardrails implies models conflate acoustic context with semantic intent, pointing to a weakness in how audio encoders condition language model outputs.
  • Benchmark generalizability: The study covers only five models and a specific set of jailbreak categories; as audio LMMs rapidly evolve (e.g., with real-time voice APIs), the attack surface may shift, requiring continuous and adaptive red-teaming methodologies.

Jailbreaking Embodied LLMs via Action-level Manipulation
Xinyu Huang, Qiang Yang, Leming Shen et al. 2026-03-02
核心贡献
Blindfold通过使有害动作序列在语义上看似安全，在动作层面破解具身LLM，绕过语言级别的安全防护。
Blindfold is an automated attack framework that jailbreaks embodied LLMs at the action level by making physically harmful instruction sequences appear semantically safe, bypassing language-level defenses.
重要性与意义 / Significance
本文揭示了当前LLM安全研究的重要盲区：语言层面的对齐无法保证具身AI的物理安全。该工作为具身AI的动作级安全评估提出了迫切需求，对部署LLM驱动机器人的从业者具有重要警示意义。
This paper exposes a critical blind spot in current LLM safety research: language-level alignment does not guarantee physical safety when LLMs control real-world actuators. By demonstrating that semantically benign instructions can produce dangerous physical outcomes, the work highlights an urgent need for action-level safety evaluation frameworks in embodied AI. Practitioners deploying LLM-driven robots or autonomous agents should treat this as a foundational security concern.
科研问题 / Research Question
攻击者如何使具身LLM在执行语义看似安全的指令时产生危险的物理后果？现有安全研究几乎完全聚焦于文本输出，而具身AI的动作层面安全漏洞尚未得到充分研究。
How can an attacker cause embodied LLMs to execute physically harmful action sequences while keeping individual instructions semantically safe and thus invisible to language-level safety filters? This matters because embodied LLMs are increasingly deployed in robotics and autonomous systems where unsafe actions can cause real-world physical harm, yet existing safety research focuses almost exclusively on textual outputs.
先前工作为何不够 / Why Prior Work Falls Short
现有破解方法针对语言层输出，依赖低效的黑盒迭代试错，未能考虑物理动作的序列性与因果性。先前研究未区分语言无害性与物理安全性，在具身系统的决策与执行层面存在根本性漏洞。
Existing jailbreak methods target language-level outputs and rely on iterative black-box trial-and-error prompt manipulation, which is inefficient and fails to account for the sequential, causal nature of physical actions. Prior work does not distinguish between linguistic harmlessness and physical safety, leaving a fundamental gap at the decision and actuation layer of embodied systems.
解决方案 / Solution
Blindfold采用对抗性代理规划策略：攻破本地代理LLM以生成逐步语义安全但整体有害的动作计划，利用具身LLM因果推理能力有限的弱点。关键恶意动作被混入正常动作序列中加以隐藏，大幅提升了逃避安全检测的能力。白盒代理方式避免了对目标系统的高成本迭代查询。
Blindfold introduces an Adversarial Proxy Planning strategy: rather than directly attacking a black-box embodied LLM, it compromises a local surrogate LLM to craft action-level plans that appear semantically benign at each step but collectively lead to harmful physical outcomes. The framework exploits the limited causal reasoning capabilities of embodied LLMs, which struggle to foresee the downstream physical consequences of individually innocuous actions. Malicious key actions are further concealed by injecting them among normal actions, making detection by safety monitors significantly harder. This white-box surrogate approach avoids expensive iterative querying of the target system.
主要结论 / Key Results
Blindfold在多种评估场景中对具身LLM智能体实现了高攻击成功率，成功绕过语言级安全过滤器。对抗代理规划方法比迭代黑盒基线方法效率显著更高，且可跨不同具身LLM目标泛化。
Blindfold demonstrates high attack success rates against embodied LLM agents across evaluated scenarios, successfully bypassing language-level safety filters by keeping individual actions semantically safe while producing harmful physical consequences. The adversarial proxy planning approach proves substantially more efficient than iterative black-box jailbreak baselines. Specific numerical results (e.g., attack success rate percentages) are not fully disclosed in the provided abstract but the framework is shown to generalize across different embodied LLM targets.
值得关注的讨论点 / Discussion Points
  • 动作级与语言级安全：通过语言安全检查不足以保证物理安全，具身AI需要专门的因果感知动作序列验证器。
  • 代理攻击的迁移性：代理LLM生成的动作计划能否有效迁移至黑盒目标，在异构动作空间中仍需深入研究。
  • 防御空白：论文识别了漏洞但未详述对策，开发能检测语义分散有害计划的动作级安全监测机制仍是紧迫的开放挑战。
  • Action-level vs. language-level safety: This work confirms that passing language safety checks is insufficient for physical security—future embodied AI systems need dedicated causal and consequence-aware action-sequence validators, not just text filters.
  • Transferability of surrogate attacks: The proxy planning strategy assumes the surrogate LLM's action plans transfer effectively to the black-box target; real-world embodied systems may have heterogeneous action spaces or grounding mechanisms that could limit transferability and deserve further investigation.
  • Defense gap: The paper identifies the vulnerability but the abstract does not detail robust countermeasures; developing action-level safety monitors that can detect semantically distributed harmful plans remains an open and urgent research challenge.

SimPO: Simple Preference Optimization with a Reference-Free Reward
Yu Meng, Mengzhou Xia, Danqi Chen 2024-05-23 NeurIPS
核心贡献
SimPO用序列平均对数概率替代DPO的参考模型奖励，并引入目标奖励边际，在减少计算开销的同时提升对齐性能。
SimPO replaces DPO's reference-model-based reward with average log probability and adds a target reward margin, achieving better alignment performance with less compute.
重要性与意义 / Significance
SimPO完全消除参考模型，显著降低内存与计算成本，同时在多个基准上超越DPO及其最新变体。该方法在Mistral、Llama 3、Gemma 2等主流模型上验证，具有广泛实用价值。
SimPO removes the reference model entirely, cutting memory and compute costs significantly while outperforming DPO and its latest variants across multiple competitive benchmarks. Its simplicity makes it immediately practical for researchers and practitioners working on LLM alignment at scale. The method is validated on widely-used models (Mistral, Llama 3, Gemma 2), ensuring broad applicability across the current LLM ecosystem.
科研问题 / Research Question
如何设计一种更简单、更高效的偏好优化算法，使其更符合语言模型的实际生成机制？DPO依赖参考模型，增加了内存开销，且奖励公式与生成行为存在偏差。
How can we design a simpler, more compute-efficient preference optimization algorithm that better aligns with how language models actually generate text? DPO and its variants rely on a reference model during training, which increases memory overhead and introduces a reward formulation misaligned with generation-time behavior.
先前工作为何不够 / Why Prior Work Falls Short
DPO及其变体虽简化了RLHF，但仍需参考模型，内存需求翻倍。其原始对数概率奖励存在长度偏差，与自回归生成机制不一致，倾向于奖励较短响应。
DPO and its variants (e.g., IPO, SLiC) reparameterize RLHF rewards but still require a reference model during training, doubling GPU memory requirements. 值得关注的讨论点 / Discussion Points
  • 长度偏差风险：平均对数概率虽缓解了长度偏差，但固定的目标边际γ仍需针对不同数据集和模型规模仔细调参，可能误惩高质量的较长响应。
  • 离线方法局限：SimPO仍是基于静态偏好数据集的离线方法，无法在训练中自适应利用模型生成样本，与在线RLHF相比存在差距。

  • Offline limitation: SimPO remains an offline method trained on static preference datasets, so it cannot adaptively improve from model-generated outputs during training — iterative or online variants would be needed to close the gap with online RLHF methods.
  • Reward signal interpretability: Removing the reference model simplifies training but also removes an explicit anchor for reward calibration; it remains unclear how well SimPO's implicit reward generalizes to out-of-distribution prompts or adversarial inputs.
