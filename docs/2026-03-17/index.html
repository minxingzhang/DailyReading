AGD: Adversarial Game Defense Against Jailbreak Attacks in Large Language Models
Shilong Pan, Zhiliang Tian, Zhen Huang et al. 2025-01-01 ACL
核心贡献
AGD提出基于对抗博弈的防御方法，通过动态调整LLM内部表示，在有用性与无害性之间实现平衡，以抵御越狱攻击。
AGD proposes an adversarial game-based defense that dynamically adjusts LLMs' internal representations to balance helpfulness and harmlessness against jailbreak attacks.
重要性与意义 / Significance
越狱攻击对已部署的LLM构成重大安全威胁，现有防御方法难以泛化至未知攻击。AGD的博弈论框架为在不重新训练的情况下处理有用性与安全性之间的内在矛盾提供了原则性思路，对未来实时部署阶段的安全机制设计具有重要参考价值。
Jailbreak attacks pose a critical safety risk for deployed LLMs, and existing defenses struggle to generalize to unseen attack patterns. AGD's game-theoretic framing offers a principled way to handle the inherent tension between utility and safety without retraining. This could influence how future LLM safety mechanisms are designed, particularly in real-time, deployment-stage settings.
科研问题 / Research Question
如何在不牺牲有用性的前提下，使LLM实时防御分布外越狱攻击？现有对齐与提示工程方法对新型攻击脆弱，而表示引导方法会破坏推理过程并降低模型效用。核心挑战在于在单一防御框架内调和有用性与无害性这两个相互竞争的目标。
How can LLMs defend against out-of-distribution jailbreak attacks in real time without sacrificing helpfulness? Existing alignment and prompt-engineering defenses are brittle to novel attacks, while representation-steering methods degrade model utility by disrupting inference. The core challenge is reconciling the competing objectives of helpfulness and harmlessness within a single defense framework.
先前工作为何不够 / Why Prior Work Falls Short
后训练对齐方法（如RLHF、安全微调）依赖安全标注数据集，对分布外越狱提示泛化能力差。提示工程防御依赖固定模板，攻击者可通过创意输入绕过。表示引导方法虽具实时适应性，但会干扰推理前向传播，损害模型整体效用。
Post-training alignment methods (e.g., RLHF, safety fine-tuning) depend on safety-annotated datasets and generalize poorly to OOD jailbreak prompts. Prompt engineering defenses rely on fixed safe templates that attackers can circumvent with sufficiently creative inputs. Representation-steering approaches offer real-time adaptability but disrupt the forward pass of inference, degrading overall model utility.
解决方案 / Solution
AGD将有用性与无害性的冲突建模为两个竞争目标之间的对抗博弈，在推理时动态调整LLM内部表示以寻找均衡点。该方法实时引导表示，使模型在抵御有害越狱输入的同时保留对良性查询的有用响应。通过博弈建模而非静态优化，AGD无需在新安全数据上重新训练即可适应分布外攻击。核心贡献是基于内部表示的对抗博弈机制，同时实现自适应性与效用保留。
AGD frames the helpfulness-harmlessness conflict as an adversarial game between two competing objectives and dynamically adjusts LLMs' internal representations at inference time to find an equilibrium. The method steers representations in real time, allowing the model to resist harmful jailbreak inputs while preserving useful responses to benign queries. By modeling the problem as a game rather than a static optimization, AGD adapts to OOD attacks without requiring retraining on new safety data. The core contribution is a principled adversarial game mechanism that operates on internal representations, enabling both adaptability and utility preservation simultaneously.
主要结论 / Key Results
由于摘要不完整，具体量化结果暂不可知，但AGD据报道在越狱攻击场景下实现了有用性与无害性的平衡权衡。该方法在维持模型效用的同时降低有害响应率，预期优于先前的表示引导基线。完整实验细节与基准对比（如AdvBench或JailbreakBench）有待完整论文披露。
Specific quantitative results are not available due to the incomplete abstract, but AGD is reported to achieve a balanced trade-off between helpfulness and harmlessness under jailbreak attack scenarios. The method is designed to outperform prior representation-steering baselines by better maintaining model utility while reducing harmful response rates. Full experimental details and benchmark comparisons (e.g., on datasets like AdvBench or JailbreakBench) are expected in the complete paper.

  • 论文框架假设有用性与无害性是主要竞争目标，但实际部署还涉及事实性、延迟等约束，双人博弈模型可能无法完全覆盖。

  • Representation steering at inference time is a promising direction, but the stability of the adversarial game equilibrium under diverse and adaptive attacks remains an open question — attackers could specifically target the game dynamics.
  • The paper's framing assumes helpfulness and harmlessness are the primary competing objectives, but real deployments involve additional constraints (e.g., factuality, latency), which the two-player game model may not fully capture.
  • Since the abstract is truncated, key details — such as how the game is solved (Nash equilibrium vs. minimax), the computational overhead at inference, and evaluation benchmarks — are unknown, making full assessment difficult.

Uncovering Security Threats and Architecting Defenses in Autonomous Agents: A Case Study of OpenClaw
Zonghao Ying, Xiao Yang, Siyang Wu et al. 2026-03-13
核心贡献
本文系统分析了OpenClaw自主LLM智能体框架的安全漏洞，提出三层风险分类法与架构级防御机制，以应对提示注入驱动的RCE及供应链污染等威胁。
This paper systematically analyzes security vulnerabilities in the OpenClaw autonomous LLM agent framework and proposes a tri-layered risk taxonomy plus architectural defense mechanisms to counter threats like prompt injection-driven RCE and supply chain contamination.
重要性与意义 / Significance
随着LLM智能体获得操作系统级权限与自主调用工具的能力，传统内容过滤防御已根本不足，本研究对部署智能体框架的从业者极具参考价值。所提三层分类法为研究界提供了系统性语言，用于分析跨认知、执行与信息系统维度的智能体专属威胁。
As LLM agents gain OS-level permissions and autonomous tool-calling capabilities, traditional content-filtering defenses are fundamentally insufficient, making this security analysis highly relevant for practitioners deploying agent frameworks. The proposed tri-layered taxonomy provides a structured vocabulary for the research community to reason about agent-specific threats across cognitive, execution, and information system dimensions. The OpenClaw case study offers a concrete, reproducible blueprint for auditing other autonomous agent frameworks.
科研问题 / Research Question
OpenClaw等框架赋予AI智能体操作系统级访问权限与多步骤工作流执行能力，催生了现有防御无法应对的新攻击面。核心问题是：自主工具调用智能体引入了哪些类别的漏洞，以及如何从架构层面进行系统性防御。
As LLM frameworks like OpenClaw grant AI agents operating-system-level access and the ability to execute complex multi-step workflows, novel attack surfaces emerge that existing defenses cannot address. The core problem is understanding what classes of vulnerabilities are introduced specifically by autonomous, tool-calling agents and how to architect systemic defenses against them.
先前工作为何不够 / Why Prior Work Falls Short
传统LLM安全研究聚焦于内容过滤与提示层防御，假设无状态单轮交互模型，无法应对持久化上下文与链式工具调用。此前的智能体安全研究缺乏同时覆盖认知操纵、软件执行漏洞与信息系统入侵的统一分类框架。
Traditional LLM security research focuses on content-filtering and prompt-level defenses, which assume a stateless, single-turn interaction model. These approaches fail to account for persistent context, chained tool invocations, and OS-level execution privileges inherent to autonomous agents. Prior agent security work lacks a unified taxonomy that spans cognitive manipulation, software execution exploits, and information system compromises simultaneously.
解决方案 / Solution
作者对OpenClaw生态进行全面安全审计，识别出四类关键漏洞：提示注入驱动的RCE、顺序工具攻击链、上下文失忆及供应链污染。提出三层风险分类法，覆盖AI认知层（目标劫持）、软件执行层（RCE、权限提升）与信息系统层（数据泄露、供应链攻击），并给出架构级防御建议。
The authors conduct a comprehensive security audit of the OpenClaw ecosystem, identifying four critical vulnerability classes: prompt injection-driven Remote Code Execution (RCE), sequential tool attack chains, context amnesia (loss of security state across conversation turns), and supply chain contamination via malicious tool/plugin integration. They propose a novel tri-layered risk taxonomy organizing threats across the AI Cognitive layer (e.g., goal hijacking), Software Execution layer (e.g., RCE, privilege escalation), and Information System layer (e.g., data exfiltration, supply chain attacks). Architectural defense mechanisms are proposed to address these systemic flaws, moving beyond reactive content filtering toward proactive structural hardening of agent pipelines.
主要结论 / Key Results
分析发现，由于工具调用管道沙箱隔离不足，提示注入可可靠触发OpenClaw中的RCE。顺序工具攻击链表明，多步工作流显著放大单一被污染输入的危害范围，实现跨工具边界的权限提升。上下文失忆被确认为结构性缺陷，早期建立的安全约束可在后续轮次中被遗忘或覆盖。
The analysis uncovers that prompt injection can reliably trigger RCE within OpenClaw due to insufficient sandboxing of tool-calling pipelines. Sequential tool attack chains demonstrate that multi-step agent workflows amplify the blast radius of a single compromised input, enabling privilege escalation across tool boundaries. Context amnesia is identified as a structural flaw where security constraints established early in a session can be forgotten or overridden in later turns, leaving agents persistently vulnerable.
值得关注的讨论点 / Discussion Points
  • 上下文失忆是传统软件安全中不存在的独特挑战：与无状态API不同，智能体记忆系统需要显式的会话级安全状态管理，而当前框架原生不支持此机制。
  • 供应链污染攻击向量表明，LLM智能体工具注册表亟需签名、来源验证与沙箱执行机制，类似npm或PyPI的包管理器安全实践。
  • 本文研究基于单一框架OpenClaw，三层分类法与防御机制能否推广至LangChain、AutoGPT或Semantic Kernel等主流框架，仍是开放性问题。
  • Context amnesia poses a unique challenge absent in traditional software security: unlike stateless APIs, agent memory systems require explicit session-scoped security state management, which current frameworks do not natively enforce.
  • The supply chain contamination threat vector — where malicious third-party tools or plugins silently alter agent behavior — suggests that tool registries for LLM agents urgently need signing, provenance verification, and sandboxed execution analogous to package managers like npm or PyPI.
  • The paper's findings are based on a single framework (OpenClaw), and it remains an open question how well the tri-layered taxonomy and proposed defenses generalize to other major agent frameworks such as LangChain, AutoGPT, or Microsoft's Semantic Kernel.

This paper provides rare practitioner-level insight into AI agent security from an organization operating agentic systems at scale (millions of users, thousands of enterprises). Its direct input to NIST/CAISI policy-making means its recommendations could shape industry standards and regulatory frameworks for AI agent deployment. Researchers and engineers building agentic pipelines will find the threat taxonomy and defense stack immediately actionable.
科研问题 / Research Question
AI智能体架构如何打破代码与数据分离、权限边界固定等传统安全假设，并由此引发机密性、完整性与可用性方面的新型威胁？
How do agentic AI architectures introduce fundamentally new security risks beyond those of traditional software or static LLM deployments? The paper argues that agents break classical assumptions of code-data separation, fixed authority boundaries, and predictable execution, creating novel failure modes across confidentiality, integrity, and availability.

Existing security frameworks (e.g., for static LLMs or conventional software) do not account for the dynamic, multi-step, tool-using nature of agents. Prior defenses focus on single-turn input filtering and model alignment, missing threats that emerge across multi-agent coordination, long-running workflows, and cross-boundary tool invocations. There is no established industry standard for agentic system security, leaving practitioners without authoritative guidance.
解决方案 / Solution
提出四大攻击面（工具、连接器、托管边界、多智能体协调）的结构化威胁模型，重点分析间接提示注入、混淆代理攻击和级联失败三类威胁，并构建输入过滤→模型级缓解→沙箱执行→确定性策略执行的分层防御栈。
The paper introduces a structured threat model mapping four principal attack surfaces: tools, connectors, hosting boundaries, and multi-agent coordination channels. It gives particular emphasis to three attack classes — indirect prompt injection (malicious instructions embedded in retrieved data), confused-deputy attacks (agents acting beyond their authorized scope by being manipulated into misusing delegated privileges), and cascading failures in long-horizon workflows. Defenses are organized as a layered stack: input/output filtering, model-level mitigations, sandboxed execution environments, and deterministic policy enforcement for high-stakes actions. The framework is grounded in operational lessons from Perplexity's production agentic systems.
主要结论 / Key Results
本文为立场文件，无定量实验结果。核心结论为：间接提示注入最难根除；混淆代理威胁被系统性低估；不可逆操作必须依赖确定性策略执行而非仅靠模型判断。
As a position/RFI response paper, quantitative experimental results are not provided. The key findings are qualitative: indirect prompt injection is identified as the most pervasive and difficult-to-eliminate threat; confused-deputy behavior is flagged as systematically underestimated; and no single defense layer is sufficient, making the layered stack approach essential. The authors conclude that deterministic policy enforcement (not model judgment alone) is necessary for actions with irreversible consequences.
值得关注的讨论点 / Discussion Points
  • 间接提示注入仍是开放难题：智能体正常运行中需消费非结构化外部数据，如何在不损失功能的前提下净化所有注入向量，本文指出问题但未给出完整方案。
  • 混淆代理威胁框架有价值但防御措施偏启发式：范围限制和确认提示等缓解手段对自适应对手的有效性尚未经实证验证。
  • 本文为RFI回应而非同行评审研究，威胁分类法和防御建议缺乏实验支撑，后续需构建红队基准测试以量化各防御层的实际效果。
  • Indirect prompt injection remains an open research problem: because agents consume unstructured external data (web pages, emails, documents) as part of normal operation, sanitizing all possible injection vectors without breaking functionality is fundamentally hard — the paper highlights this gap but does not offer a complete solution.
  • The confused-deputy framing is valuable but underspecified: the paper correctly identifies that agents can be manipulated into misusing delegated privileges (e.g., an email-reading agent tricked into exfiltrating data), but the proposed mitigations (scope restriction, confirmation prompts) are heuristic and their effectiveness against adaptive adversaries is unvalidated.
  • The policy/research gap is significant: this paper is an RFI response, not a peer-reviewed study, so its threat taxonomy and defense recommendations lack empirical grounding. Future work should include red-team benchmarks and ablation studies on individual defense layers to quantify their effectiveness.

ToolTree用蒙特卡洛树搜索替代贪心工具选择策略，通过双阶段LLM评估与双向剪枝实现更高效、准确的多步工具规划。
ToolTree: Efficient LLM Agent Tool Planning via Dual-Feedback Monte Carlo Tree Search and Bidirectional Pruning
Shuo Yang, Soyeon Caren Han, Yihao Ding et al. 2026-03-13 ICLR
核心贡献
ToolTree用蒙特卡洛树搜索替代贪心工具选择策略，通过双阶段LLM评估与双向剪枝实现更高效、准确的多步工具规划。
ToolTree replaces greedy tool selection in LLM agents with a Monte Carlo Tree Search framework that uses dual-stage LLM evaluation and bidirectional pruning to plan multi-step tool-use sequences more efficiently and accurately.
重要性与意义 / Significance
ToolTree解决了LLM智能体在复杂多步任务中工具规划失误导致级联失败的核心瓶颈，在4个基准上平均提升约10%性能，同时保持高效率，为工具增强型LLM提供了实用的规划升级方案。
ToolTree addresses a critical bottleneck in deploying LLM agents on real-world, multi-step tasks where poor tool planning cascades into task failure. By achieving ~10% average performance gain over state-of-the-art baselines across 4 benchmarks while maintaining efficiency, it offers a practical, drop-in planning upgrade for LLM agent systems. This work establishes MCTS-based planning as a viable and scalable alternative to greedy decoding in tool-augmented LLMs.
科研问题 / Research Question
如何让LLM智能体在复杂多步任务中更可靠地规划工具调用序列，而非依赖短视的贪心选择？现实中工具间依赖关系复杂，早期错误会在整个流程中累积放大。
How can LLM agents plan sequences of tool calls more reliably for complex, multi-step tasks without resorting to myopic, greedy selection? This matters because real-world agentic tasks—such as API chaining, code execution, and web interaction—require anticipating inter-tool dependencies, and failures in early steps compound throughout the pipeline.
先前工作为何不够 / Why Prior Work Falls Short
现有方法（如ReAct、Toolformer）采用逐步贪心选择，缺乏前瞻性且忽视工具间依赖。已有树搜索变体（如思维树）计算代价高，且未针对工具调用环境设计，无法有效剪除低质量工具轨迹。
Existing LLM agent tool planning methods (e.g., ReAct, Toolformer) use greedy, step-by-step selection that lacks lookahead and ignores dependencies between tools in a sequence. Prior tree-search adaptations for LLMs (e.g., Tree of Thoughts) are not designed for tool-use environments and are computationally expensive due to exhaustive expansion. These approaches fail to efficiently prune low-quality tool trajectories before or after execution, wasting inference budget.
解决方案 / Solution
ToolTree将工具规划建模为MCTS问题，每个节点代表一次工具调用决策。双阶段LLM评估分别在执行前（基于前瞻评分的预剪枝）和执行后（基于输出结果的后剪枝）筛选分支，双向剪枝大幅压缩搜索空间，同时适用于开放集与封闭集工具规划任务。
ToolTree frames tool planning as a Monte Carlo Tree Search problem, where each tree node represents a tool-call decision and branches represent alternative tool sequences. A dual-stage LLM evaluation mechanism scores candidate branches both before execution (pre-pruning via LLM-based foresight scoring) and after execution (post-pruning based on observed tool outputs), focusing compute on the most promising trajectories. Bidirectional pruning removes low-value branches at both stages, dramatically reducing the search space without sacrificing coverage. This design allows ToolTree to handle open-set (unseen tools) and closed-set (predefined tool libraries) planning tasks uniformly.
主要结论 / Key Results
ToolTree在4个基准测试中平均性能提升约10%，同时在所有对比方法中保持最高效率。在需要长链依赖工具调用的任务上，相较贪心方法的优势最为显著。
ToolTree achieves an average performance gain of ~10% over state-of-the-art baselines across 4 benchmarks covering both open-set and closed-set tool planning tasks. It simultaneously maintains the highest efficiency among compared methods, meaning the performance gains do not come at the cost of significantly increased inference calls or latency. Specific benchmark results validate improvements on tasks requiring long, dependent tool-call chains where greedy methods degrade most.
值得关注的讨论点 / Discussion Points
  • 双反馈设计是核心创新：执行前前瞻剪枝与执行后结果剪枝的分离，有效平衡了避免无效工具调用与利用真实反馈纠错的需求，是此前MCTS变体未能解决的关键问题。
  • 效率主张需审慎评估：MCTS本质上比贪心方法需要更多LLM调用，实际效率优势高度依赖双向剪枝的激进程度，各基准上的具体调用次数对比值得深入核查。
  • 开放集工具泛化是难点：处理未见工具要求LLM评估器对陌生API进行推理，开放集与封闭集基准上的性能差距可揭示双阶段评估器对工具分布的依赖程度。
  • Dual-feedback design is key: separating pre-execution (foresight) and post-execution (outcome) pruning is the core innovation—it avoids wasting tool API calls on low-quality branches while still correcting mistakes after real feedback, a balance prior MCTS adaptations missed.
  • Efficiency claim needs scrutiny: while the paper claims 'highest efficiency,' MCTS inherently involves more LLM calls than greedy methods; the efficiency advantage likely depends on how aggressively bidirectional pruning reduces tree width, and the exact call-count comparisons across benchmarks deserve close examination.
  • Generalization to open-set tools is non-trivial: handling unseen tools requires the LLM evaluator to reason about unfamiliar APIs, and ToolTree's performance on open-set benchmarks relative to closed-set ones would reveal how much the dual-stage evaluator depends on in-distribution tool knowledge.

随着LLM智能体在科学推理和决策支持等高风险领域的部署，语义不变性成为安全关键需求。本文首次提供针对语义不变性的系统性多模型基准，为从业者提供可复用的评估协议。

This paper provides the first systematic, multi-model benchmark specifically targeting semantic invariance, offering a reusable evaluation protocol for practitioners. 本文研究LLM推理智能体在语义等价但表述不同的输入下能否保持一致输出（即语义不变性）。在高风险应用中，推理不一致可能导致不可预测的决策后果。
The paper asks whether LLM reasoning agents produce consistent outputs when the same problem is expressed in semantically equivalent but syntactically different ways — a property called semantic invariance. This matters because inconsistent reasoning in agentic pipelines can lead to unpredictable, unreliable decisions in consequential real-world applications.
先前工作为何不够 / Why Prior Work Falls Short
现有基准仅在固定标准表述上评估准确率，无法反映输出稳定性。已有鲁棒性研究多关注对抗扰动，而非语义保留变换，也未在多架构模型间进行系统比较。

解决方案 / Solution

Results show that no model achieves full semantic invariance across all eight transformations, with consistency scores varying substantially both across models and transformation types. Larger models (e.g., Hermes 405B, Qwen3 235B-A22B) tend to show higher invariance than smaller counterparts, but architectural family matters as much as scale. Contrastive formulation and context-shift transformations (academic vs. business) induce the largest consistency drops across nearly all tested models.
值得关注的讨论点 / Discussion Points
  • 语义不变性并非仅由模型规模决定——部分小型MoE模型（如Qwen3-30B-A3B）在特定变换上优于更大的密集模型，说明架构设计对输入改写的响应方式有独立影响。
  • 8种变换的破坏性差异显著：语境切换（学术/商业框架）和对比表述对一致性的破坏最大，表明LLM推理路径对语用表面线索高度敏感，即便命题内容不变。
  • 本研究在单模型隔离环境下评估，而真实智能体系统涉及多智能体流水线，不变性失效可能级联放大，未来工作需将框架扩展至多轮、多智能体场景。
  • Semantic invariance is not guaranteed by scale alone — smaller MoE models like Qwen3-30B-A3B sometimes outperform larger dense models on specific transformations, suggesting architectural choices (e.g., mixture-of-experts routing) interact non-trivially with input rephrasing.
  • The eight transformations are not equally disruptive: context-shift transformations (academic vs. business framing) and contrastive formulations are disproportionately destabilizing, implying that surface-level pragmatic cues heavily influence LLM reasoning paths even when propositional content is unchanged.
  • The study evaluates models in isolation, but real agentic systems involve multi-agent pipelines where invariance failures can compound. Future work should extend this framework to multi-turn and multi-agent settings to assess cascading inconsistency effects.

科研问题 / Research Question
单一神经运动规划器如何在不重新训练的情况下泛化到不同机械臂本体？这一问题直接影响多机器人系统的可扩展性。
How can a single neural motion planner generalize across different robot manipulator embodiments without retraining? This matters because the combinatorial cost of training separate planners for each robot body severely limits scalability in real-world multi-robot deployments.

  • Embodiment distribution coverage: The zero-shot claim is bounded by the training distribution — it remains unclear how far outside that distribution (e.g., novel joint topologies, mobile manipulators) XMoP can generalize, which is critical for real-world applicability.
  • Implicit vs. explicit kinematics: Implicitly learning kinematic constraints from data may fail silently on edge-case configurations; a hybrid approach combining learned policies with analytical forward kinematics checks could improve reliability.
  • Scalability to real hardware: The work appears simulation-only; bridging the sim-to-real gap for cross-embodiment policies — where each robot has unique friction, backlash, and sensor noise profiles — is a significant open challenge that the paper does not yet address.
